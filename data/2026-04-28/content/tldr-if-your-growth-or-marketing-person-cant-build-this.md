description: 'If your growth or marketing person can''t build this in 2 days, I''d genuinely consider firing them from that role. This is table stakes in 2026. Every company produces signals. People visiting your website, engaging with your content, checking out your product. Most of these people never book a demo. But they said yes at least once. They visited. They signed up. They clicked something. Your job is to find the qualified ones and reach out before they forget you exist. Here''s how we built the system: 𝗦𝘁𝗲𝗽 𝟭: 𝗠𝗮𝗽 𝗲𝘃𝗲𝗿𝘆 𝘀𝗶𝗴𝗻𝗮𝗹 𝘆𝗼𝘂𝗿 𝗰𝗼𝗺𝗽𝗮𝗻𝘆 𝗽𝗿𝗼𝗱𝘂𝗰𝗲𝘀 List every source of interest that isn''t a demo booking: → Website visitors (we use RB2B) → Interactive demo viewers (we use Navattic - tells us who watched, how long they spent) → Partial form submissions (people who gave their email but didn''t book a call - we use Surface Labs) → ROI calculator users → Lead magnet downloads → Newsletter signups These are all people who said yes to something but haven''t said yes to a meeting. They''re
  primed. They just need one more push. 𝗦𝘁𝗲𝗽 𝟮: 𝗘𝗻𝗿𝗶𝗰𝗵 𝗲𝘃𝗲𝗿𝘆𝘁𝗵𝗶𝗻𝗴 Every signal source feeds into a dedicated Freckle board. Freckle.io enriches each lead with: → Company name + website → Full name + email → Job title → Company description → Employee count → Location → Estimated revenue All automatic. No manual research. Lead comes in, enrichment runs immediately. 𝗦𝘁𝗲𝗽 𝟯: 𝗤𝘂𝗮𝗹𝗶𝗳𝘆 𝘄𝗶𝘁𝗵 𝗔𝗜 Inside Freckle, we set up an automation using ChatGPT. It takes the enriched data - company description, job title, revenue, employee size - visits their website for more context, then scores every lead against our ICP Bible. The ICP Bible is a one-page doc that defines exactly who Valley''s ideal customer is. Industry, size, persona, use case. Clear enough that an AI can read it and make a qualified/not qualified decision. 100 people might engage with you. Maybe 30 are qualified. The AI finds those 30. 𝗦𝘁𝗲𝗽 𝟰: 𝗘𝗻𝗿𝗶𝗰𝗵 𝗽𝗵𝗼𝗻𝗲 𝗻𝘂𝗺𝗯𝗲𝗿𝘀 𝗼𝗻𝗹𝘆 𝗳𝗼𝗿 𝗾𝘂𝗮𝗹𝗶𝗳𝗶𝗲𝗱 𝗹𝗲𝗮𝗱𝘀 Phone enrichment burns credits fast. So we only enrich
  numbers for leads that passed ICP qualification. No wasted spend on students and interns. 𝗦𝘁𝗲𝗽 𝟱: 𝗣𝘂𝘀𝗵 𝘁𝗼 𝘀𝗮𝗹𝗲𝘀 Qualified leads with phone numbers get pushed to the sales team via Slack. The rep knows exactly who they are, what they engaged with, and why they''re qualified before picking up the phone.
