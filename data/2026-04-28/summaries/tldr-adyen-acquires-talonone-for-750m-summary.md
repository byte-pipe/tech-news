# Adyen acquires Talon.One for €750m

## Deal overview
- Adyen will acquire 100 % of Talon.One, a Berlin‑based loyalty and incentives platform.  
- Total consideration is €750 million, funded entirely from Adyen’s existing cash reserves.  
- Completion is expected in the second half of 2026, subject to regulatory approvals and standard closing conditions.  

## Financial and operational details
- Talon.One serves more than 300 merchants worldwide.  
- Projected annual recurring revenue for the end of the current year is about €60 million, with a recent growth rate of 30–40 % per year.  
- Co‑founders Christoph Gerber and Sebastian Haas will reinvest part of their proceeds into newly issued Adyen ordinary shares.  

## Strategic rationale
- The acquisition supports Adyen’s Unified Commerce strategy, aiming to merge online and in‑store customer interactions.  
- Integration will combine Adyen’s global payments infrastructure and transaction data with Talon.One’s real‑time decisioning engine.  
- Expected outcome: merchants can create a consistent customer identity across channels and apply promotions or dynamic pricing instantly at the point of sale.  

## Executive statements
- **Adyen co‑CEO Ingo Uytdehaage**: merchants need to connect online and in‑store data in real time; Talon.One enables instant shopper recognition and offer application before payment completion, driving higher revenue.  
- **Talon.One co‑founders Christoph Gerber & Sebastian Haas**: joining Adyen embeds real‑time decisioning in every transaction, allowing merchants to link customer identity with pricing and promotions across channels.  

## Advisors
- Legal counsel: De Brauw Blackstone Westbroek and Hengeler Mueller.  
- Financial due‑diligence advisor: KPMG Advisory.
