3. **Rotate voice‑print enrollments:** delete and re‑record for Google Voice Match, Amazon Alexa Voice ID, Apple Personal Voice, and any banking voice‑print services.

## Forensic checklist used by experts
- **Codec mismatch:** audio claimed to be a phone call but lacks telephony codec signatures.  
- **Breath patterns:** synthetic speech often omits natural inhalations or places breaths incorrectly.  
- **Micro‑jitter:** real voices show millisecond‑level irregularities; clones are overly smooth.  
- **Formant trajectory:** vowel transitions may follow impossible paths in generated audio.  
- **Room acoustics inconsistency:** reverberation should be uniform; generated clips often switch between dry and reverberant sections.  
- **Prosody flatness:** limited pitch and energy variation compared with natural speech.  
- **Speech rate stability:** clones tend to maintain a metronomic speed across long passages.
