## Core Methodology
1. Identify demand in the niche  
2. Categorize user intent (educational, problem‑aware, tool comparison)  
3. Align content type with intent (problem‑aware content converts ~54% better in some niches)  
4. Focus on the 20% of content that drives results, discard the rest  
5. Track keywords that lead to actual sign‑ups, optimize for conversions rather than vanity metrics
