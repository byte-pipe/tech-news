## Summary

The author had been relying on a second external monitor for productivity in their studio space. However, due to excessive screen usage, they began experiencing negative effects such as slowed thinking and increased eye movement. - Using multiple monitors can be efficient but may lead to decreased cognitive abilities.
- The author's experience serves as a reminder of the importance of "the hard way" over shortcuts.
- Simplifying work environments can have positive effects on productivity and overall well-being.
