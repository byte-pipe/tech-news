## FreeCodeCamp: An Open-Source Codebase and Curriculum for Developers

### Overview
FreeCodeCamp is an open-source codebase and curriculum developed by coders under the guidance of experienced mentors. The platform offers a wide range of courses, projects, and competitions in programming, web development, and other areas.

### Key Concepts
* **Public**: FreeCodeCamp's website is publicly accessible.
* **License:** BSD-3-Clause license, making their code available for use, modification, and distribution.
* **Stars**: Users can rate the quality of discussions, assignments, and mentors on various projects.
* **Forks**: A measure of the popularity and activity level of an open-source project.

**Contributions**
* Anyone with an account can contribute to the platform by creating content, solving problems, or leading projects.
* **Notifications:** Users must be signed in to change notification settings for updates and feedback.
