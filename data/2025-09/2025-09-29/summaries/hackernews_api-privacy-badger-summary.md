# Privacy Badger

**Privacy Badger: A Free Browser Extension to Guard Your Online Privacy**

**Overview**
----------


Privacy Badger is a free browser extension developed by the leading digital rights nonprofit EFF that aims to protect users from unwanted online tracking and advertising. The extension blocks advertisers and other third-party trackers, preventing them from collecting sensitive user data without explicit consent.

**Key Features**
----------------

* Blocks ads on websites that have been compromised or are not properly configured
* Automatically detects and prevents tracking by multiple sources
* Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Opera, and other browsers
* Offers alternative installation options for users who wish to use the extension in their preferred browser
* Provides a menu-based interface for accessing settings and information

**What Are Third-Party Trackers?**
-------------------------------

* Third-party trackers refer to any entity that collects data about a user's behavior on websites without their explicit permission.
* Examples of third-party trackers include advertisers' cookies, social media analytics tool providers, and ad networks.

**How Does Privacy Badger Prevent Tracking?**
-----------------------------------------

* The extension creates a "red" slider in the menu bar when tracking is enabled
* Users can then select from three sliders to adjust their level of tracking protection: red for full protection, yellow for partial blocking, and green for disabling tracking altogether

**Why Isn't Privacy Badger Blocking All Ads?**
-------------------------------------------

* Some ads may still be allowed or even blocked by the browser's built-in security features
* Users must manually add ad blockers to websites that have been compromised or offer poor privacy configurations

**What Happens if a User Dies with Unwanted Tracking Running on Their Profile?**
//-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


* EFF has been notified, and their dedicated support team is working to remove any tracking-related data from deceased users' profiles
* Users should not rely on this information as it only pertains to deceased individuals
