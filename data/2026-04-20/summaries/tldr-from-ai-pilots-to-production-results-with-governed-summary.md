**The Gap Between Experimentation and Execution: Overcoming the Challenges of AI in Production**

Rackspace Technology is addressing a major challenge in the use of artificial intelligence (AI) by providing managed services that bridge the gap between experimentally successful projects and production-level impact. Key issues include:

*   **Data governance**: Collecting, managing, and integrating high-quality data is crucial for reliable AI outputs.
*   **Infrastructure and alignment**: Establishing a unified data foundation requires aligning teams across systems and business processes.
*   **Scaling and practicality**: Scaling AI capabilities remains impractical without addressing fragmented datasets and the need for deeper expertise in data systems.

**Mitigating these Challenges through Partnerships**

Rackspace Technology works alongside Palantir to help organizations operationalize AI. By combining platform capabilities with engineering execution, enterprises can overcome the challenges of experimentation and execute AI effectively:

*   **Platform collaboration**: Platforms like Palantir Foundry and AIP create a connected data layer that enables seamless integration into day-to-day operations.
*   **Expertise building**: Rackspace Technology offers support in aligning data, systems, and business processes to operationalize AI.

**Key Takeaways**

*   Effective execution of AI requires more than just technology – it demands partnerships between organizations with complementary expertise.
*   Addressing the challenges of experimentation and execution is critical for realizing the full potential of AI in businesses.
