# Anthropic expands partnership with Google and Broadcom for multiple gigawatts of next-generation compute \ Anthropic

**Anthropic Expands Partnership with Google and Broadcom for Next-Generation Compute**

Anthropic has signed a partnership agreement with Google and Broadcom to provide multiple gigawatts of next-generation Trained Precision Turing (TPU) capacity. This expansion is expected to come online in 2027, supporting the company's growth-oriented approach.

**Key Facts:**

* The partnership will power the development of new Frontier Claude models.
* Demand for Anthropic's offerings has increased significantly since the company raised its Series G funding last year.
* Over 1,000 customers are now investing at least $1 million annually with an average spend of twice that amount (over $2 million) in less than two months.

**US Expansion:**

* The majority of new compute will be stationed in the United States.
* Existing investments in US infrastructure, including a November 2025 commitment to $50 billion, are continuing under this agreement.
