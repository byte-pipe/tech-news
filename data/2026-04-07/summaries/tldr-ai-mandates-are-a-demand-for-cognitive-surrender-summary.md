# AI mandates are a demand for cognitive surrender

# Cognitive Surrender: The Devastating Consequences of Abandoning Critical Thinking

* **The Threat to Critical Thinking**: Studies show that reducing our scope of judgment to checking AI outputs leads to abandoning critical thinking altogether. This phenomenon, dubbed "cognitive surrender," threatens the ability to evaluate information and make informed decisions.

# Examples of Cognitive Surrender in Action

* 73.2% of subjects accepted faulty AI reasoning despite being able to overrule it.
* Only 19.7% of subjects successfully overruled faulty AI reasoning.
* Excessive relying on fluff, or "false confidence," from AI systems (as seen with the paper's findings).

# The Consequences of Cognitive Surrender

* **Lack of accountability**: When people trust AI to make decisions, they also lose accountability for those conclusions.
* **Rising reliance on automated systems**: As a result, we're becoming increasingly reliant on tools that prioritize speed over accuracy and responsibility.

**The Impact on Leadership & Innovation**

* **Blurred lines between innovator and party pooper**: Surrendering to AI can lead individuals to be seen as innovators rather than critics. This creates confusion for stakeholders who want to have a clear understanding of where accountability lies.
* **Pressure to produce results quickly**: With the expectation of rapid progress, teams are being pressured into automating decision-making processes without fully considering the consequences.

**A Call to Action**

* Recognize that critical thinking is still essential in today's world and prioritize its preservation amidst the demands of productivity and efficiency.
* Take responsibility for evaluating AI-driven outcomes and accept some level of accountability when possible.
