# 🎙️ Podcasts

Host, distribute, and monetize your podcast directly on beehiiv.

Tyler Denk

Apr 2, 2026

You can now launch, host, and monetize your podcast entirely on beehiiv. 🎙️

Publish an episode, share it with your subscribers, and track performance and growth—all in one place.

No more juggling tools. No more paying three platforms to do one job. And no more giving up your revenue, because we don’t take a cent.

Welcome to the podcast era. Let’s dive in 👇️

No podcast yet? This is your sign. In just a few clicks, you can launch your first show and start building audience relationships beyond the inbox.

Or if you already have one hosted elsewhere, you can cut that subscription and migrate your entire archive over to beehiiv in just a few minutes.

Upload new episodes directly to beehiiv, and we’ll automatically distribute them to every major podcast platform: Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Overcast, Castro, and beyond.

Behind the scenes, we supportMP3, M4A, and WAV, with automatic audio normalization so every episode sounds polished and consistent. Every upload also includes a full transcript, helping you get discovered in search and LLMs, improving accessibility, and turning every episode into reusable content.

### Your podcast🤝your website

Since your podcast is fully integrated with your beehiiv website, each episode has its own SEO-optimized webpage to drive traffic and capture subscribers.

So instead of sending your audience to Apple, Spotify, or YouTube, you can keep them in your ecosystem. Just send them to your episode page, where they can listen or choose their preferred player.

We even built an interactive transcript that lets listeners skim, search, and jump to exactly what they want. It’s an audio experience designed around your audience.

And because podcast pages are powered by the beehiiv Website Builder, each one can be customized down to the last pixel. Explore the drag-and-drop editor, or use our AI tools to build your site from scratch with just a prompt.

### Metrics that matter

Our podcast analytics are built to IAB standards, with breakdowns by country, listening app, device, and operating system, plus episode-level downloads and real-time data. No more waiting 24 hours for reports.

We also built advanced bot filtering to separate real listeners from noise, so you know exactly who’s tuning in.

### Unified monetization

Bundle your podcast with your existing subscription and offer a private feed to paying subscribers. It’s a new way to create a premium experience for your most engaged audience, with exclusive episodes, early access, and more.

No third-party tools. No separate payment flows. No asking subscribers to pay twice. And unlike other platforms that feel entitled to a cut, beehiiv doesn’t take a cent.

### One platform to rule them all.

When your podcast listeners, newsletter readers, and website visitors all live in one unified platform, you can get more out of your audience.

On beehiiv, your content goes further:

* Publish an episode and notify your audience instantly.
* Capture downloads through your integrated website and link in bio.
* Then build automations based on how people actually engage.

You can now offer a cohesive audience experience without juggling a half-dozen different tools. What are you waiting for?
