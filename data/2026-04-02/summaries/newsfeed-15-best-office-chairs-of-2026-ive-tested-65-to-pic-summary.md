## Buying Guide Highlights  
- **Sit properly**: keep feet flat, knees at ~90°, hips slightly higher than knees, and back against the lumbar support.  
- **Common adjustments**: seat height, depth, tilt, recline tension, arm‑rest height/width/angle, lumbar depth, and headrest height.  
- **Accessories**: consider a footrest, monitor arm, or seat cushion if additional support is needed.  
- **Avoid**: chairs with limited adjustability, low‑quality foam that retains heat, or upholstery that pills quickly.
