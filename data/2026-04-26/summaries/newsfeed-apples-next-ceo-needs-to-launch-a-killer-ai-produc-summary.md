- Millions use AI, but many remain skeptical; Apple must make AI accessible and trustworthy for the mass market.

- Ternus has spent most of his career out of the public eye, known as a methodical operator rather than a visionary.  
- He emphasizes shipping “amazing products, features, and experiences” without exposing underlying technology.
