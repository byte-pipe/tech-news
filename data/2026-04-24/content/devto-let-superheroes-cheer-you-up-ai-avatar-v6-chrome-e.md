## Intro

I created anAI avatar, a VRM 3D character that reacts when you chat with AI or press Enter, and I keep updating it. I have been using the same avatar (Ninja Girl) as the default avatar from the beginning. Although you can change the avatar to your own, it would be more fun if there were several default avatars and each had its own animations and stories. We can change the avatars based on our mood. This time, my coworker Maximum Blue helped me make avatars. Thanks a lot!

## Version 5 Updates

I also made several updates, fixes and adjustments.

Added bones to the fingers.In the previous versions, the hands were always open. Now we can make peace signs and close the hands. This makes the avatar more lively.

Changed the values of animation from radians (e.g., 1.57) to degrees (e.g., 90), so that we can understand them intuitively.I also kept backward compatibility for the old files.

Made the background gray.I realized that when the avatar is wearing black gloves, we cannot see them well because the background is black. Now users can choose the background color from black or gray.

Fixed theEnter key in the terminal to trigger animations(VS Code). Users can enjoy the animations in more situations with this update.
