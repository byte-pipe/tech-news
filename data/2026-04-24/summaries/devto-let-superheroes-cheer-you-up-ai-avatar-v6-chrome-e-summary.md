## Version 5 Updates
- Added finger bones, enabling hand gestures such as peace signs and closed fists.
- Switched animation values from radians to degrees for easier understanding, while keeping backward compatibility.
- Changed default background to gray (optional black) to improve visibility of black‑gloved avatars.
- Fixed the Enter‑key trigger for animations in the VS Code terminal.
