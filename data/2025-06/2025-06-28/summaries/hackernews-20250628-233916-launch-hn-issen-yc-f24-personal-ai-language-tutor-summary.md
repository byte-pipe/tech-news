# Launch HN: Issen (YC F24) – Personal AI language tutor | Hacker News

The article discusses a solo developer business called Issen (YC F24) that offers a personal AI language tutor app for users to practice speaking Japanese and French in real conversations. The product excites Mariano and Anton, the founders, as it aims to improve the learning experience by simulating real-life interactions, reducing the reliance on expensive tutors, and offering an efficient way to learn languages.

The problem being discussed is that there are existing tools for language learning that can be too expensive, difficult to schedule, and not effective in teaching fluency. The founders of Issen recognized this need and created a custom voice AI pipeline combining various technologies to create a more natural conversation experience.
