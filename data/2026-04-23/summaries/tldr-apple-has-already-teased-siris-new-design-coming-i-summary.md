# Apple has already teased Siri’s new design coming in iOS 27

## Overview
- Bloomberg analyst Mark Gurman says WWDC26 artwork gives the first look at Siri’s redesign for iOS 27.  
- The new visual style features a glow effect similar to the WWDC poster, most striking in dark mode.  
- Siri will launch from the Dynamic Island, expanding into a glowing pill with a “Search or Ask” prompt.

## Design Details
- The expanded pill mirrors the glowing “26” in the conference logo.  
- The search‑bar text field in the standalone Siri app receives the same glow treatment.  
- Apple VP Greg Joswiak’s WWDC announcement video also displayed the glow effect.

## Comparison to Previous Redesign
- This is the second Siri redesign in the large‑language‑model era.  
- The first, “Apple Intelligence,” used a rainbow lighting effect and shipped in iOS 18, but its intended features were delayed, prompting a redesign.

## New Functionality
- Conversational AI: supports ongoing back‑and‑forth dialogue and multiple requests in a single utterance.  
- Adds personal context and onscreen awareness to interactions.  
- Powered by new foundation models built on Google Gemini technology.  
- A separate Siri app will let users view past conversations.  
- Siri and Spotlight search interfaces are expected to be unified.

## Release Timeline
- Siri’s redesign is slated as the headline feature of iOS 27.  
- Apple will preview it at WWDC26, with the keynote on June 8 and the conference running June 8‑12.
