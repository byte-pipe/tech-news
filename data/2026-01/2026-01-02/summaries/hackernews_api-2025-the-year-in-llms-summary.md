## Overview of 2025 LLMs Trends

The year 2025 saw a diverse range of trends in the LLM space.

### Key Discoveries

- **Reasoning**: OpenAI started implementing Reinforcement Learning from Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) and achieved significant improvements, showcasing the potential for models to perform reasoning tasks beyond inference.
- **Hybrid Models**: Several labs released hybrid models that could operate in both reasoning and non-reasoning modes.

### Notable Releases

- Notably, many notable AI laboratories:
  - Released at least one reasoning model
  - Exchanged or introduced hybrids of reasoning-enabled versus non-reasoning modes
  - Implemented Reasoning dials for customizing reasoning behavior

### Highlights

- 2025 Models Achieved ~Similar LLM Sizes but Long RL Runs Due to Overhead from New Stage of Development Requires High Compute Resources
- Several Notable Labs Conducted Successful Experiments with Reasoning Capabilities, Including Demonstrations Solving Math Logic Problems and Counting Objectives
