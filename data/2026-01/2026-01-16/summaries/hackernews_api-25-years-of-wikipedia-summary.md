# 25 years of Wikipedia

**25 Years of Wikipedia**

### Introduction

Wikipedia has been a groundbreaking online encyclopedia since its inception in 2001. Over two decades, it has revolutionized the way we access and share information.

### Key Statistics

* Wikipedia is the world's largest edited website, with over 50 million articles.
* It has more than 200,000 active editors worldwide.
* The English version of Wikipedia has been credited with providing reliable information to millions of people.

### History

Wikipedia was founded by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger in February 2001. Initially, it started as a small project called "Nupedia," but soon evolved into a full-fledged online encyclopedia.


* **Development:** The first version of Wikipedia, Nupedia, was launched on January 15, 2000. It contained basic information on various subjects.
* **Open-source model:** In March 2001, Jimmy Wales became the sole editor-in-chief of Wikipedia. He introduced an open-source model, allowing anyone to access and edit articles.
* **First website:** The first version of Wikipedia was successfully launched online in February 2001.


### Wikipedia's Impact

Wikipedia has had a profound impact on various fields:

* **Democratization of information:** Wikipedia has provided free and editable content to people worldwide, bridging language barriers.
* **Research and scholarship:** Its vast repository of knowledge has improved research outcomes, particularly in humanities and social sciences.
* **Education:** Wikipedia has expanded educational opportunities, making it an attractive platform for students to learn.

### Future Directions

As the largest online encyclopedia, Wikipedia faces ongoing challenges:

* **Security concerns:** Users need to exercise caution when editing articles to prevent malicious content and vandalism.
* **Citations and references:** Proper citation practices are crucial to maintain accuracy and credibility.
* **Interdisciplinary collaboration:** Collaboration between experts in different fields is essential for creating accurate and comprehensive content.

### Conclusion

Wikipedia has come a long way since its inception. Its commitment to open-source information sharing, collaborative editing, and quality research demonstrates the power of online communities in shaping our understanding of the world.
