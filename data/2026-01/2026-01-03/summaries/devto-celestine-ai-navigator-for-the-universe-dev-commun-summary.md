# Celestine: AI Navigator for the Universe 🪐 - DEV Community

**Dev's Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge Submission: Celestine - An Intelligent AI Navigator for the Universe**

**What I Built:**

Celestine is an intelligent, multi-modal AI navigator for the Solar System. It extends the intuitive experience of Google Maps to the cosmos by allowing users to explore planets in 3D and "land" on them to discover their secrets.

**Key Features:**

* **AI Co-Pilot:** An AI agent powered by Gemini 2.5 acts as a bridge between alien worlds and our own.
* **Geological Analogies:** The AI finds a similar geological feature on Earth, connecting the user's cosmic journey to their home planet.
* **Multimodal Interaction:** Users can interact with Celestine via voice or real-time video avatar.

**Project Overview:**

Google Maps mastered the navigation of Earth, but what about the rest of the Universe? I builtCelestine to revive the dream of a "Space Mode" and make it interactive, personal, and intelligent.

**Technical Highlights:**

* Full-stack application combining 3D rendering with advanced AI agent orchestration.
* Frontend: React + react-three-fiber for immersive 3D solar system.
* Back end: Python (FastAPI) + containerized Docker deployment on Google Cloud Run.
