# Making sure you're not a bot!

## Making Sure You're Not a Bot

### What Makes You Suspicious?

*   **Unusual login requests**: Being asked to re-enter personal details, passwords, or account information may trigger suspicion.
*   **Multiple sign-in attempts from the same device**: Attempting multiple times within a short period indicates potential unauthorized access.
*   **Lack of password changes after initial login attempt**: If passwords do not change after an individual's first login attempt, it may indicate automation.

### How to Verify Yourself

1.  **Check your account activity**: Review recent transactions and verify that all financial or important accounts have unique login information.
2.  **Use two-factor authentication (2FA) when possible**: Enable 2FA whenever available to add an extra layer of security against bot attacks.
3.  **Monitor for unusual behavior**: Periodically check the account and surrounding areas online for suspicious activity.

### Stay Safe Online

*   Be cautious with links and attachments from unknown or unusual sources
*   Avoid using public Wi-Fi networks without verifying their security first
*   Regularly update software, operating systems, and browser versions to stay protected
