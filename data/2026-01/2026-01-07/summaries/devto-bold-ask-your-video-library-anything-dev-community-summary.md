### What is Bold?

### The Story Behind It

I've been building video infrastructure for nearly two decades. A headless video platform was my starting point – just encoding, hosting, and delivery. Some of my former customers are still using it today.

In recent years, Large Language Models (LLMs) significantly improved the problem I initially sought to solve: extracting valuable insights from videos that defy traditional searching methods. With the rise of coaching programs through SaaS Academy, I encountered numerous issues when scouring their vast content libraries. The outcome was often a collection of dusty videos with no answers accessible.

### How Bold Works
By integrating the most effective transcription tools and custom dictionaries, we've devised an intelligent search method that extracts structured knowledge from each lecture. Our tool now searches actively through video content to provide the right answers to user queries with timestamps verifying them.

The real beauty lies in how these insights come directly from your library. Mux has been instrumental in my journey as a developer, and I look forward to working together to bring this innovation to more people.
