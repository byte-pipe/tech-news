# Building my MVP: Glyph — Your AI-Powered Writing Assistant - DEV Community

# DEV's Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge Submission

## What I Built
I built the MVP of my startup called Glyph.

Glyph is a word editor with an AI assistant that can:

* Write for you from scratch
* Edit text however you like
* Answer questions about what you're writing
* Let you write yourself if you prefer

## Key Features and Highlights

- Frontend: Responsive, Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript interface built using TipTapas rich-text editor.
- Backend: Django handles API logic, user session management, and interacts with third-party services for seamless use.
- AI Engine: Llama 3 model is used for the MVP, prioritizing cost efficiency for production readiness. I'm planning to switch to GPT-4 or Claude in production.
- Video Intelligence: Built by Muxshi for real-time video-to-text transcription.

## Story Behind Glyph
As a student and developer, I've always experienced frustration while writing. From finding the next word to manually transcribing meeting notes, my experience was inefficient.

I developed Glyph as an accessible solution that makes writing faster, less stressful, and more focused on ideas rather than mechanics. Our goal is to help users be creative without sacrificing clarity or speed.
