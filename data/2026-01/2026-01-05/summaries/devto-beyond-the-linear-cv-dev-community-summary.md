# Beyond the Linear CV - DEV Community Submission

## Introduction

In the pursuit of a modern personal brand, careers don't fit neatly into traditional chronological timelines. Instead, they unfold as networks of interconnected skills, projects, and experiences.

## About The Project

This portfolio app aims to redefine the way we showcase our professional journeys. For those who prefer a more non-linear approach, "New Year, New You" challenges them to represent their careers in a dynamic, interactive format – not a static timeline.

## Portfolio Product

* **Live Demo:** A fully functional AI Knowledge Graph CV Builder that loads a user's personal portfolio as an immersive experience.
* **Features:**
	+ Network Graph: Visual representation of skills, projects, and expertise domains
	+ Flow Diagram: Displaying career trajectory through connections between skills and experiences
	+ Skills Matrix: Heatmap highlighting the overlap of technology usage across projects
	+ Zero Friction: Upload-only feature to explore in action

## Career Evolution Demo

A hands-on demo demonstrating how the portfolio can track career changes, including pivots from legacy industries (Flash, ORACLE) to modern ones (Astro, LLM/RAG).

Key Points:

* From corporate generalist to independent specialist with 8 core technologies reduced
* Transitioned focus from Legacy Tech (~5) to Modern Stack (+23%) and broader scope of specialty
* Focus expanded from Corporate Generalist to Independent Specialist

## Technical Implementation Philosophy

The development process followed the following principles:
- Design for User Experience (UX), prioritizing an intuitive interface and engaging layout.
- Leverage AI-powered technology, incorporating a Knowledge Graph API that captures project connections and skills usage hierarchies.
- Utilize real-world testing examples from various career profiles to iterate on the design.

## Conclusion

The proposed portfolio app, "New Year, New You," seeks to revolutionize the way we document careers. By redefining time lines as dynamic networks of expertise, it challenges individuals to represent their professional journeys in a more meaningful and interactive manner – ultimately enhancing self-awareness, creativity, and adaptability within complex industries.
