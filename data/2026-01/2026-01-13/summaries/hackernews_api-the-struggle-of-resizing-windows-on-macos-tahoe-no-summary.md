**Identical Usability Concerns**

While aesthetics may offer a variation in decision-making, usability concerns remain. The problem lies not with the users' preferences but rather with the inherent functionality and design flaws of macOS Tahoe's window management system.

**The Problem: Window Corner Radius**

The unique "corner" shape on these windows causes issues when attempting to drag or resize them. Because most of the available pixels in these corners actually lie outside a user-friendly area, dragging anywhere near it triggers errors instead of successful resizing.

*   To put this into perspective, only 25% (1/4) of the window area lies within the expected response range.
*   This disparity causes frequent failures to resize windows due to users attempting to drag their cursor in unintended regions.
