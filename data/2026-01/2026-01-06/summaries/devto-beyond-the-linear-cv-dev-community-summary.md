# Beyond the Linear CV - DEV Community

**Google AI Challenge Submission**

**About Me**

I didn't follow a traditional career path. My professional journey is non-linear, composed of skill acquisitions across different contexts and transitions that defy the classic portfolio format.

**Portfolio**: A "New Year, New You" Solution

I created an app called live demo:AI Knowledge Graph CV Builder to represent my career evolution in a novel way.

## What's in the Portfolio

* **Network Graph**: A visual representation of skills and projects as interconnected nodes.
* **Flow Diagram**: Demonstrates the progression from skills to projects to specialized areas.
* **Skills Matrix**: Displays the heatmap of which projects use which technologies.
* **Zero Friction Upload**: No upload required to explore my portfolio immediately.

**Visualizing Career Evolution**

I tested the app using my CV from 2021 versus one from 2025. The graph reveals strategic pivots, such as shifting from a corporate generalist to an independent specialist.
