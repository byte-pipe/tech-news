### Summary:
Google denied my DMCA complaint to remove a pirated copy of my book from their search results. After receiving the email denying my existence, I asked how to prove my identity. The Google Team then questioned my copyright ownership.

*   They claimed I don't have permission to submit a copyright removal request.
*   They suggested that only the copyright owner or their authorized representative could submit such a request, implying legal consequences if accurate information is not provided.
