## # Anna’s Archive Loses .Org Domain After Surprise Suspension
The web archive's main domain, annas-archive.org, lost its availability after a sudden suspension due to an investigation by domain registry Public Interest Registry (PIR). This action was not voluntary and is under the grounds of a court order.

## Key Points:

• Anna’s Archive has been operating since 2022 as a metadata search engine for pirated books and sources.
• The site has experienced pushback from rightsholders, leading to its domain being suspended in various countries.
• This suspension occurred after Spotify was backed up by the archive.
• Rightsholders had filed for an injunction targeting the domain name, however no evidence suggests that this is the case.
• Another website named PirateBay.org was previously targeted in a legal crackdown and has not undergone these same actions.

## What Happened to Anna’s Archive’s .Org Domain

• The website originally launched as annas-archive.org
• Recently moved to .GS domain while anticipating potential seizure
• Suspected that suspension of the .ORG domain through Public Interest Registry was related due to its "DRM-circumventing" Spotify backup request
