# Pebble Round 2 - The Most Stylish Pebble Ever | rePebble Blog

**Pebble Pebble Time Round Reboot: What's New and Improved**
====================================================================================

*   **New Display:** A 1.3-inch color e-paper display covers the entire face, replacing the bezel from the original Pebble Time Round.
*   **Longer Battery Life:** Conserves up to two weeks of battery life with Bluetooth chip technology advanced in recent times.
*   **Thinner Profile:** Made from stainless steel and comes in three colors: matte black, brushed silver, polished rose gold.

**Key Features Remain the Same**
------------------------------------

*   **Battery Life:** Still lasts around 2 weeks on low and moderate usage.
*   **Display:** Always-on e-paper screen that reflects light rather than emits it.
*   **Operation System:** Powered by open-source PebbleOS, works great with iOS and Android smartphones.

**Software Updates Available**
------------------------------

*   Includes a built-in health tracking feature with steps and sleep data.
*   Supports watchfaces from over 15,000 apps in the Pebble Appstore or via SDK access.
*   Robust app ecosystem includes thousands of existing watches on its store.
