# BOLD: Ask Your Video Library Anything - DEV Community

**DEV's Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge Submission**

BOLD: A Revolutionary Video Library Search Tool

This is DEV's Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge submission for the "Ask Your Video Library Anything" competition presented by Mux. **Upload your training videos**, and Bold will extract what's being taught, with timestamps to prove it.

*   **What I Built**: A dynamic search application that allows users to ask questions and receive accurate answers without a list of search results.
*   **Demo**: Watch the demo video for a hands-on experience, which utilizes the Yo! Podcast by Rob Hope as an example use case.
*   **Technical Highlights**:
  *   Extracts structured understanding from training videos using customized dictionaries and tools like AssemblyAI, Deepgram, Speechmatics, and Sonix.
  *   Uses this extracted signal to answer questions with timestamps, providing a more accurate search experience.

Key Points:

*   BOLD revolutionizes the way users interact with video libraries by turning them into interactive conversations.
*   It extracts structured understanding first, which is then used to provide accurate answers when asked specific questions.
*   The application provides a seamless experience and utilizes advanced tools like AssemblyAI and Deepgram for enhanced accuracy.

Technical Specifications:

*   Training videos
*   Customized dictionaries and tools (AssemblyAI, Deepgram, Speechmatics, Sonix)
*   Timestamps for answered questions

Benefits:

*   Provides a more accurate search experience by extracting structured understanding from video content.
*   Allows users to interact with their video libraries in a new and innovative way.
