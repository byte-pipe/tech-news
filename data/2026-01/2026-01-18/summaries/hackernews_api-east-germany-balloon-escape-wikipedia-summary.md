**East Germany Balloon Escape**

* **Date and Location:** September 16, 1979, Oberlemnitz, East Germany; Naila, West Germany

**Background**

The East German government was separated from Western Germany by an inner border and the Berlin Wall. A strict "no-entry" policy enforced by heavily fortified borders, land mines, armed soldiers, and watchtowers prevented individuals from attempting to cross.

**Escape Plan**

*   Peter Strelzyk (1942-2017) and Günter Wetzel's plan involved building a hot air balloon capable of carrying eight passengers.
*   Strelzyk came up with the idea in 1978 after reading about a television program on ballooning, while Wetzel experimented to find an engine to power such a craft at local plastics factory.
