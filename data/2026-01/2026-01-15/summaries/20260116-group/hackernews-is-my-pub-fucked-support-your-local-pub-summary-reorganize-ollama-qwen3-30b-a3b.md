# Is My Pub Fucked? | Support Your Local Pub

## Purpose of the Resource
This platform helps patrons identify and support pubs facing financial challenges, particularly amid potential rate changes.

## Finding Your Local Supporting Pubs

### How to Locate Nearby Pubs Needing Support
| Method                | Details                                      |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Google Maps/Postcode  | Search using location tools and postcode     |
| Map Tools             | Utilize interactive maps to locate pubs nearyou |
| Revaluation Awareness | Note pubs affected by 2026 property revaluation |

> **Note**: Government proposals for potential pub rate adjustments are under review but unconfirmed.

## Struggling Pub Index

### What the Index Measures
A data-driven metric that identifies pubs at highest risk of financial strain due to:
- Local property valuation changes
- Shifts in community patronage patterns

### How It Works
| Component              | Description                                  |
|------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Data Sources           | Google Maps data + fiscal impact modeling    |
| Target Pubs            | Geographically proximate venues requiring support |
| Action Guidance        | Clear recommendations for patronage during revaluation periods |
