# Ford F-150 Lightning Outperformed Cybertruck in Sales but Was Canceled Due to Low Production

## Sales Comparison
- Ford F-150 Lightning outsold Tesla Cybertruck in 2025 sales volume
- Despite this sales lead, overall demand was insufficient to support production targets

## Production Efficiency
| Metric                     | Status                       |
|----------------------------|------------------------------|
| Planned production capacity | Not met                       |
| Actual production volume   | ~10% of planned capacity      |
| Indicated demand issue     | Persistent low sales volume   |

## Reason for Cancellation
- Insufficient sales volume directly led to production shutdown
- Production at 10% capacity signaled unsustainable market performance
- Continued operations would have posed financial risks for Ford, similar to challenges faced by Tesla with Cybertruck's initial launch

## Clarification of Contradiction
The term "outsold" refers specifically to comparison with Tesla Cybertruck in 2025, not to overall market success. Despite outperforming its primary competitor, the F-150 Lightning still failed to achieve sufficient sales volume to justify continued manufacturing at scale.
