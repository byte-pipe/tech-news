# Ask HN: Share your personal website

## Overview
A community-maintained directory of personal websites, currently featuring a small number of entries. The goal is to expand the directory through contributions and community reviews.

## How to Participate
- **Share your personal website**: Submit links to your site if it meets the criteria (must be well-received in past Hacker News discussions).
- **Join the GitHub project**: Become a maintainer by replying via IRC or commenting on this post.
