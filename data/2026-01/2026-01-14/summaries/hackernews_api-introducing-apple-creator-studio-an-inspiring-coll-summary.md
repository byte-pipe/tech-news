# Introducing Apple Creator Studio, an inspiring collection of creative apps - Apple

**Apple Introduces Apple Creator Studio: A Comprehensive Creative Suite**

The company has unveiled Apple Creator Studio, an innovative collection of powerhouses designed to empower individual creators, producers, and professionals. This revolutionary suite includes:

* **Final Cut Pro**: Advanced video editing software with intelligent features, perfect for Mac and iPad.
* **Logic Pro**: Music creation app on Mac and iPad, introducing new features like Synth Player and Chord ID.
* **Pixelmator Pro**: Optimized digital painting app for touch devices.
* **Keynote**, **Pages**, and **Numbers**: New premium content and intelligent features for creativity across Mac, iPad, and iPhone.

App Creator Studio is available on the App Store, priced at $12.99 per month or $129 per year, with a one-month free trial. The subscription offers exclusive access to Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro, Pixelmator Pro, Mouse Stage Motion Compressor MainStage apps, Keynote, Pages Numbers and Freeform.

**Key Features:**

* Convenience with single subscription or individual purchase
* Premium support for college students and educators: $2.99 per month or $29.99 per year

By combining these creative tools in one location, Apple Creator Studio provides users with a valuable gateway to their artistic expression.
