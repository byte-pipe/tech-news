# 2025: The year in LLMs

## 2025: The Year in LLMs

### Key Points

* OpenAI's 2024-25 "reasoning" revival began with `o1-mini` and expanded to `o3`, `o4-mini`
* Reasoning capabilities became a signature feature of models from nearly every other major AI lab
* Significant progress was seen in RLVR (Inference Scaling), enabling higher-capability models at reduced compute usage

## The Year of "Reasoning"

### Highlights

* OpenAI's use of RLVR revolutionized LLM training and led to high capability/$ models
* Most notable labs chomged through the new stage, resulting in ~similar-sized LLMs but longer RL runs
* Hybrid models that could run both reasoning and non-reasoning modes were common

### What Reasoning Is Useful For

* Initially showed success with mathematical logic puzzles like counting Rs in strawberry
* Found applications to tasks requiring reasoning, although specific usage was limited
