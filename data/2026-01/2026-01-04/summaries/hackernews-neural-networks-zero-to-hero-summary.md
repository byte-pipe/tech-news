# Neural Networks: Zero To Hero

## Neural Networks: Zero to Hero

A course by Andrej Karpathy that explains building neural networks from scratch using Python.

### Topics Covered

* **Basics**: Introduces backpropagation, derivative, Gaussian distribution
* **Language Modeling**: Spoken input language modeling using bigrams and Transformer architecture
* **MLP Building Blocks**: Multilayer perceptron (MLP), Batch Normalization, activations and gradients, training
* **Debugging**: Inspecting model's health through diagnostics tools

### Key Points

* Neural networks are an excellent place to learn deep learning concepts
* Most new skills will transfer from language models to other areas like computer vision
* Backpropagation is a fundamental concept in neural networks
* Batch Normalization makes training easier by normalizing activations and gradients

### Bullet Points with Subpoints

#### Syllabus

- 2 hours and 25 minutes: Introduction to neural networks and backpropagation
- 57 minutes: Bigram input language modeling, Torch.Tensor details, language modeling framework
- 15 minutes: Multilayer perceptron (MLP) building blocks - multilayer perceptron training, model evaluation
- 55 minutes: Activations & Gradients, BatchNorm (Batch Normalization), pitfalls in neural network training

#### Building Language Models

* Implementing bigram character-level language model and Transformer architecture
* Torch.Tensor for efficient numerical computations and data manipulation
* Model training and evaluation, including loss function and sampling techniques
* Introduction to Adam optimizer, hyperparameters tuning, and train/dev/test splits

#### Additional Topics

* **Activations**: Derivatives, activation functions (hinge, softmax), backpropagation
* **Gradients**: Calculating gradients in forward and backward passes
* **Batch Norm**: Normalizing activations and gradients for efficient computation
* **Residual Connections**: Designing convolutional neural networks with residual blocks
* **Diagnostic Tools and Visualizations**: Understanding model health through tools like tensorboard
