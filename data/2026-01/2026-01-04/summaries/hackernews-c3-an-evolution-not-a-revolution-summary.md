# C3 | An evolution, not a revolution

## TheC3Programming Language Overview

The `C3` programming language is described as an ergonomic, safe, and familiar evolution of the C programming language. It has three primary platforms: Windows, MacOS, and Linux.

### Key Features

- **Portability**: The language's flexibility in achieving portability across different systems makes it a viable option for developers.

- **Ease of Use**: Its similarities with C underscore its ergonomic nature which appeals to experienced programmers.

- **Safety**: This aspect ensures minimum risk to users during application development and deployment processes.
