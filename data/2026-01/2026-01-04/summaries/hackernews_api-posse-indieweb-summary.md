# POSSE - IndieWeb

Here's a concise and informative summary of the article "POSSE - IndieWeb" in Markdown format:

**What is POSSE?**

POSSE is an abbreviation for Publish (on your) Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere. It means posting content on your own site first, then sharing it with others via social media sites.

**Why POSSE?**

* **Friendship Focus**: Instead of trying to maintain contact with distant friends in the future, focus on the current ones.
* **Reducing Dependence on Third-Party Services**
	+ By publishing directly to your own site, you're not dependent on services like Google Search Console or Analytics.
* **Ownership and Control**: Posting first on your own site creates a direct ownership chain that can be traced back to you.
* **Canonical URLs**: You have control over the canonical URLs of your content.

**Benefits**

* Improved search results: Searching through your posts works better from within your domain.
* Backfeed: Use forward syndication (reversing syndication) to pull in responses from other services and stay up-to-date with their social layers.
* Aggregation features: Take advantage of storage capacity from others' social media sites while maintaining a canonical copy of your content on your own site.

**In Summary**

POSSE is an approach that focuses on keeping personal connections with friends closer, rather than trying to expand connections in the future. By publishing directly to your own site and sharing links or copies through third-party services, you maintain control over your audience and content.
