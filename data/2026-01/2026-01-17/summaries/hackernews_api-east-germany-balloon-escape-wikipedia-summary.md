# East Germany Balloon Escape

## Coordinates and Dates

The East German balloon escape occurred on September 16, 1979. The initial flight took place around 2:00 am local time.

## Location
- Takeoff location: Oberlemnitz, East Germany (the craft's departure point)
- Landing location: Naila, West Germany (where the passengers landed safely)

## Organizers and Participants

* Peter Strelzyk & family ( organisers)
* Günter Wetzel & family ( participants in the balloon rise and descent)

## Outcome
SuccessfuI escape with minimal injuries occurred after two attempted flights.

## Background
The story begins with a planned escape by Peter and Günter from East Germany to West Germany. They spent over a year and a half preparing for the escape, involving three balloons, modifications, and failed attempts until their successful second flight two months later.

## Preparation
* The passengers consisted of eight family members from two different families.
> Preparing a homemade hot air balloon was seen as a high-risk strategy after an unsuccessful attempt alerted authorities to their plot.
