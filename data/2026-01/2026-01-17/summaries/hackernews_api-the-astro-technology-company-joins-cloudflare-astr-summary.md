# The Astro Technology Company Joins Cloudflare

The Astro Technology Company, behind the popular web framework Astro, has announced its partnership with Cloudflare. This integration marks an significant milestone in Astro's adoption and further bolsters Cloudflare's offerings as the go-to choice for content-driven websites.

## Key Benefits of Partnership

* **Stay Open-Source and MIT-Licensed**: Astro will continue to be freely available under open-source and MIT licenses, maintaining its flexibility and community engagement.
* **Continued Active Maintenance**: The team behind Astro expects continued support and updates, ensuring that the framework stays current and performant.
* **Expanded Deployment Options**: With Cloudflare's resources, Astro can now cover a wider range of deployment targets beyond its usual cloud-based infrastructure, reducing complexity for developers.
* **Governance and Roadmap Continuity**: All full-time employees of The Astro Technology Company are now part of the Cloudflare team, ensuring continuity of the open governance model and current roadmap.

## Astro's Origins

Astro was born out of frustration in 2021 with the traditional approach to website architecture - building applications and shipping them directly to users. This resulted in a high-ping performance issue due to complex setups like SSR, ISR, etc. The initial mission focused on creating a content-driven framework for websites that better differentiates itself from data-driven applications.

## Community Impact

To date:

* Astro has been downloaded over 1 million times per week
* Many developers worldwide have successfully integrated the framework into their projects, using Astro to build fast and beautiful websites

Established in 2021 as The Astro Technology Company, initially aimed at developing a massive developer platform with optional hosted primitives. Despite attempts at paid hosting and a separate database client, users generally preferred the flexibility offered by the community-driven approach.

Despite challenges and distractions over time, the Astro team persisted in open-sourcing their ongoing e-commerce layer project to bring value back to the community.

Astro will continue to be optimized for performance with Cloudflare's support.
