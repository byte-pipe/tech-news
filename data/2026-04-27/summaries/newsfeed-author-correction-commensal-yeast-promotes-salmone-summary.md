# Author Correction: Commensal yeast promotes Salmonella Typhimurium virulence  

## Summary of Corrections  
- **Text error**: In the “C. albicans enhances Salmonella virulence” section, “cage 3” was mistakenly written instead of “cage 1” in the phrase “Faeces of mice from cage 1.”  
- **Statistical test notation**: Figure 5b,d legends were updated to indicate that Mann‑Whitney U tests were used, replacing the original reference to two‑tailed unpaired Student’s t‑tests.  
- **P‑value adjustments**: In Fig. 5b, the significance bars now show P = 0.0107 and P = 0.0274, replacing the previous values P = 0.0202 and P = 0.0343.  
- **Extended Data Fig. 1a**: Missing data points for mice in cage 1 before infection were added; the legend was changed from “Data are geometric mean ± s.d.” to “Data are median with range.”  
- **Implementation**: All textual and figure changes have been incorporated into the HTML and PDF versions of the article.
