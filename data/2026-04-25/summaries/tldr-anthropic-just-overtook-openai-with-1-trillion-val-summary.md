# Anthropic just overtook OpenAI with $1 trillion valuation

## Summary
- Anthropic’s shares traded on Forge Global reached a $1 trillion valuation, surpassing OpenAI’s roughly $880 billion valuation on the same platform.  
- The previous valuation for Anthropic three months earlier was $380 billion, indicating a rapid increase driven by limited share supply and high investor demand.  
- Investor enthusiasm is linked to Anthropic’s revenue surge, with the annualised run rate climbing from $9 billion in late 2025 to $39 billion in March 2026, spurred by widespread adoption of its Claude Code tool and partnerships with Amazon and Palantir.  
- Multiple investors reported unsolicited offers for their Anthropic stakes, with valuations cited at $1.05 trillion, $960 billion, and even proposals to exchange property for shares.  
- Comments from early investors and venture partners highlighted the “generational opportunity” perception, suggesting the appeal is as much about prestige as financial return.  
- The Independent sought comments from both Anthropic and OpenAI but had not received a response at the time of publication.
