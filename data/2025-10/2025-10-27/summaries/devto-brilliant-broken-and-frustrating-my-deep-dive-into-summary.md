# Brilliant, Broken, and Frustrating: My Deep Dive into Amazon’s Kiro AI IDE, the Flawed Junior Developer - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

### Key Points

* Kiro AI IDE is a new agentic tool from AWS that differs from traditional "vibe coding" by injecting engineering discipline into aid-assisted development.
* The core mission of Kiro is to create a structured, spec-driven workflow using Mermaid.js for requirements, design, and tasks.
* This approach forces users to plan and clarify their intent upfront, providing rigour in software engineering.

### Contradictions

1. **Contrasting Philosophy**: While Kiro's goal is clear, its process can feel rigid and slower due to the need for requirement gathering and updates of specifications.
2. **Design Decision Transparency**: Unlike some AI tools that present unstructured ideas, Kiro keeps design decisions transparent.
3. **No "Walled Garden" Reputation**: By offering an independent platform not branded as AWS, developers may gain more trust in the tool.

### Summary

* Using Kiro can provide engineers with a structured process to meet their requirements and build specifications effectively
* However, it requires discipline and focus due to its iterative design phase and specification management systems.
* Users can gain developer-friendly features like logging support without needing an AWS account.
* While Kiro aims to address the drawbacks of traditional vibe coding, its own limitations pose interesting contrasts with other AI tool offerings.
