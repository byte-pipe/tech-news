# Building Disco Week 2: Making Event Networking Less Random 🪩 - DEV Community

## Building Disco: Documenting the Journey Towards an In-Person Networking App

### Early Insights from My Journey

I started building a networking app called Discomore two weeks ago. Our goal is to provide a platform for in-person events, helping users find the right people at those events based on their goals.

### Overcoming Initial Challenges

My journey so far has been filled with excitement and fear of speaking about my ideas publicly. Despite initial hesitation, making connections with potential team members, setting clear visions for our MVP, securing partnerships, and sticking to a daily work routine became essential parts of the building process.

### Realizing the Complexity of Networking Events

As an introvert, I often find networking events overwhelming due to the awkward small talk and frantic search for someone new to speak to. Most meaningful connections I've made at events happened by chance. However, my experience has led me to wonder: what if I were able to meet potential collaborators or clients at events without relying on luck?

Disco aims to solve this problem, connecting users based on their shared goals and interests. By creating profiles sharing their desired outcomes for attending events, users can find like-minded people at conferences and meetups.

### What Disco Can Offer

Currently, the app is a prototype with basic features, but I'm willing to take feedback from others who've tried it out and be open to iterating on our idea. Dropping a comment or sharing your thoughts on using Discomore would mean the world to me!

Disco is my project to create the perfect solution for networking events - let's get this conversation started!
