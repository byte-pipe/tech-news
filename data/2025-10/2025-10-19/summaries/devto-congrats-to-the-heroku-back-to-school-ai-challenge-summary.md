# Congrats to the Heroku "Back to School" AI Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

**Heroku "Back to School" AI Challenge Winners**

The winners of the Heroku "Back to School" AI Challenge have been announced, and we are excited to share their achievement with you.

*   **Crazy Creative Winner**: @prime_299792 created a comprehensive AI-powered study management platform, Study-Mate, which goes beyond traditional study tools. This solution leverages MentorMind, a multi-agent architecture with intelligent orchestration between GPT-OSS, Nova Lite, and Claude agents.
*   **Educator Empowerment Winner**: @victor_lakra_e1910abe17fcbuilt an AI-powered teacher assistant, Teacher Assistant, which transforms the lesson planning process for educators. This application enables teachers to upload teaching materials and receive comprehensive lesson plans in minutes.
*   **Student Success Winner**: @juliodiaz0209 created a powerful multi-agent learning platform, StudyFlow AI, which coordinates seven specialized AI agents working together to provide personalized learning experiences. Theseagents use Heroku's Model Context Protocol for real-time agent communication, pgvector for context storage, and Managed Inference.

**Benefits of Participating in the Challenge**

*   A chance to build with cutting-edge AI features
*   Opportunity to demonstrate creative approaches to solving educational problems
*   App prize: $1,000 USD plus DEV++ Membership and an exclusive badge

Each winner will receive a completion badge for rising to the challenge.
