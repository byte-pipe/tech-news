# Agentic Engineering: Lessons Learned Vol. 1 - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article "Context Engineering 101" by DEV Community:

**Summary**

The article discusses the importance of **context engineering** in agentic engineering, specifically highlighting its role in providing software development with the necessary information to perform tasks.

**Key Points**

* Context engineering is not just about prompt engineering, but a more dynamic skill that encompasses instructions, knowledge, tools, and documentation.
* The context is crucial for stateless LLMs, as it provides the input required for the model to perform a task.

**Main Issues with Long Contexts**

The article warns against common pitfalls when working with long contexts:

• **Context Poisoning**: errors or hallucinations entering the context and getting repeated
• A lack of clear boundaries between categories (e.g., prompt vs. instructions, knowledge vs. tools)

**Best Practices for Effective Context Engineering**

To get it right, developers should consider the following strategies:

• Understand that context is more than just a prompt and all other aspects need to be considered.
• Use smart people's frameworks like Claude Code to validate best practices.
• Be aware of common issues like context poisoning.

**Validation Statement**

The article concludes with a validated statement: Context engineering is becoming increasingly valuable, but it requires careful consideration and validation for individual use cases.
