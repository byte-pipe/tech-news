# Open Printer Summary

The Open Printer is a unique inkjet printer designed to bring open-source ethos and simplicity to the world of printing. Its key features include:

* **DIY assembly**: Built with standard mechanical components and modular parts for easier modification and repair
* **Standard components**: Runs on a Raspberry Pi W board, reducing proprietary dependencies and hardware complexity
* **Open design**: Mechanical design files will be open sourced, allowing users to customize and modify the printer as desired
* **Innovative printing capabilities**: Prints not only standard A4 and A3 sheets but also onto 27 mm wide paper rolls via an integrated cutter

The Open Printer's interface features a small LCD screen and jog wheel, with wireless connectivity via Wi-Fi and Bluetooth for easy job management. Its compact design includes an elongated shape that allows it to be mounted on a desktop or wall.

**Key Features:**

* Supports standard HP 63 (or HP 302 in Europe) cartridges
* Dual-ink printing for black and color ink
* Standard paper roll compatibility
* Integrated cutter for trimming paper rolls
* Open design files available for customization

**Availability and Pricing:**

* Launching through a crowdfunding campaign on Crowd Supply
* Details on pricing and availability have not been shared yet.

The Open Printer aims to provide a reliable, open-source printer solution that simplifies the printing process while minimizing dependency on proprietary hardware.
