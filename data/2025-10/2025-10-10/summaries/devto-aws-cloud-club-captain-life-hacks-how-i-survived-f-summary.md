# AWS Cloud Club Captain Life Hacks: How I Survived Final Year Without Losing My Mind (Much) - DEV Community

**Surviving Final Year Without Losing My Mind (Much) - Tips and Tricks as an AWS Cloud Club Captain**

Being the final-year engineering student and AWS Cloud Club Captain required balance, leadership, and adaptability. Here are key takeaways to help me navigate life's challenges.

### **Managing Time and Tasks Like a Pro**

*   **Divide and Conquer**: Team management is crucial. Assign each role and task, providing mini-ecosystems for technical, design, social media, events, documentation, and logistics teams.
*   **Time Management ≠ Magic**: Focus on securing event approvals from college offices to prioritize cloud events over "luxury time" (emphasis added).

### **Staying Motivated Despite Challenges**

*   **Show Impact**: Acknowledge team members' contributions by highlighting the positive outcomes of their efforts.
*   **Keep it Light-Ribbed**: Balance gentle encouragement with tough love, as motivating teams in the field can be unpredictable.

### **Cloud Management for the Modern Captain**

*   **Document Everything**: Templates and pre-approved designs enable swift resource creation on AWS.
*   **Panic Page**: Prepare a central hub for event agendas, contacts, backup slides, and all information in one place.
*   **Backup Plans Are Your First Plans**: Have a prepared response in case of last-minute speaker cancellations or other unexpected events.

### **Lessons Learned**

This experience taught me that leadership is about embracing uncertainty, finding innovative solutions, and prioritizing time wisely. By applying these strategies to future projects and personal pursuits, anyone can become an effective leader in their chosen field.
