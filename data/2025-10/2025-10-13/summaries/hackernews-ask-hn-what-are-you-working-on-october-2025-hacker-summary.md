# Ask HN: What are you working on? (October 2025) | Hacker News

**What is Laboratory.love?**

A one-man side project for independent, crowdfunded lab testing of specific products, aiming to detect endocrine disruptors in consumers' everyday items.

**Key Features:**

- Crowdfunding approach with rewards for reaching funding goals
- Using the same ISO 17025-accredited methodology as PlasticList.org but with smaller scale
- Three separate production lots are tested, detecting down to parts-per-billion
- Publishing results publicly

**Goals and Approach:**

- Facilitating transparency in supply chains
- Encouraging cleaner market outcomes based on consumer data

**Current Development:**

- 10 funded products, covering various categories (yogurt, snacks, etc.)
- 30 individual samples tested so far with 60 total chemical panels
- 6 results published and 4 in progress
