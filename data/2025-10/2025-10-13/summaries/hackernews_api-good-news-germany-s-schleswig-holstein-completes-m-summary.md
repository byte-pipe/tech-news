# Good News! Germany's Schleswig-Holstein Completes Massive Migration to Open Source Email Systems

The German state of Schleswig-Holstein has successfully migrated its entire state administration email system from Microsoft Exchange and Outlook to open source alternatives, Open-Xchange and Thunderbird. This move aims to achieve "digital sovereignty" by ditching Microsoft for more self-sustaining solutions.

## Key Points:
* Schleswig-Holstein completes massive migration of state administration email systems
* The move replaces 30,000 employees across various government departments
* Employers are over 40,000 mailboxes containing 100 million emails and calendar entries
* Open-Xchange and Thunderbird are the chosen email server solution and client alternatives
