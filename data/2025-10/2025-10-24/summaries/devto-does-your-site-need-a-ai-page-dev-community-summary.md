### Introducing an AI Transparency Page to Your Site

The authors of [oncassidoo's site](http://www.oncassidoo.com/) suggest adding an "AI Transparency" page to your website. This page aims to provide transparency about how you use AI on your site, including whether posts are generated with or without AI assistance.

**Why is it important?**

* Being transparent helps build trust with your audience
* It's not necessary to cover all the details of using AI, but rather be clear and authentic

### How to create a template page

Templates can help answer frequently asked questions (FAQs) or store reusable snippets for later use. The page will also function as a record in a public database of websites using AI.
