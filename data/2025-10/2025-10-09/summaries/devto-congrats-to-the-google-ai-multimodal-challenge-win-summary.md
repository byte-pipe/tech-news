# Congrats to the Google AI Multimodal Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**Google AI Multimodal Challenge Winners**

The Google AI Studio Multimodal Challenge was held with participants from around the world submitting their innovative applications. The winners demonstrate remarkable ingenuity in creating multimodal solutions that combine visual, audio, and text understanding.

**Congratulations to the Winners:**

1. **Sunbeam**: An AI-powered visual assistant for visitors worldwide.
2. **VisionGen**: A "Video-to-Video" generation tool for consistent narrative-driven videos.
3. **Reverse Engineering Reality**: A platform transforming curiosity into an interactive learning experience.

Each winner received a $1,000 USD prize, DEV++ Membership, exclusive DEV badge, and completion badge on their DEV profile.

**Thank you to Google AI:** The partnership with Google AI enables the community to push the boundaries of multimodal AI and creates exciting challenges for participants.
