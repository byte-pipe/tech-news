# Animated Vampire Lady - DEV Community

**Animated Vampire Lady - CSS Art Submission**

**Inspiration and Introduction**
I created this art piece to combine inspiration from realistic portraits, specifically those of ladies with self-portrait themes. The goal was to push the boundaries of what's typically expected in such representations.

**Demographics (Initial Goal)**
The overall aim was to create a self-portrait-style portrait of a vampire lady using CSS techniques.

**Key Points and Details**

* Initial plan included sketching up ideas before starting the project.
* Inspiration from a "lady that does realistic portraits with CSS" caught my attention.
* I initially sketched out ideas, including adding more details like blood drips and eyelid blinking.
* Adding a more deliberate positioning of her through castle windows, using an alternate idea about peaking through brick walls.
* Experimenting with making brick walls, spider webs, and evil eyes for added interest.
* Refining the look through tweaking paint properties for invisibility and hair effects.

**CSS Techniques Used**

* **position: absolute;**: Utilized to manage positioning of elements.
* `@keyframes`: Applied to create a blinking effect on her eyelids using keyframe animation.
* `grid` and `offsetting alternating rows with margin**: Employed to create the brick wall effect.
* Shading, shadows, and gradients used for facial expression details.

**Results**
The resulting design features a more dramatic look, incorporating various elements like blood drips, spiders, and a "peeping" effect in her eyes. The overall appearance has been refined through experimenting with CSS techniques to match the desired style.
