# Structured Procrastination

*Structured Procrastination*

* Definition:*
	+ The practice of intentionally putting off certain tasks until they become less pressing or convenient.
* Key Points:
 1. Procrastinators often put off important work due to not feeling motivated enough.
 2. Structured Procrastination involves making the most important tasks appear first on a list, and then working on them in a way that minimizes distractions.
 3. This habit allows individuals to avoid doing things they don't want to do directly when it's easiest or feels less daunting.
