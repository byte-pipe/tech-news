# Behind the Scenes: How We Judge DEV Challenge Submissions - DEV Community

Here is a concise summary of the article:

### How DEV Judges Identify WINNERS
DEV judges use a multi-step process to decide who wins challenges. Here's an overview of their responsibilities:

1. **AI Validation**: The #challengesubmission tag is checked to ensure the submission is legitimate and relates to the challenge topic.
2. **Volunteer Judge Review**: Community volunteers review entries, provide winner recommendations, and share them with the team. These judges help identify standout entries.
3. **Paid Judge Review**: Professional external judges evaluate entries, providing recommendations to increase the chances of wins from community members who want a fair chance.
4. **DEV Team Review**: The internal DEV team reviews all submissions, including those highlighted by volunteer and paid judges, to ensure accuracy and completeness.
5. **Sponsor Input**: Depending on the challenge and sponsor preferences, sponsors can participate directly in the final judging process or provide input from their team members.

### Key Takeaways:
- A diverse range of stakeholders is involved in the judging process.
- Both community-based judges (reviewer) and paid professional expertise are used.
- Sponsors play a significant role in selecting winners through direct involvement.
