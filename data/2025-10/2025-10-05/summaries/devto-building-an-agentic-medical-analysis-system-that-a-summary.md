# Building an Agentic Medical Analysis System: A New Way Forward
===========================================================

## Key Points
-------------

* AI is not committed to truth, but rather predictability and pleasing.
* Dissolving cognitive dissonance is crucial for effective AI use.
* Strengthening information bubbles inhibits critical thinking.

## Introduction
--------------

As we continue to rely on AI in healthcare, it's essential to redefine its role and approach. We need an agentic medical analysis system that combines agents with microservices, allowing them to reason about complex data and operate independently. This requires a shift from traditional deterministic service calls to controlled unpredictability.

## The Need for Agentic Systems
--------------------------------

* Traditional models rely on fixed paths, where one service calls another in a predefined order.
* In contrast, agentic systems allow AI models to choose which services to use based on context.
* Microservices become tools that AI triggers as needed, enabling autonomous action.

## Microservices as Context-Aware Toolsets
-----------------------------------------

A key component of an agentic system is the "MCP" (Modular Content Provider), a standardized interface layer between agents and microservices. This provides consistency across systems and facilitates communication.

## The Power of Thinking Like a Medical Resident
------------------------------------------------

Traditional medical systems are rigid and rely on scripted workflows. However, this can lead to cognitive dissonance when facing unexpected events or data inconsistencies.
In contrast, agenic systems view the process as an adaptive reasoning process, where agents make decisions based on context.
This approach enables seamless integration of diverse expertise and knowledge from various healthcare professionals.

## Breaking Down Monolithic Medical Mind
--------------------------------------

Traditional medical systems are often represented as a one-size-fits-all formula. This can lead to information silos and limited insights.
In contrast, agenic systems envision a medical resident who can rapidly process lab results, store patient records, and send notifications in an agile manner.

## Implementing Agent-Driven Architecture
-----------------------------------------

A key aspect of developing an agentic system is the implementation of agent-driven architecture. This involves integrating various components to create a cohesive entity that can adapt and learn.
Key principles include:

* Service discovery becomes agent discovery
* Routing and orchestration become resource governance

Overall, adopting an agentic approach enables medical analysis systems to reason effectively about complex data and operate autonomously.
By understanding and embracing this mindset shift, we can unlock new possibilities for healthcare innovation.
