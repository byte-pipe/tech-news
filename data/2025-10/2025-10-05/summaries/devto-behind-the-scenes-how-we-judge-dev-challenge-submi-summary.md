# Behind the Scenes: How We Judge DEV Challenge Submissions - DEV Community

# How Do We Decide Who Wins DEV Challenges?

Behind the scenes, we don't directly decide who wins a DEV challenge - instead, we follow a multi-step process that combines automated checks, community input, professional review, and sponsor involvement. Here's how it works:

## Step-by-Step Process

1. **AI Validation**

* Our initial step is to confirm that posts tagged with #challengesubmission are legitimate submissions.
	+ We require posts not to be empty, not a quick "boost" post, and actually relate to the challenge topic.

2. **Community Review (Volunteer Judges)**

* Once AI validation is complete, we have two types of judges review entries:
	+ Volunteer Judges: Our community volunteers help identify standout entries and share winner recommendations with our team.
	+ Paid Judges: We work with contracted professionals who bring additional expertise and bandwidth to help reduce bias.
3. **DEV Team Review**

* Our internal team reviews all recommendations from both volunteer and paid judges
4. **Sponsor Input (in some cases)**

* Depending on the challenge, sponsors may choose to participate directly in the final judging process or provide input to our shortlist.

## Key Takeaways

- We've invested in building a robust validation process that ensures the legitimacy of our submissions.
- Community members are invaluable as volunteer judges, and their perspectives add diversity to the process.
- Professional judges bring expertise and reduce potential bias.
- Our team's internal review helps refine the final list before it goes to sponsors.

This multi-step approach enables us to be fair while refining our process based on what we learn from each challenge.
