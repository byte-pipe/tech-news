# Offline Card Payments for Essential Goods Should Be Possible by April 2026 and then July 26, 2026

**Background**
Sweden has been shifting towards digital payments but still uses cash significantly. To enhance emergency preparedness, the Riksbank and payment market representatives have reached an agreement to make offline card payments feasible.

**Key Points**

* Agreement aims for offline card payments in place by April 25, 2026
* Measures include updating regulatory frameworks for financial agents
* Retailers will introduce technological solutions to enable offline purchases of essential goods like food and medicine
* The Riksbank is responsible for monitoring implementation

**Implementation Timeline**

* By: April 25, 2026
* After July 26, 2026 (for other payment methods)

## Implementation Details

* Payment market representatives will implement measures to increase offline card payments
* Financial agents will adapt their regulatory frameworks
* Retailers will introduce technological solutions for certain essential goods
