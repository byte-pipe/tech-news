## The Quantum Echoes Algorithm

### Overview

The Quantum Echoes algorithm is designed to solve specific problems by demonstrating the capability of quantum computers to run algorithms that cannot be solved using classical supercomputers. This breakthrough pushes the limits of what has been achieved so far in terms of verifiable quantum advantage.

### Achievements

* **Real-time computation**: The algorithm runs on a 53-qubit chip, outperforming current classical supercomputers by nearly 13,000 times.
* **Verifying results**: Quantum Echoes demonstrates the first-ever verifiable quantum advantage through a trusted verification process on a dedicated quantum emulator.
