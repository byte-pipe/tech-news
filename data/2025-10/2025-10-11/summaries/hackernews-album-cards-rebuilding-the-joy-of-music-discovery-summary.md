# Album Cards: Rebuilding the Joy of Music Discovery for My 10-Year-Old

## Album Cards: Rebuilding the Joy of Music Discovery for My 10-Year-Old

**Introduction**

As a music enthusiast, I had a unique experience as a child listening to CDs. I spent my money on them and went through every single album collection I owned before deciding what was left worth keeping. By age 10, all the physical media had been blown away in a matter of months.

### Music Discovery in the Digital Age

Fast forward to today, where music discovery has become more infinite than ever with streaming services. However, for many children like my 10-year-old self, this experience lacks the tangible elements I loved back then.

**Algorithmic Solution: Combining Old and New Ideas**

To create an engaging music discovery experience that combines nostalgia with a modern twist, I came up with the idea of creating "album cards" – physical trading card-style albums that can be collected and played using NFC tags.

### The Setup

To bring this concept to life, I:

* Utilized a combination of AI diffusion models to extend existing album artwork onto trading card aspect ratio
* Combined my familiarity with CD collecting with modern tech to dress up the cards (album art and NFC tags)

## Making the Cards

Using Adobe Canva, I designed an eye-catching cover template for the initial "pack" focused on Dad Rock. Once I had mastered that, I set out to build more packs tailored to specific genres or moods.
