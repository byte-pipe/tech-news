# Behind the Scenes: How We Judge DEV Challenge Submissions - DEV Community

# How We Decide Who Wins DEV Challenges

### Our Judging Process is a Combination of Multiple Steps

*   **Automated Checks**: Validate initial submissions to ensure legitimacy.
*   **Community Involvement**: Volunteer judges review submissions simultaneously alongside paid professionals. These external reviewers bring specialized knowledge and expertise, reducing potential biases within the community.

### What Happens Next

1.  **Final Selection by DEV Team**:
    *   Internal team reviews recommendations from volunteer and paid judges to select top entries.
2.  **Sponsor Input and Validation**:
    *   Sponsors may be involved in the final selection process or provide input on specific entries.

### How We Continuously Improve

*   Refine our judging system based on outcomes of challenges
*   Stay transparent about our processes while maintaining fairness
