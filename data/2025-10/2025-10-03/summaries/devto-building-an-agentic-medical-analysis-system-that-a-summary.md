# Building an Agentic Medical Analysis System That Actually Thinks - DEV Community

**Building an Agentic Medical Analysis System That Actually Thinks**

**Overview:**
This section discusses the importance of adopting agentic methods in modern systems, specifically focusing on how to create a medical analysis system that truly thinks and reasons like humans. It explores key concepts such as microservices, event-driven architecture, and critical thinking.

**Key Points:**

* **Emphasize Predictability Over Determinism**: AI should provide predictable results instead of acting predictably based on pre-determined rules.
* **Focus on Contextual Understanding**: Agentic methods prioritize understanding context over rigid rules and protocols.
* **Microservices Become Essential Tooling**: Microservices become the primary means of accessing tools, processing events, and making decisions.
* **Standardize Interfaces for Global Context-Adaptation**: A common interface layer is essential to adapt to different contexts and microservices.

**Real-World Implications:**

* **Challenges Traditional Medical Mindsets**: Current medical systems are often rigid and linear, while agentic methods facilitate a more nuanced understanding of the human body and behavior.
* **Increased Complexity in Medical Analysis**: An agnostic approach enables complex decision-making in real-time, overcoming challenges commonly found in traditional clinical decision support systems.

**In summary**, adopting an agenic approach can transform medical analysis into a thought-provoking, data-driven endeavor that rivals the creativity of human doctors.
