# Behind the Scenes: How We Judge DEV Challenge Submissions - DEV Community

### Winning The DEV Challenge Submissions Process
---------------------------

The **DEV Community** has a straightforward process for deciding who wins the challenges. This is done in an effort to ensure fairness while continuously refining it based on what they learn from each challenge.

#### Key Steps

1.  **AI Validation**: Automated checks confirm legitimate submissions that meet specific criteria (not empty posts, quickly posted content with no relation to the challenge topic).
2.  **Volunteer and Paid Judge Review**: Two types of judges review entries: volunteer members who are community volunteers, providing diverse perspectives, while paid professionals evaluate entries for additional expertise and bandwidth.
3.  **DEV Team Review**: Community-submitted entries that pass through volunteering/paid judging rounds are then screened by the internal team to ensure they aren't duplicated or have already been assessed.

#### Key Details

*   Volunteer Judges: Participating in a challenge does not mean a volunteer judge is allowed to vote on their own submission.
*   Paid Judges: Worked with contracted professionals who provide expertise from both within and outside the community.
*   DEV Team Review: Conducts a thorough review of all recommendations, including additional submissions that may have been overlooked.

#### Key Takeaways

1.  A combination of automated checks, community input, professional review, and sponsor involvement helps ensure fairness while allowing for continuous refinement and improvement.
2.  Both volunteer and paid judges contribute to the judging process simultaneously, adding depth and expertise.
3.  The DEV team continually screen submissions to prevent duplicates or submissions that have already been assessed.

#### Additional Information

*   A thorough review by the DEV Team helps identify standout entries and provides valuable insights for future challenges.
