# Timelinize - Personal Archival Suite

## Overview
Timelinize is an open source personal archival suite designed to organize family history on user's computer, providing a unified timeline with photos, videos, texts, social media, and more. This summary highlights its main features and benefits.

### Key Features
- Provides data in a unified single timeline on the user's own computer
- Unifies all data into one place, eliminating duplicates and reducing clutter
- Allows for viewing and exploring family history through linear or map view
- Offers advanced exploration with multiple projections (e.g., Google Maps)

### Benefits

* Preserves personal story and details over standard photo libraries and journaling apps
* Provides a more comprehensive view of family life than available services alone
* Protects data by keeping it locally on user's computer
* Allows access to multiple sources in one place, such as emails and texts
- Integrates with popular platforms like Apple Photos, Google Takeout, and Facebook

### Features Overview
Timelinize organizes your:

* Photos and videos in a single library
* Text messages and chat logs
* Social media accounts and updates
* Workouts and fitness tracking data
* Notes and bookmarks from various sources

And preserves details such as events, location records, weather, news, planned activities, workouts, and calendars.
