## FSF Announces Librephone Project

### The Free Software Foundation (FSF) Unveils Mobile Phone Freedom Initiative
#### The FSF's New Effort Brings Full Freedom to the Android Environment

The **Free Software Foundation** has announced its long-awaited initiative, titled "Librephone," aimed at bringing mobile phone freedom to users worldwide. Since 1984, the organization has been advocating for computing freedoms, pushing forward significant reforms over forty years.

**Key Points:**

*   The FSF aims to rectify obstacles hindering full mobility in the Android ecosystem
*   Librephone is a new project focused on overcoming technical hurdles toward a free and open mobile phone operating system
*   Developed by Rob Savoye, who will lead the technical endeavor, amidst initial funding provided by board member John Gilmore
