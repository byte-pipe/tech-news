# AI in Coding: The Hype That Delivered an Expensive Autocomplete – Why Devs Are Still Essential in 2025 - DEV Community

## The Limits of AI in Coding

### Summary

The article critiques the hype surrounding Artificial Intelligence (AI) in coding and its impact on human developers. Despite initial promises of a utopia where machines could build entire applications faster and more efficiently, reality has failed to meet expectations.

Key points:

*   The idea that AI would replace single developers is unfounded.
*   AI "autocompletion" has become an expensive and unreliable tool.
*   Real productivity gains are still attributed to human effort and nuance.
*   Companies are struggling to justify the cost of hiring back AI-developed talent after automation kicks in.

### Importance of Human Touch

The author emphasizes that coding is not just about writing code, but also involves expertise, creativity, and problem-solving. While AI can augment tasks, it cannot fully replace human developers without careful planning and management.


## Conclusion

In conclusion, the article highlights the limitations of relying on AI alone for coding tasks. While technology has enabled faster development and more efficient productivity, it is essential to remember that human touch and expertise are still crucial in creating high-quality software solutions.
