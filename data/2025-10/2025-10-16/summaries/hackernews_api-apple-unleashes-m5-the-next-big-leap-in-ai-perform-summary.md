# Apple Unleashes M5: Next Big Leap in AI Performance

Apple today announced M5, the next-generation system on a chip built specifically for Artificial Intelligence (AI) computing. This major upgrade promises to deliver significant performance improvements across various aspects of the chip.

## Key Highlights

- **GPU Architecture:** M5 boasts an advanced 10-core GPU architecture featuring a dedicated Neural Accelerator in each core. The Neural Accelerators enable exceptional performance for AI workloads, such as running diffusion models and large language models.
- **Performance Comparison:** Compared to its predecessor, M4, M5 delivers over 4x peak GPU compute performance for AI tasks and up to 6x for overall AI performance.
- **Graphics Capabilities:** M5 includes first-generation ray tracing capabilities that deliver a graphics performance boost of up to 45% compared to M4.
- **Computational Power:** The world's fastest performance core allows for significant speedup in various computational tasks, including multithreaded processing and media processing.

## Product Lineup

All devices launched under the Apple ecosystem are available for pre-order today. This includes:

- The 14-inch MacBook Pro
- The iPad Pro (with and without ProMotion displays)
- The Apple Vision Pro

Apple's decision to introduce M5 in its lineup is aimed at providing a robust AI-focused platform, expected to power high-performance workloads within the devices.
