# Claude for Excel

## Introduction
Claude is an Excel add-in for enterprise-level team collaboration, designed to help teams with complex financial models and assumptions. Initially released as a research preview, it offers explanations with cell-level citations, support for updating assumptions across the entire model, and transparency features for real-time updates.

### How Teams Use Claude in Financial Modeling
Claude proves itself useful by quickly analyzing any cell in seconds, helping to test scenarios on existing templates or draft models during testing. Upward adaptation capabilities allow teams to fix errors with minimal disruptions while maintaining formula integrity. Models can also be built from scratch based on requirements or populated using existing data.

## Benefits of Using Claude

### 1. Enhanced Accuracy via Real-Time Explanation
Claude is capable of explaining every change, including debugging errors and circular references.

### 2. Build and Test Complex Financial Models Efficiently
Teams can utilize the platform's capabilities for creating financial models from scratch or modifying existing ones to suit their specific needs.

### 4. Improved Team Collaboration and Transparency

**Overview**
Claude offers transparent data sharing by displaying changes in real-time with detailed explanations.



## Explanation of Key Features
- **Transparency:** Claude enables visible updates on model complexities, maintaining formulas.
- **Debugging Capabilities:** Identifying errors becomes faster due to immediate feedback.
- **Formula Integrity:** Structure and formatting are preserved during model updates.
- **Security:** Compliant within existing security frameworks.



### Limited Availability
Claude remains available as a research preview for the initial 1,000 Max plan customers. The platform will be gradually expanded upon further research and customer support.
