# Does Your Site Need a /ai Page? - DEV Community

**Adding an "AI Transparency" Page to Your Site**

Consider adding a page to your site that details how you use generative AI tools. Here's why:

*   It's not about covering up your base with legalese; it's about being authentic, clear, and transparent.
*   Recognize the benefits of AI: it can be a helpful tool for writing, but also understand its limitations and dangers.

**How to Create an "AI Transparency" Page**

To create this page, you'll need some basic HTML and Markdown skills. Here are a few options:

1.  **Use a template**: You can find many free templates online that make it easy to add custom fields and metadata.
2.  **Create from scratch**: Use Markdown or HTML to build your own page, including headers, paragraphs, and links.
3.  **Modify existing pages**: Visit templates like WordPress or Wix to see if there's a pre-made template that suits your needs.

**Adding Key Information**

When building this page, consider the following key points:

*   Specify whether AI is used on-site
*   Provide examples of how AI was utilized (e.g., generating text)
*   Discuss potential benefits and limitations of AI usage (pros, cons, strengths, and dangers)
*   Include links to reputable sources for more information

**Best Practices**

To maintain transparency and trust, follow these best practices:

*   Use clear and concise headings and paragraphs
*   Provide context for unfamiliar terms or technologies
*   Avoid using overly technical language or jargon
*   Clearly indicate where the content comes from (e.g., a third-party source)
