# Building Disco Week 2: Making Event Networking Less Random 🪩 - DEV Community

## Building Disco: A Discovolution of Event Networking

### Key Points

* The author started building a networking app called Discomowalking with two friends
* Initially received great feedback from events, including Startup Open House and DMZ incubators
* Found that the success of the app depends on finding the right team members, setting clear vision, and staying disciplined
* Project aims to solve real-world problem of in-person event networking for introverts

### About Disco

Disco is an app designed to connect people at in-person events based on their shared goals.

## Building Discomowith a Small Team

The author started building the app with two friends over a period of two weeks, sharing ideas through social media and demoing it at various events.

## Refining Ideas with Feedback

As discussions with potential users took place, the author was able to refine what matters most for the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and next steps. Early validation from partnerships such as York University helped shape the app's direction.

### What Disco Aims To Solve

The app aims to solve a common problem in networking events: finding meaningful connections amidst crowded spaces. Prior to Discom, users often found it challenging to connect with others who shared similar goals at events. The app seeks to change this by connecting users based on their interests and objectives.
