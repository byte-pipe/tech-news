# AWS Cloud Club Captain Life Hacks: How I Survived Final Year Without Losing My Mind (Much) - DEV Community

**AWS Cloud Club Captain Life Hacks: Expert Tips for Survival**

Being an AWS Cloud Club Captain can be overwhelming, balancing multiple responsibilities and dealing with unexpected challenges. Here's how I, **iamlabeeq**, managed to survive the final year without losing my mind - most of the time.

### 1. Divide and Conquer (Literally)

*   **Team Structure**: Divided the club into teams for different aspects: technical, design, social media, events, documentation, and logistics.
*   **Roles and Responsibilities**: Each team had its own mini-ecosystem, with designated tasks and leaders.

### 2. Template Everything

*   **Reusable Templates**: Saved time by having design templates and pitch decks stored in a central folder.
*   **Quick Solution**: Reused existing PowerPoint presentations for various events and needs.

### 3. Backup Plans Are Your First Plans

*   **Preparation is Key**: Always had backup plans in place, including mini sessions or workshops before an event.
*   **Stay Flexible**: Adjusted to unexpected changes and cancellations of speakers.

### 4. Time Management ≠ Magic

*   **Effective Calendar Management**: Utilized Google Calendar for approvals from college administration.
*   **Guilt Reminders**: Set reminders for time management and cloud event approval (from a humorous perspective).

### 5. Motivation Is Complicated

*   **Positive Reinforcement**: Used encouragement to motivate teams, especially when tasks were challenging.
*   **Recognition and Appreciation**: Acknowledged team achievements through milestones or accomplishments.

### 6. Genius Hacks

*   **Pre-Approved Designs**: Saved hours by having a folder of reusable templates and designs.
*   **Panic Page**: Combined event information with backup pages, resources, and contact details in one location.

### 7. Unfiltered Advice for Future Captains

*   **Caffeine is a Tool**
*   **Lead With Empathy**
*   **Laugh at Mistakes**
*   **Never Forget Passion**

**Final Thoughts:**

Being an AWS Cloud Club Captain requires dedication, strategy, and adaptability. While it may seem overwhelming to balance multiple responsibilities, with these expert tips, you can survive (even thrive) the final year without losing your mind.
