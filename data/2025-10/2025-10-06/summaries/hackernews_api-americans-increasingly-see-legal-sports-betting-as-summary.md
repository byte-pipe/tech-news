**Growing Shares View Sports Betting as Negative Impacts on Society**

* Public awareness of legal sports betting has grown rapidly in recent years
* Many Americans believe that it's a bad thing for society and sports
* A Pew Research Center survey found that 43% of U.S. adults say it's an abad thing for society, up from 34% in 2022
* Additionally, 40% of adults believe it's an abad thing for sports, up from 33% (July 2025) and more than double the number who think so in 2022
