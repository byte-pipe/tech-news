# What We Talk About When We Talk About Sideloading | F-Droid - Free and Open Source Android App Repository

**Counter-messaging on Google's Sideloading Policy**
===========================================================

### Key Points:

* Google's "sideloading is not going away" statement means there will be no shift from sideloading in Android.
* The "developer verification decree" effectively forces individuals to use the Google Play Store for software installation.
* Installing software installed through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store involves signing into the device with a non-consensual payment, rendering original freedom of choice obsolete.

### Counterpoints:

* The term "sideloading" means installing apps from non-vendor approved sources.
 * Wikipedia defines it as "the transfer of apps from web sources that are not vendor-approved" and Google will indeed approve this path for their certified devices.
* Consumers were misled believing Android offered an open computing platform; instead, users are now being pressured into using the Google Play Store for software installation.
