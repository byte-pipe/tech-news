# AI Transparency on Website

## Introduction to the Concept of AI Transparency

Adding an "AI" page to a website, such as Oceanassidoo's site, is a great idea that promotes authenticity and transparency in our work. The goal is not to conceal or hide how AI is used, but rather to provide clarity and context for users.

## Importance of AI Transparency

Transparency about the use of AI helps build trust between consumers and creators. It enables users to understand how AI is applied in a work, its pros and cons, and potential strengths and weaknesses.

## How to Implement an "AI" Page on Your Site

To add an `AI` page to your site:

*   Provide details on your use of AI
*   Discuss the benefits and limitations of using AI
*   Address concerns and questions users may have
*   Consider creating a template for frequent FAQs or storing snippets in a database
*   Use a clear, concise, and professional tone

#### Tips for Implementing "AI" Pages on Websites:

*   Keep it simple and avoid legalese language
*   Focus on providing clear and useful information to users
*   Be authentic and transparent about the use of AI
*   Consider using templates or storing snippets in a database for future reference
