# I Spent 3 Days Making App Store Screenshots (So I Built a Tool That Does It in 15 Minutes) - DEV Community

# Why I Built Lemmi Studio

## Because App Store Screenshots Are the Worst Part of Launching

- **TL;DR:** I spent 3 days designing screenshots for my last app launch, which was taking way too long. So I built a tool called Lemmi Studio to speed up the process.

## The Problem Every mobile Developer Faces
You've just spent months building your app and are ready to ship. But then you hit the wall:marketing assets. It's frustrating because there aren't any good design tools or writing resources for marketing copy that fit Apple App Store policies.

### What You Actually Need

- 10 polished App Store screenshots (1200x2600px, device-framed)
- App Store copy (title, subtitle, description, keywords)
- A landing page
- Everything optimized for ASO

## The Problem? You Can't Design
Or write marketing copy. Or build marketing sites.

### Your "Options"

1. Hire a designer: $500-1000 + wait 1-2 weeks
2. Learn Figma yourself: 20+ hours of tutorials
3. Use Canva: still 4-6 hours per app
4. Ship with ugly screenshots

## I Know Because...
I've launched a few apps and every launch was frustrating because of these bottlenecks.

## The Solution I Wish Existed

After my last launch, where I spent 3 days in Figma, I sketched out what I would build:

- A tool that takes raw iPhone screenshots
- Generates professional device-framed marketing images
- Writes all the App Store copy (optimized for ASO)
- Builds a landing page
- Does it all in under 15 minutes
- Costs less than one designer hour

## That Tool Didn't Exist
So I built my own: Lemmi Studio.

## Meet Lemmi Studio

### What It Does

- AI-generated App Store copy, optimized for 30-character limits
- Professional marketing screenshots with device frames and gradients
- User-controlled typography overlays
- A visual canvas editor for multi-screenshot editing

#### How It Works (The 15-Minute Process)

1. Upload your original images
2. Preview and customize everything (colors, fonts)
3. Export in desired languages
