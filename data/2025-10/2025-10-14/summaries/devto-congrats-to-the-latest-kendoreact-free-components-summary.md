# Congrats to the latest KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

## KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners Announced

The 3 winners of the recent KendoReact Free Components Challenge have been announced. These winners demonstrated creativity and variety in their use cases, showcasing the modern UI components offered by KendoReact, a React-based library with a growing user base.

*   **EduBox: An Intelligent Student Hub**
    *   Developed by @aryprogrammercreated, EduBox tackles the universal pain point of managing academic life across multiple disconnected tools. It incorporates file management with semantic search, an AI-powered planner, campus life integration, and an AI assistant for quick answer sessions on materials.
*   **Bot or Not: Can You Spot the AI?**
    *   Built by @kaoutar_benhadine_9c91300, Bot or Not features a Daily Challenge mode with three rounds, providing players with progressive difficulty from easy to challenging. This game also features a complete admin panel for content management and features Bot or Not's Daily Challenge mode.
*   **Consultify: Your Doctor Speaks Every Language**
    *   Constructed by @iamceejaytackled a critical healthcare challenge – language barriers in Africa, where 1,500 to 3,000 languages create obstacles between patients and providers. Consilyfy tackles this problem through context-aware RAG and multilingual communication.

Each of these projects showcases the capabilities and potential of KendoReact's modern UI components, demonstrating their effectiveness as an essential tool for developers working in various fields.
