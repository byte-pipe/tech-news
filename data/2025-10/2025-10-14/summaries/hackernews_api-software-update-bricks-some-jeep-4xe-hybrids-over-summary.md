# Software update bricks some Jeep 4xe hybrids over the weekend - Ars Technica

** Jeep Wrangler 4xe Hybrids Stranded After Software Update**

Owners of some Jeep Wrangler 4xe hybrids have been affected after installing an over-the-air software update this weekend. The automaker pushed out a telematics update for the Uconnect infotainment system, but allegedly didn't meet industry standards.

**Key Issues:**

- Some owners lost power while driving and became stranded.
- Others experienced powertrain failures at highway speeds.

**Corrective Action by Jeep:**

* Owners are advised to ignore the update pop-up if they haven't installed it yet. For those who have, a fix has been pushed out.

**Lesson Learned by Stellantis:**

* Jeep learned that pushing software updates during critical times (e.g., Friday afternoons) is not effective and can cause problems like this.
This experience highlights the importance of thorough testing before releasing updates to prevent such issues.
