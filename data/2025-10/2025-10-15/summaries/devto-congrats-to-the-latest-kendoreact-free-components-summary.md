# Congrats to the latest KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

Here's a concise and informative summary of the KendoReact Free Components Challenge winners:

* **Overall Winner**: Arya Pratap Singh created EduBox, an AI-powered student hub that addresses academic life chaos.
* **Code Smarter, Not Harder Winner**: Kaoutar Benhadine built Bot or Not, a game featuring Daily Challenge and Streak Modes with progressive difficulty.
* **RAGs to Riches Winner**: Chijioke Osadebe developed Consultify, a context-aware telemedicine solution that revolutionizes language barriers in Africa.

Additionally, the winners will each receive $1,000 USD, an exclusive DEV Badge, and a DEV++ Membership. The challenge is supported by Progressfor with NucliaRAG platform as the sponsor.
