# Congrats to the Heroku "Back to School" AI Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

### Heroku "Back to School" AI Challenge Winners Announced

Congrats to all participants who took on the Heroku "Back to School" AI Challenge! The winners were chosen based on their thought-provoking applications of Heroku's AI features, which aimed to make the back-to-school transition smoother, smarter, and more successful.

* **Crazy Creative Winner**: @prime_299792 designed a comprehensive AI-powered study management platform with MentorMind, an intelligent assistant that helps with multi-agent architecture.
* **Educator Empowerment Winner**: @victor_lakra_e1910abe17fcbuilt a practical solution to address teacher planning challenges, enabling teachers to upload materials and receive lesson plans in minutes.
* **Student Success Winner**: @juliodiaz0209 created a powerful multi-agent learning platform that demonstrates the future of AI-powered education with StudyFlow AI.

### What Demanded Attention

Each winner won:

* $1,000 USD
* DEV++ Membership
* Exclusive DEV badge

All participants received a completion badge and rose to the challenge!

Heroku congratulates the winners for their dedication and technical sophistication in building these innovative applications.
