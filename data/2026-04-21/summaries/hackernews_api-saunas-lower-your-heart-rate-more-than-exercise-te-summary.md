**Saunas Can Lower Heart Rate More Than Exercise**

### Saunas Versus Regular Exercise: Same-Day Effects

Saunas have been used for centuries to stimulate cardiovascular system, promote sweating, and deliver toxins. Recent studies suggest that consistent sauna use can have additional benefits beyond exercise recovery. In a study of 59,000 daily records from 256 users, researchers found that:

* Sauna days led to:
	+ Higher activity levels
	+ Greater minimum heart rate compared to non-sauna days
	+ Lower night-time heart rate (by an average of 5% across all participants)

The results were statistically robust and suggest that sauna use may have a direct impact on same-day recovery.

### Saunas vs. Women's Health: Activity Increase and Heart Rate

**Research Findings**

* Females showed larger increases in exercise time compared to males
* However, females experienced smaller drops in minimum heart rate when using the sauna compared to non-users.
