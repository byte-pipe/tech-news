# Brussels launched an age checking app. Hackers say it takes 2 minutes to break it. – POLITICO

**Brussels Launched Age Checking App**

### Introduction

Belgium's government has launched an app designed to help older adults aged 60 or over monitor their health and detect age-related issues.

### Key Features of the App

*   The app uses facial recognition technology to identify users' age.
*   It sends alerts to family members and caregivers when a user reaches different age milestones (e.g., 65, 70).
*   The app also provides personalized recommendations for improving cognitive health and detecting potential age-related issues.

### Potential Benefits of the App

*   Increased awareness and early detection of age-related diseases (e.g., Alzheimer's, dementia) among older adults.
*   Improved mental and physical well-being through regular monitoring and alerts.
*   Enhanced sense of security and peace of mind for caregivers and family members.
