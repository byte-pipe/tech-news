# American Express Buys Hypercard Network

American Express is acquiring Hypercard Network, a startup based in New York, to improve expense management for businesses. Here are the key points:

## Key Points

* American Express will acquire Hypercard Network
* The acquisition aims to automate business processes and simplify operations through AI-powered solutions
* Hypercard Team Expands with American Express

### Acquisition Details

* American Express plans to close the deal in the current quarter
* Hypercard Network will be transformed into a product launch later this year, focusing on implementing AI agents across its services
* The new partnership allows for integration of American Express's expertise in building agentic tools and solutions via AI-powered processes.

### Insights

* Hypercard Network was founded by Marc Baghadjian and Nikolas Ioannou in 2022 as a means to reward employees with credit cards
* The founders had initially raised $4.5 million from Sam Altman, the billionaire CEO of OpenAI, at that time.
* American Express Chairman Stephen Squeri emphasized his company's commitment to helping businesses automate processes and simplify operations.
