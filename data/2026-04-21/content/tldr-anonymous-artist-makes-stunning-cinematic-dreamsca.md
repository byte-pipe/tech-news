# Anonymous Artist Makes Stunning Cinematic Dreamscapes Created To Escape The Stress Of Daily Life

Bunch of Fantasies is an anonymous digital creator who uses generative AI to craft cinematic, atmospheric dreamscapes. Describing himself simply as a “random boy” looking to escape a stressful job, he has built an engaged audience of over 47,000 followers on Instagram by sharing his tranquil and poetic visual escapes.

The portfolio is defined by its deep sense of calm, focusing on themes of tranquility, peaceful loneliness, and romantic nature. Using tools like Midjourney and AI video generators like Veo 3, the artist creates hyper-realistic and stylized landscapes featuring glowing horizons, massive clouds, starlit skies, and solitary figures. He frequently pairs his melancholic, cinematic imagery with highly poetic captions, reflecting on human emotions, passion, and the transient beauty of life.
