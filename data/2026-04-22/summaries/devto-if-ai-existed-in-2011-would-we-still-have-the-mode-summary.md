# If AI Existed in 2011, Would We Still Have the Modern Web? - DEV Community

**Summary of Key Points**

* In 2011, the web was primarily server-rendered PHP templates, with limited interactivity and client-side app development existing.
* Suddenly dropping modern AI technologies (LLMs) could have led to significant acceleration in web development trends.
* However, instead of accelerating progress, AI might stabilise it due to its ability to replicate complex tasks and provide pre-written features from prompts.
* This would mean that the pace of innovation in the web may not increase significantly with the advent of AI.

**Summary Structure**

## The Current State of the Web

* 2011: Server-rendered PHP templates, limited client-side app development
* Suddenly stopping modern AI: LLMs, coding agents, tools for generating features from prompts

## Stabilization vs. Acceleration

* AI could potentially stabilize the web by replicating complex tasks and providing pre-written features.
* However, this might not lead to significant innovation in software development.

## Consequences of AI Impact on Web Development

* AI might reinforce existing technologies instead of driving progress forward
* A world where AI stabilises technology may be less exciting than increasing innovation
