**Earth Recovery Protocol: An Interactive Simulation**

This project aims to help users understand and cope with the irreversible consequences of environmental destruction through an immersive, interactive simulation. Through a terminal interface with cinematic animations and typewriter effects, it reveals the harsh reality of ecological limits.

**Features and Goals**

* Immersive experience
* Educational impact (comparable to "undoing" permanent scars on the planet)
* Encourages users to feel their surroundings

**Development**

The project uses a terminal-style interface with glitch animations, cinematic transitions, and typewriter effects. Each "recovery attempt" reveals scientific truths about irreversibility while challenging users' perceptions.

**Impact**

The project aims to inspire critical thinking and action on environmental issues, rather than providing an easy solution. It encourages users to confront the reality of ecological limits and take responsibility for their planet's future.

Overall, this project is a thought-provoking response to the growing urgency of climate change and environmental degradation.
