# Steerling-8B: The First Inherently Interpretable Language Model

- Steerling-8B is presented as the first language model with inherent interpretability, capable of tracing generated tokens back to their input context, human-understandable concepts, and training data.
- The model was trained on a substantial dataset of 1.35 trillion tokens, achieving downstream performance comparable to models trained on 2 to 7 times more data.
- Key capabilities unlocked by Steerling-8B include:
    - Suppressing or amplifying specific concepts during inference without requiring retraining.
    - Providing training data provenance for any generated text segment.
    - Enabling inference-time alignment through concept control, offering an alternative to extensive safety training examples with explicit concept steering.
