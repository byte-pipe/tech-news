# An Update on Heroku

## An Update on Heroku

### Status: Transitioning to Sustaining Engineering Model
* Last Updated: February 06, 2026

Heroku is shifting towards a sustaining engineering model focused on stability, security, reliability, and support.

### What This Means for Customers
Customers already using Heroku can continue with their current plans without any changes.

#### Key Points:
- No pricing or billing changes are necessary for customers using credit card payment methods.
- Core platform functionality remains unaffected.
- Existing contracts may renew, but Enterprise Account contracts will not be offered to new customers.

### Plans:
- Existing Enterprise subscriptions and support contracts will continue to honor as usual.

## Further Details
- Focus on providing enterprise-grade AI applications in a secure environment with trusted features.
