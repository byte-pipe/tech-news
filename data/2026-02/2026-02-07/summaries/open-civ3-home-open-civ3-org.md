# OpenCiv3 Home | OpenCiv3.org

## What is OpenCiv3?

OpenCiv3 is an open-source project inspired by Civilization III, aiming to rebuild the game based on fan feedback and best practices. It utilizes the Godot Engine, C#, and is designed for mod development.

## Vision
The vision of OpenCiv3 is to create a more modernized, simplified, and expanded version of Civilization III that retains its gameplay elements while addressing limitations and broken features in its original game.

## Status
OpenCiv3 is under active development and currently in an early pre-alpha state. The game is playable but lacks many mechanics, and errors are still present.

## Contributing
New players can connect with the community through Discord, a subforum on CivFanatics, and GitHub.

## Get Started

1. **Download and Install**: Download the app for your OS (Windows, Linux, or Mac) from the Dutch Preview 1 release.
2. **System Requirements**: Ensure you have a compatible system and necessary software requirements set.
