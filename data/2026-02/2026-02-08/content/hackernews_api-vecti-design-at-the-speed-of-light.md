Do I need a credit card to use Vecti?



No, you can get started with Vecti for free and use Vecti for as long as you want
 without needing a card on file. You're only asked to provide a card when you want to
 upgrade your plan.



How long does a plan last?



You can choose between a monthly or annual billing cycle.Annual pricing gives you the ability to pay for a plan upfront at a discounted rate.
 Paying for a plan upfront can save up to 20% compared to paying monthly.



Can I cancel my plan at anytime?



Your plan automatically renews at the end of each billing cycle, but you can stop it
 from renewing by cancelling at any time. You will still pay the remainder of the
 term for the professional plan.



Will I lose my work if I downgrade?



You will not lose any of your work if you downgrade. The projects and workspaces
 will still be available, yet with some limitations. You will not be able to create
 more projects (if you have more than 5 already). You will also lose the
 professional features.



Do you have a discount for students and teachers?



Yes, we provide free plans for students and teachers! Please contact us.



Do you have a discount for non-profit organisations?



We do not have any discounts for non-profit organisations. Anyone is welcome to use
 Vecti's free plan.
