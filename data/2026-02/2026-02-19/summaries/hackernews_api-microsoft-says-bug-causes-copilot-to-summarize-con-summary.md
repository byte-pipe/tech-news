# Microsoft Says Bug Causes Copilot to Summarize Confidential Emails

## Summary
- A bug in Microsoft 365 Copilot is causing the AI assistant to incorrectly summarize confidential emails since late January.
- The bug affects the Copilot "work tab" chat feature, which improperly reads and summarizes emails in users' Sent Items and Drafts folders, even those with confidentiality labels and Data Loss Prevention (DLP) policies in place.
- Microsoft confirmed that an unspecified code error is responsible and began rolling out a fix in early February.
- The company is currently monitoring the fix's deployment and reaching out to affected users.
- The full scope of users and organizations impacted is unknown, and Microsoft has not provided a final timeline for remediation.
- This incident is currently categorized as an advisory, indicating a limited scope or impact.

## Key Points
- The bug bypasses data loss prevention policies designed to protect sensitive information.
- Copilot Chat was rolled out to paying Microsoft 365 business customers in September 2025.
- The issue allows Copilot to access and summarize emails with confidentiality labels.
- Microsoft is actively working on a fix and is verifying its effectiveness.
- The impact of the bug may change as the investigation continues.
