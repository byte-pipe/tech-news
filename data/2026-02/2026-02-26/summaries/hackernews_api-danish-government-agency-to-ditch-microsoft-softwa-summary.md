* This move is seen as a strategic shift towards greater control over its digital destiny and aligns with broader European trends towards open-source technologies.
* The transition will involve significant investment in new skills and infrastructure.
* The agency acknowledges the challenges involved but believes the long-term benefits of increased independence outweigh the short-term difficulties.
* This initiative is notable as a significant step for a major government agency in a highly Microsoft-dominated technology landscape.
