# Amazon BUSTED for Widespread Scheme to Inflate Prices Across the Economy

## Summary of California Attorney General Rob Bonta's Allegations

* California Attorney General Rob Bonta has filed a lawsuit against Amazon, alleging a widespread price-fixing scheme involving online retailers across the economy.
* Bonta claims Amazon instructs vendors to raise prices to maintain its profitability, leveraging its position as the largest online retailer.
* This scheme involves vendors complying with Amazon's demands to increase prices on competitors' websites or remove products from those sites, often with the knowledge and cooperation of the competing retailers.
* Bonta is seeking an immediate court injunction to halt this alleged scheme, believing his evidence strongly indicates Amazon will be found liable for harming consumers.
* The scale of the scheme is significant, with Amazon's North America online shopping revenue in 2025 being approximately $3000 per household in America. Prices for third-party goods on Amazon have increased at a rate more than twice the rate of inflation.
* Amazon utilizes its Prime program, with 200 million members, to control pricing through the "Buy Box," which favors Prime-eligible sellers often requiring the use of Amazon's Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) services.
* Amazon allegedly prevents sellers from offering lower prices on other platforms by restricting their access to the Buy Box.
* This "hub-and-spoke" conspiracy, or vertical price-fixing, involves Amazon coordinating with competitors to inflate prices, distinct from direct collaboration among rivals.
* Bonta's lawsuit requests that Amazon be barred from entering agreements with vendors to set prices for non-Amazon retailers and from communicating with vendors about pricing terms.
* The request for an immediate injunction indicates Bonta believes the harm to consumers is significant and that Amazon is likely to lose the case. This could incentivize Amazon to expedite the trial process.
* Bonta's complaint also suggests Amazon has internal processes to conceal its involvement in this alleged price-fixing scheme.

## How the Price-Fixing Scheme Works

* Amazon uses its Buy Box algorithm to ensure sellers cannot offer lower prices on other platforms and still access Amazon's customer base.
* Sellers who attempt to sell at a lower price on other sites are cut off from the Buy Box.
* Amazon employs several methods to inflate prices:
    * Directly telling vendors selling to competitors to increase prices during price wars.
    * Asking competitors to stop discounting items through vendors.
    * Raising its own prices when vendors stop selling at lower prices on other platforms.

## Legal Context and Future Outlook

* This lawsuit follows similar actions by the FTC, which filed a complaint against Amazon in 2023 alleging a scheme called "Project Nessie" to encourage price increases on other online retailers.
* While these cases have passed legal hurdles to go to trial, a remedy is expected to be years away.
* Bonta's request for an immediate injunction is a significant move, indicating strong confidence in his evidence and aiming to prevent further harm to consumers while the trial is ongoing.
