FEATURES

## Everything you love aboutshort-video— without the lock-in

Loops gives you modern creator tools, real community, and the freedom of the fediverse — not another walled garden.

FEEDS

Following & For You, tuned by the fediverse

A calm chronological Following feed for the people you trust — and a For You feed that surfaces new creators from across Loops and compatible ActivityPub servers. No dark-pattern growth hacks, just signals from real activity.

* • Vertical swipe short-video feed, built for thumb-first browsing
* • For You powered by engagement, hashtags, and social graph — not ads
* • Browse feeds with a web browser or our official mobile app
* • Works across Loops instances and other fediverse apps that support video
 Camera

Creator-first camera for short video

Capture vertical loops with a minimal camera built for creators, not ads. Switch lenses, add captions, and post in seconds.

 Conversations

Comments that stay in the thread

Rich comment threads with replies, likes on replies, and @mentions keep conversations anchored to the video — not lost in your inbox.

 Engagement

Likes, shares & boosts that travel

Likes, shares, reposts, and comment likes all feed back into discovery — on your server and across the fediverse where it makes sense.

 Discovery

Hashtags & search that find your people

Tag loops with hashtags, jump into topics, and search profiles or videos to find your corner of the fediverse.

 Notifications

Notifications that respect your time

Control what pings you — likes, new followers, mentions, or none at all. Turn down the noise without missing what matters. Push notification support coming soon!

 Federation

Built for Pixelfed & the fediverse

Loops speaks ActivityPub, so your videos can reach people on Mastodon, Pixelfed and other compatible apps — while you stay in control of your home server.

OUR MISSION

## Reclaiming short-video sharing forhumanity

"Loops was founded on a simple but powerful idea: social media should serve people, not exploit them.

We're building an ethical alternative to commercial short-video sharing platforms — a space where creators, people and visual artists can share their work without invasive tracking or data mining.

As an open-source project, we believe in radical transparency and true community governance. Every feature we build is designed with user privacy and experience in mind, not advertising revenue or engagement metrics.
