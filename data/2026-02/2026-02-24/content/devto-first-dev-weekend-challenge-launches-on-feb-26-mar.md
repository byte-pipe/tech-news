We're launching something brand new: our first-everDEV Weekend Challenge! 🎉

This is a short challenge format that mirrors a more traditional hackathon, designed to fit into your weekend. We're giving you a heads up now so you can mark your calendars!

## How It Works

Our challenge prompt will be revealed at launch. You can keep an eye on theDEV Weekend Challengepage or look out for a post fromDEV Team. From there, you'll have the weekend to build and submit. That's it!

Because our community spans every timezone on the planet, we've set the window so thateveryone around the world gets at least a full weekendto participate.

## Important Dates

* Launch Time:Friday, Feb 27 3:00 AM UTC
* Submissions Due:Monday, Mar 2 7:59 AM UTC
