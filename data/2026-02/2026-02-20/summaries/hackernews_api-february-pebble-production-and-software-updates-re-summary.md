# February Pebble Production and Software Updates | rePebble Blog

# Mega update on Pebble Time 2, Pebble Round 2 and Index 01

Pebble is nearing the shipping of three new hardware products (Pebble Time 2, Pebble Round 2, and Index 01) along with their software. The production process is complex, involving balancing cost, quality, and speed, with potential for both positive and negative developments. Unlike software, hardware issues are difficult to fix post-shipment.

## Pebble Time 2 Production Update

Pebble Time 2 is currently in the Production Verification Test (PVT) phase, the final stage before mass production. Recent tests have passed, and mass production is scheduled to begin on March 9th. Key improvements made include waterproofing, now rated at 30m (3ATM), allowing for swimming but not hot tubs/saunas or exposure to hot/high-pressure water. The first PT2s are expected to arrive in early April, with all pre-orders fulfilled by the beginning of June. Customers will be prompted to confirm their address, select optional accessories, and pay any applicable taxes (e.g., $10 tariff for US orders) a few weeks before shipping.

## Index 01 Production Update

Index 01 is also in the PVT phase, with waterproofing tested to a 1m submersion rating (IPX8). Mass production is still targeted for March, but a firm start date is pending. A new $10 ring sizer kit will be available to help users determine their correct size, as Pebble sizes differ from other brands. Pebble is also considering offering sizes 14 and 15, pending interest.

## Pebble Round 2 Update

Pebble Round 2 is progressing well, having completed Design Verification 1 (DVT1). A significant advantage is the near identical electrical design to Pebble Time 2, allowing the firmware team to efficiently develop features for both devices. Mass production is estimated to start in late May, following waterproofing tests and final tweaks.

## Software Updates

Significant software improvements have been made to PebbleOS and the Pebble mobile app, including:

* Weather now includes sunrise/sunset timeline pins and a dedicated Weather app.
* WhatsApp calls are now displayed as calls on Android.
* A major background crash bug in Pebble iOS causing data fetching issues has been fixed.
* Websocket support has been added to Pebble iOS.
* The Pebble mobile app now handles network requests for older, non-functional weather APIs, using Open-Meteo to keep old watch faces working.
* The Pebble App Store is now native within the Pebble mobile app.
* Users can now filter apps by functionality (e.g., older apps, open-source apps).
* Some PebbleKit 1.0 Android apps should now work due to Google's changes, but developers are encouraged to upgrade to PebbleKit 2.0 for new companion apps.
* Watch settings can now be adjusted in the Pebble mobile app and synced across all Pebble watches.
* Community contributions have added new notification icons for older apps.
* Software work has been ongoing for Obelix factory verification.
* A left-handed mode has been implemented.
* Health data is now synced from the watch to the phone.
* The SDK and developer tools are receiving updates.
