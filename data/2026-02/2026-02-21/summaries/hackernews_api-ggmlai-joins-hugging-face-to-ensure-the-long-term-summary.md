# ggm.ai Joins Hugging Face for the Future of Local AI

This GitHub discussion announces that ggm.ai, the core team behind llama.cpp, is joining Hugging Face. The primary goal of this partnership is to ensure the long-term sustainability and progress of the open-source Local AI ecosystem.

## Key Points:

* **Continued Openness:** The llama.cpp project will remain open-source and community-driven. The ggm.ai team will continue to lead, maintain, and support the libraries and related projects.
* **Long-Term Sustainability:** The partnership with Hugging Face provides ggm.ai with long-term resources, enhancing the project's ability to grow and thrive.
* **Enhanced User Experience & Integration:**  A key focus will be on improving the user experience and seamless integration with the Hugging Face Transformers library for broader model support and quality control.
* **Strong Existing Collaboration:** Hugging Face engineers have already significantly contributed to llama.cpp, including core functionalities, an inference server, multi-modal support, and integration with Hugging Face Inference Endpoints.
* **Future Technical Focus:** Joint efforts will prioritize seamless integration with Transformers and improved packaging and user experience for easier deployment of local models.
* **Long-Term Vision:** The shared goal is to provide the foundational tools for accessible open-source superintelligence, working with the growing Local AI community.

## Why the Change?

Since its inception in 2023, ggm.ai has focused on fostering a strong open-source developer community and establishing llama.cpp as the standard for efficient local AI inference. Hugging Face has been a supportive partner, contributing significantly to the project's development and infrastructure. Formalizing this collaboration is seen as a natural progression to ensure the project's future success.

## What Will Change?

The core team at ggm.ai will continue to dedicate their full time to llama.cpp. The community will maintain its autonomy in technical and architectural decisions. Hugging Face will provide the necessary resources for the project's long-term growth. Users can expect faster support for their favorite models as they are released.
