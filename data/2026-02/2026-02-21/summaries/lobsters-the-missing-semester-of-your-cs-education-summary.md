# The Missing Semester of Your CS Education

## IAP 2026

* This course addresses a critical gap in traditional CS education: proficiency with essential developer tools.
* It aims to teach students how to effectively use the command-line, text editors, version control systems (like Git), and other tools crucial for software development.
* Mastering these tools saves time and enables the solution of complex problems.
* The course integrates the use of current AI-enabled and AI-enhanced tools and workflows into each lecture.
