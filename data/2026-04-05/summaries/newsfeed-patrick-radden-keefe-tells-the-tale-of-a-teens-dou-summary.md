## Plot summary
- In early 2019 Zac Brettler, a recent graduate of an elite private school and grandson of a prominent London rabbi, jumped from the balcony of a fifth‑floor luxury apartment overlooking the River Thames.
- He struck the embankment and was found face‑down in the riverbank mud.
- The apartment, worth over $5 million, was owned by a Saudi princess and occupied by London gangster Dave Sharma.
- Brettler had been posing as “Zac Ismailov,” the son of a fictitious Russian oligarch who was supposed to inherit more than $270 million.
- Sharma eventually discovered the deception shortly before Brettler’s death.

## Investigation and unanswered questions

- The book follows the family’s search for truth and the broader inquiry into the city’s hidden criminal networks.
