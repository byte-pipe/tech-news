## Overview
- PowerWash Simulator (2021) and its sequel (2025) are “mundane job simulation” games where players clean various dirty locations with a pressure washer.
- Developed by Brighton‑based FuturLab, led by CEO Kirsty Rigden.
- The sequel has been nominated for two BAFTA Games Awards.

- Over 17 million copies sold worldwide.
- A 2021 gameplay video by Markiplier has amassed more than 9 million YouTube views.
- Comedian Ellie Gibson holds the Guinness World Record for longest continuous play (24 h 6 min 33 s).
