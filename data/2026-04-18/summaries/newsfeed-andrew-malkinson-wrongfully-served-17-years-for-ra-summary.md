## Andrew Malkinson Wrongfully Served 17 Years for Rape

Andrew Malkinson, a 33-year-old from Grimsby, has been wrongfully convicted of rape. He is serving 17 years in prison after spending 20 years in jail.

Malkinson was initially blamed for the attack on a woman who claimed to have been walked upon by one of her ex-boyfriends. The police investigation found physical evidence that linked Malkinson at the scene, but it did not match him. Instead, it matched an unidentified man who appeared to be the aggressor in the situation.

However, DNA testing later revealed that the saliva from the woman's vest top belonged to a man named Paul Quinn during the time period of the alleged attack. Initially, prosecutors considered this new evidence to be relevant and used it to secure Malkinson's conviction on attempted murder charges.

The delay in identifying Quinn comes as the true perpetrator was finally found guilty of rape, strangulation, and grievous bodily harm through a month-long trial that began in 2023.
