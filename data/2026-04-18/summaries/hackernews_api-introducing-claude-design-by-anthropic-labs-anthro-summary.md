# Introducing Claude Design by Anthropic Labs
## Overview
Anthropic Labs is introducing Claude Design, a new product that enables seamless collaboration between designers and Claude, AI-powered vision model.

## Key Features of Claude Design

- **Rapid Prototyping**: Claude Design allows designers to create interactive prototypes without code review or PRs.
- **Automatic Design System**: For teams, the system applies your design system automatically, ensuring consistency throughout the company's designs.
- **Wide Range of Projects**: Includes real-world projects like product wireframes and mockups, design explorations, pitch decks and presentations, marketing collateral, and more.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Design users can find everything they need to create visual work, with features such as rich text comments, direct edits, sliders (customizable by Claude), and collaborative editing.

## How It Works
1. Onboarding: Claude builds a design system for your team using your codebase and design files.
2. Import and Refine: designers can add elements directly from their website, edit inline, or refine the design with fine-grained controls.
3. Collaborate and Optimize: Designers can comment on specific elements, make edits directly to the design, and work concurrently with Claude.

## Notable Applications

- **Realistic Prototypes**: Enable designers to bring interactive prototype ideas into life quickly.
- **Product Wireframes and Mockups**: Get immediate feedback from team members during product development cycles.
- **Pitch Decks and Presentations**: Create polished slideshows efficiently using front-end code integration tools (Canva).
- **Marketing Collateral**: Designers can craft landing pages, social media assets, and campaign visuals with ease.

## Benefits
 Claude Design streamlines the design process for any team size. Its features make collaboration easier while reducing re-work and maintaining consistency throughout projects.
