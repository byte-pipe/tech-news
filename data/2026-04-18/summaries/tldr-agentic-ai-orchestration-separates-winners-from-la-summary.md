# Agentic AI orchestration separates winners from laggards

## The Importance of Business-led Execution with Technology

Agentic AI initiatives are typically stalling due to a lack of focus on business execution, rather than technical issues. Effective agentic AI systems rely on the coordination of multiple departments and units across an organization. However, many startups and companies have seen their workflow hindered by overly ambitious goals.

## The Problem with Technological Solutions Alone

Many failures in agentic AI endeavors can be attributed to a failure to tie technology back to business outcomes, KPIs, and organizational structure. The Qlik Technologies Inc. senior vice president of global professional services, Randy Mickey, highlights the importance of focusing on "business-led execution that ties AI workflows directly to outcomes".

## Achieving Business Goals with Agentic AI

Agentic AI orchestration involves understanding the customer's strategic objectives, pain points, and goals, then backfilling it into the chosen technology. Qlik advises building on existing systems rather than replacing them in order to scale modernization without disrupting existing investments.

## Key Takeaways from Qlik Technologies Inc. Experts

- It's not about choosing a new tech stack but leveraging existing infrastructure.
- Business-led execution is vital, emphasizing outcomes and KPIs as well as organizational structure.
- Focusing too much on technology can lead to failure; instead, aim for agentic AI orchestration integrating business goals with technological solutions.

# In Conclusion

Effective leadership in agenic AI projects is more important than the specific technologies chosen or applied. Establishing strong connections between business strategies and technology should be accomplished through focused execution rather than adopting technologically oriented methods alone.
