# Nomura: Four out five institutional investors plan to pile into crypto with DeFi a top focus - DL News

**Four out of Five Institutional Investors Plan to Invest in Crypto**

Almost four out of five institutional investors plan to invest between 2% and 5% of their total assets under management into cryptocurrencies, according to Nomura. This shift indicates a preparatory period rather than immediate investment.

**Key Points:**

* Around 80% of institutions surveyed by Nomura said they will increase their crypto investments in the coming year.
* Decentralized finance (DeFi) and stablecoins are key areas of focus for many institutional investors.
* Institutions prioritize improved security, risk management frameworks, and regulatory reforms before investing heavily in cryptocurrencies.

**Institutional Investors' Focus:**

* 65% view cryptocurrency as a diversification tool against stocks and other assets.
* Many institutions see crypto as a way to increase returns while minimizing volatility.
* The use of cryptocurrency yield strategies has become more prevalent among institutional investors.

**Challenges Persisting Despite Growth:**

* Clearance on valuation methods
* Continued uncertainty around regulatory environments
* Volatility affecting investment performance

**Increased Investment in DeFi Strategies:**

* Institutional investors aim to leverage DeFi opportunities, including stablecoins and decentralized lending platforms.
* Some investors hope for deals or partnerships to broaden their crypto exposure beyond traditional investments.

## Market Outlook

While institutional investors continue to explore ways to tap into cryptocurrencies, the growing trend is marked by a gradual acceptance of the asset class rather than an immediate rush.
