**Used Electric Vehicle Options: $20,000-$25,000**

* The $20,000-$25,000 price range is ideal for buying used electric vehicles (EVs) as it offers significant value for money with thousands of EVs available.
* Buyers can expect to find:
	+ Older models with smaller batteries that may not have the same range as newer cars

* The recent abolishment of the used clean vehicle tax credit by the Trump administration creates uncertainty and drives buyers to explore new markets for less expensive EV options
* Electric Hyundais are also becoming increasingly popular, but there is limited selection in certain models due to missing ports or rear windscreen wipers

**Target Audience**

* Buyers who are only looking at $5,000 budget should be more choosy about their choices and focus on the 4-5 times price bracket
• **Comparing Residuals**

* According to Deloitte's report, EV residuals (incentives passed down from existing owners) underperform expectation, making it increasingly difficult for buyers to justify their current vehicle

**Advantage: Choosing $20,000-$25,000 EVs**

* Buyers can purchase better spec, newer model years, or fewer miles already on the odometer compared to spending the same money on a gas-powered or new car
