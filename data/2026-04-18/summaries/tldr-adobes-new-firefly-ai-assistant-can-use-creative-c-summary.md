# Adobe's new Firefly AI assistant can use Creative Cloud apps to complete tasks | TechCrunch

**Adobe Introduces Firefly AI Assistant**

* **Summary**
  - Adobe has introduced a new AI-powered assistant called Firefly, which can help users complete tasks using various Adobe apps such as Photoshop, Express, and Acrobat.
  - The product will be available for public beta in the coming weeks and is priced similarly to previous subscription tiers.
  - Users can control the outputs of the assistant using text prompts, buttons, sliders, or controls based on the project they are working on.

**Key Features**

* **Task Completion**: Firefly AI Assistant can take over tasks such as image editing, data analysis, or script writing.
* **Creative Preferences**: The assistant will learn more about users' creative preferences and suggest actions accordingly.
* **Skills Library**: Adobe offers various skills for the assistant, including social media assets that can help with ad creation.

**How it Works**

* **Task Description**: Users describe what they want to create or task the assistant with using text prompts, buttons, sliders, and controls based on the project.
* **Control Over Outputs**: The assistant shows control over outputs from all connected Adobe apps, allowing users to adjust settings in real-time.
* **Flexibility and Customization**: The assistant learns from users' creative preferences and adapts over time.

**Technical Details**

* **Availability**: Public beta will be available for the Firefly AI Assistant "in the coming weeks".
* **Pricing**: Pricing is similar to previous subscription tiers, but exact details are not yet disclosed.
* **Third-Party Support**: Adobe plans to work with third-party large language models that can enhance the assistant's capabilities.
