# InsightFinder raises $15M to help companies figure out where AI agents go wrong | TechCrunch

**Current State of AI Observability**

* The market for solutions to ensure tech system reliability has grown over the years
* Companies are shifting from "track everything" to "control complexity and costs"
* Rapid influx of AI agents within enterprises requires observability tools

**InsightFinder's Solution**

* InsightFinder uses machine learning to monitor, identify, and fix IT infrastructure issues since 2016
* Now addressing today's AI model reliability issue with an AI agent solution that includes:
	+ Detection and diagnosis
	+ Remediation and prevention

**Diagnosing AI Model Problems**

* Diagnosing AI model problems requires monitoring and analyzing multiple factors (data, models, infrastructure)
* Not just a machine learning problem or data problem; often a combination of these issues
* Infrastructures' operations must be understood to diagnose AI model problems

**Real-World Example**

* InsightFinder monitors its customers' infrastructure to identify outdated cache causing one company's fraud-detection model drift
* A similar issue was identified on another customer's server node, leading to proactive steps taken by the company to address it.

**InsightFinder's Product: Auton**

* Auton is a next-generation AI observability platform providing end-to-end feedback loop support for development, evaluation, and production stages
