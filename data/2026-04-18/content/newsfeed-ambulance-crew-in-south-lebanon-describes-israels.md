Ambulance crew in south Lebanon describes Israel’s ‘triple-tap’ attack

Ambulance workers in Lebanon recalled Israeli ‘triple-tap’ targeted strikes which killed four paramedics and wounded six. Video from the attack showed a barrage of fire hitting the medics as they aided their colleagues injured in two previous bombings. At least 100 medical workers have been killed in deliberate Israeli attacks.
