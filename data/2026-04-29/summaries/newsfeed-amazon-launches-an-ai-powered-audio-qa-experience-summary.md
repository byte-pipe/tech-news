# Amazon launches AI‑powered audio Q&A experience on product pages

## Key points
- Amazon introduced “Join the chat,” an AI‑driven feature that answers product questions with real‑time conversational audio.
- The AI acts as a “shopping expert,” synthesizing product specs, customer reviews, and other data into natural‑language responses.
- Answers are contextual, building on previous interactions without repeating information, mimicking a knowledgeable store associate.
- The feature is part of the broader “Hear the highlights” experience, which already provides short audio summaries for millions of items in the Amazon Shopping app (U.S. only, limited product set).

## How it works
- Users open a product page in the Amazon Shopping app and tap the **Hear the highlights** button beneath the product image.
- They can listen to a brief audio overview or tap the **Join the chat** icon to ask specific questions via text or voice.
- Audio playback continues while users browse other parts of the app.

## Related AI shopping tools
- **Rufus** – generative AI assistant for product research and comparison.  
- **Interests** – continuously surfaces new items aligned with a shopper’s preferences.  
- **Help me decide** – suggests products based on search, browsing, and purchase history.
