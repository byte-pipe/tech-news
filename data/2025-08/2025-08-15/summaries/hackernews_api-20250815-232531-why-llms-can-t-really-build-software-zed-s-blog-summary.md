While Large Language Models (LLMs) such as those used in AI chatbots have made significant strides in generating high-quality output, they lack the ability to maintain this critical mental model, which is essential for creating and troubleshooting complex software systems.

**Technical Feasibility**

Currently, it's challenging for LLMs to be considered effective software engineers due to their limited ability to maintain clear mental models. The process involves several stages:

1. Building a mathematical model of the requirements
2. Writing code that meets those expectations
3. Updating and refining that code (which requires testing)
4. Identifying problems and updating the model accordingly

To perform these tasks efficiently, specialized techniques, tools, and experience are needed that aren't typically associated with LLMs.
