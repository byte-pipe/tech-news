The wait is over! We are excited to announce the winners of theAlgolia MCP Server Challenge.

This was a tough one for our judges! From global sanctions indexes to stock analysis platforms to accessibility-focused search engines, we reviewed so many brilliant projects. The submissions from the community demonstrated the remarkable potential of combining Algolia's search capabilities with intelligent agents.

Regardless of whether or not you won, we hope you learned a thing or two and are proud of what you accomplished. Thank you to everyone who particiapted!

And now, our winners.
