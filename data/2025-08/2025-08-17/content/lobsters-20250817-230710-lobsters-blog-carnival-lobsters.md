Ablog carnivalsees everyone write a blog post about a topic and then the host links to them (perhaps with summaries)! I think this would be really fun and many Lobsters have blogs.

The submission deadline will be October 1st. (To submit, simply send the host (me, for now) a link to your article.) The host will try to come out with the summary quickly.

But what topic? Here are some initial ideas to seed discussion:

* (useless) but fun software
* show the code you’re most proud of
* what you love about programming and/or computation
* what is software engineering to you?

To actually choose a topic, I will make a comment below this which topic proposals should reply to. The proposal with the most upvotes will be our topic! Other discussion doesn’t go there.
