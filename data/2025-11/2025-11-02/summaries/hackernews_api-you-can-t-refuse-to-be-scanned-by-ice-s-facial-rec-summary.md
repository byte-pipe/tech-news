# You Can't Refuse To Be Scanned by ICE's Facial Recognition App, DHS Document Says

**You Can't Refuse To Be Scanned by ICE's Facial Recognition App**

A new document from the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) reveals that Immigration Enforcement's (ICE) mobile app, Mobile Fortify, stores photos captured during scans for up to 15 years, regardless of immigration or citizenship status.

**Key Points:**

* The app will store all captured photos for 15 years.
* Photos are stored in a non-redactable format.
* The document does not mention any restrictions on using the metadata found within the images.
