# The last-ever penny was minted today in Philadelphia | CNN Business

**The Last Penny to Be Minted**

*   The American penny has been officially discontinued in the United States and will stop being produced by the US Mint.
*   Its last minting was done on Wednesday afternoon at the Philadelphia Mint.
*   Due to the high production cost, pennies previously worth nearly four cents each now cost only 1.8 cents - still valuable enough for everyday uses like buying "penny candy" or purchasing parking meters.

**Impact and Challenges**

*   The phased-out decision has caused problems for retailers, who might incur fines or difficulties when handling cash transactions.
*   Merchants that choose to round prices may face penalties if their customers report non-compliance with the new regulations.
*   To mitigate these issues, some retail chains like Kwik Trip have opted not to carry coins worth less than a nickel, while others are seeking additional funding from companies operating blanks used for making pennies.
