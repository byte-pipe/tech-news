# Developers vs AI: How Smart Tools Make Us Dumber Coders - DEV Community

**The Dark Side of AI: How Overreliance on Technology is Dulling Our Edge**

Despite being able to write regex code every day, I realized I hadn't by hand for weeks. This was not because the tool didn't require the level of expertise; in fact, it worked perfectly – until it stopped.

At first, chatbots like ChatGPT seemed to have my back. However, when they weren't actively involved, I found out that AI had taken over. The once-great developer began to feel dull and unresponsive, as if they were relying on machines rather than human ingenuity.

**The Problems with Overreliance**

We often talk about the benefits of AI in development – faster learning, more efficient automation, and increased productivity. However, we rarely discuss the downsides when we overuse these tools. Specifically:

*   **Problem-solving skills dwindle**: When relying too heavily on AI, I no longer have to exercise my critical thinking skills.
*   **Learning is lost**: With prompt-based learning becoming increasingly prevalent, our knowledge acquisition process becomes less engaging and more superficial.
*   **Communication suffers**: By not explicitly articulating thoughts in code comments and documentation, developers' mental clarity falters.
*   **Creativity decreases**: AI often suggests predictable solutions, leaving the ability to experiment with new ideas and explore uncharted territories dormant.

The AI-driven world might seem exciting at first, but we must be cautious about our reliance on these technologies. As developers, it's essential to strike a balance between harnessing the power of AI and maintaining human intuition, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
