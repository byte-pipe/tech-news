# X5.1 solar flare, G4 geomagnetic storm watch | SpaceWeatherLive.com

**X5.1 Solar Flare and G4 Geomagnetic Storm Watch**

**Summary:**

A powerful X5.1 solar flare, the sixth strongest in years, has been detected by space weather agencies, prompting a G4 geomagnetic storm watch for November 12-13.

* A large coronal mass ejection (CME) is predicted to impact Earth on November 16 UTC due to its fast speed and favorable solar wind conditions.
* The X5.1 solar flare will likely produce an aurora visible from locations south of Germany, Ukraine, Switzerland, Austria, France, and northern France, in the US as far as Nevada and Arkansas.
* Two other CMEs from a neighboring region are expected to arrive within 6-18 hours.
