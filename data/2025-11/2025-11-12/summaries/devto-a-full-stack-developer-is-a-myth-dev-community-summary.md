# A Full-Stack Developer Is A Myth - DEV Community

**A Full-Stack Developer is Not As Mastered As They Claim**

### Key Points

* A "full-stack developer" is a myth with no depth in most modern technologies
* Technologies have evolved significantly since 20 years ago, requiring new skill sets
* Core skills are shallow and often overlap between areas, leading to unnecessary specialization
* Mastery of the entire stack requires intense, focused study and problem-solving experience

### Original Perspective

* The article challenges viewers to reevaluate their assumptions about full-stack development
* It highlights the rapidly changing landscape of technologies and the difficulty in mastering them all
* True mastery involves understanding underlying principles and systems, not just surface-level features
