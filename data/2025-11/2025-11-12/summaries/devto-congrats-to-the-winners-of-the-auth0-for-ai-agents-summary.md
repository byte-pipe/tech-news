# Congrats to the Winners of the Auth0 for AI Agents Challenge! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

* The winning participants in the Authentic for AI Agents Challenge have demonstrated the importance of prioritizing security, user control, and transparency as it relates to authentication and authorization.
* Winners included ESG Copilot (@omkar598) who leveraged Auth0's security stack to secure multi-agent ESG compliance, Assistant0 (@async_dimedelivered) who implemented comprehensive security features for an enterprise-grade AI assistant, and Study-Flow (Anshu Mandal) who integrated Token Vault into an existing study management platform using a hybrid authentication approach.
* Each winner will receive $1,000 USD, participate in the DEV community with their submission badge, and be eligible to win exclusives from Auth0 for participating in the challenge.
* Auth0 has expressed gratitude to its sponsors for providing tools to build secure AI agents and wishes good luck to all participants.
