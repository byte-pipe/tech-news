# From Skeptic to Believer: A Process-Driven Approach to Vibe Coding - DEV Community

**Vibe Coding A Structured Approach**

The article discusses the concept of **vibe coding**, which is a workflow optimization technique for developers. The author initially struggled with the term, but after encountering Keith Ballinger's approach on an Agent Factor podcast episode, they reevaluated their perspective.

**Key Points:**

* Vibe coding is not about chaos; it's about creating a structured template
* It involves defining the user experience and goals from the end-user's perspective
* The template includes three steps:
 1. User Experience (UG) creation to establish clear guidelines
 2. Programming Language selection based on LLM recommendations
 3. Technical Design creation for the mdview project

**Value of Vibe Coding:**

* It's a powerful tool for exploration in unfamiliar territory or expertise when working with complex projects
* It's also suitable for acceleration when working efficiently within familiar environments
