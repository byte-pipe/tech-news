# Devs' Changing Role

The industry has changed from 80% senior developers to around 90% junior developers who implement AI-assisted coding tools. The process of becoming a senior developer is changing dramatically due to increased reliance on AI-generated code.

Ultimately, this scenario highlights the need for open discussions and reassessments about the human role in development. To adapt to this rapidly evolving landscape, individuals and organizations must reevaluate their priorities regarding coding skills, focus shifts towards specialized skill sets, or develop robust training programs focusing on those areas – an option many have been hesitant to address thus far.
