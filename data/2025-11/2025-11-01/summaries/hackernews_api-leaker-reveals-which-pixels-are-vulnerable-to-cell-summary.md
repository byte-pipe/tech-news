# Leaker reveals which Pixels are vulnerable to Cellebrite phone hacking - Ars Technica

**List of Pixel Phones Vulnerable to Cellebrite Phone Hacking**

*   **Pixel 6**: Not protected in any state
*   **Pixel 7**: Not protected in any state for unlocked phones
*   **Pixel 8**: Protected only after first unlock (BFU)
*   **Pixel 9**: Protected only after first unlock (BFU) and unlocked

**Security Measures for Pixel Phones with GrapheneOS**

|  | **Locked State** | **Unlocked State** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Cellebrite Extraction | Limited to data accessible through GrapheneOS | Affected by updated builds on GrapheneOS, secure from full control |
| Unlock Detection | Not detected in calls, but police may still notice unsecured networks or compromised accounts |
| ESIM Copying | Not protected for unlocked devices or those not running after late 2022 software |

**Key Information**

*   Cellebrite can extract data from Pixel 6, 7, 8, and 9 phones in unlocked, AFU (after first unlock), and BFU states on stock software.
*   Access to Pixel 10 series is limited for those running GrapheneOS due to the newer operating system not supporting security measures.
*   Police can have a harder time accessing devices with GrapheneOS due to limitations in data extraction during certain states.
