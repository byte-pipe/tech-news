# Breaking News: OpenAI Rebrands to OpaqueAI - DEV Community

### Key Points
- OpenAI's MCP support launched on September 10 but started breaking shortly after.
- Developers reported numerous issues, including malformed JSON, missing handshake responses, and reproducible crashes.
- The problems were public, reproducible, and ignored by the company.
- Custom connectors stopped working, and previously stable MCP servers went dark.
