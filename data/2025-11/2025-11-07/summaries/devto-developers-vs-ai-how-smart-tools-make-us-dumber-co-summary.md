# The Dark Side of Technology: How AI is Dulling Your Edge as a Developer

## Key Points:
- ChatGPT can write code by hand, but when relied upon too much, it dulls the developer's edge due to lack of intentional coding.
- Pattern matching abilities are essential in debugging and problem-solving but become less effective without user involvement.
- The loss of learning experience is another significant effect of relying on technology for task completion.
- Communication clarity, creativity, intuition, and ownership suffer as developers opt for AI assistance over their own skills.
- Staying sharp requires intentional coding practice, reflection, and teaching.

## Important Details:

* ChatGPT generates regex code by hand, leading to a reliance on the tool after a certain period of time.
* Intentional coding is crucial in problem-solving and learning process efficiency reduction.
* AI assistance reduces the need for extensive debugging efforts and can lead to less understanding of programming concepts.
* The importance of explaining thought processes through code comments cannot be overstated.
* Developers must recognize that too much reliance on technology can dull their edge, making it essential to maintain an active role in coding tasks.

## Best Practices:

* Develop critical thinking skills to complement AI assistance.
* Take breaks from using AI tools for task completion and engage in unrelated projects.
* Focus on reflective learning methods like debugging individual parts of code with AI help as a supplement.
* Teach your colleagues about effective coding practices considering the strengths and limitations of AI.
