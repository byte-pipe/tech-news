# Congrats to the Winners of the Auth0 for AI Agents Challenge! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article passage:

**Auth0 for AI Agents Challenge Winners Announced**

The winners of the Auth0 challenge are:

1. **ESG Copilot**: Created an autonomous multi-agent AI system to automate ESG compliance, leveraging Auth0's security stack.
2. **Assistant0**: Designed a secure enterprise-grade AI assistant with comprehensive authentication and authorization features.
3. **Study-Flow**: Integrated Auth0 Token Vault into an existing study management platform while maintaining their original Convex Auth system.

These winners were selected based on the community's submission of use cases that demonstrated best practices in prioritizing security, user control, and transparency for authentication and authorization as it relates to AI agents.

**What's Next**

The challenge organizers are grateful to Auth0 for sponsoring the event and encourage continued participation in future challenges.
