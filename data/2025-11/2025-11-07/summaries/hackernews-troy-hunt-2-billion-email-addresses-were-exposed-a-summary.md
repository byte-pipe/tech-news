# Troy Hunt: 2 Billion Email Addresses Were Exposed, and We Indexed Them All in Have I Been Pwned

**Troy Hunt: 2 Billion Email Addresses Exposed**

The email address database of 2 billion individuals has been compromised due to several breaches across various companies and organizations. This is not an exaggeration; a significant margin of the data was found to be new, never before seen, or reused passwords.

**Key Points:**

*   A comprehensive dataset of 1.3 billion unique passwords has been extracted from Synthient's threat intelligence platform.
*   This is part of a broader dataset that includes around 183 million unique email addresses also indexed in their platform's threat intelligence.
*   Stealer logs are collected through malware infection, which can then be used to hijack compromised accounts or logged-in sessions.

**Summary:**

The exposure of sensitive data due to various breaches has led to the creation of a massive dataset containing:

*   2 billion email addresses
*   Approximately 1.3 billion unique passwords

This dataset is not exaggerated but rather a reflection of ongoing threats across multiple industries, including Synthient's phishing (I) threat intelligence platform.

The process of verifying and utilizing this data involves:

*   Authentication of the data source to ensure integrity
*   Verification that the identified data belongs to real individuals or organizations
*   Use cases such as credential stuffing which use stolen login information extracted from one breach to infiltrate multiple accounts
