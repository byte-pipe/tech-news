# Congrats to the Winners of the Auth0 for AI Agents Challenge! - DEV Community

**Winners of the Auth0 for AI Agents Challenge**

*   **EGS Copilot**: A multi-agent AI system that automates ESG compliance for small and medium businesses, securing data through Auth0 Universal Login and Token Vault.
*   **Assistant0**: An enterprise-grade AI assistant secured by Auth0's comprehensive security features, utilizing Client-Initiated Backchannel Authentication (CIBA), and FGA with Okta.

**Key Points**

### Secure ESG Compliance
EGS Copilot automates ESG compliance for businesses through a multi-agent system.

### Enterprise AI Assistants
Assistant0 securely manages Gmail, Google Calendar, web search, and document access via Auth0's security features.

### Versatility of Auth0

Study-Flow demonstrates the versatility of Auth0 by integrating Token Vault into an existing study management platform.

### Sponsorship

Auth0 sponsors the challenge, highlighting its ability to provide secure authentication and authorization tools for building AI Agents.
