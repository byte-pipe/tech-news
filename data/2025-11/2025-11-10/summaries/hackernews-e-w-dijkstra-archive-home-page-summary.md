# Edsger W. Dijkstra Archive Home Page

## Overview

Edsger Wybe Dijkstra was a pioneering computing scientist who made significant contributions to computer science's founding generation.

## Key Contributions

* Algorithm design
* Programming languages
* Operating systems
* Distributed processing
* Formal specification and verification
* Design of mathematical arguments

## Teaching and Industry Engagement

Dijkstra was interested in teaching and relations between academia and industry, reflecting his conviction that scientists should engage with their scientific colleagues.

## EWDs and Publications

Since the early 20th century, Dijkstra has published numerous technical notes known as "EWDs" (Edsger W. Dijkstra Archive Documents) through over four decades of postal correspondence.

## Access to Unpublished Manuscripts

Thousands of his manuscripts remain unpublished due to their high value, access limitations, and lack of citation opportunities, with over 1,000 documented online for public viewing at The Center for American History.
