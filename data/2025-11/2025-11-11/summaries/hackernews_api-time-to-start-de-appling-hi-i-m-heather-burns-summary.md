# Time to start de-Appling – Hi, I'm Heather Burns

**Time to Start De-Appling: A Key Reminder for UK Users**

It is essential for all UK users with Advanced Data Protection (ADP) enabled on their Apple devices to take immediate action. As of a September update, ADP will be withdrawn from the UK market, and users who have already activated it will not have the option to switch off their Advanced Data Protection unless they manually disable it.

**What This Means for You**

The withdrawal of ADP will effectively change the way data is secured on your Apple devices. Without end-to-end encryption as previously available, a significant 10 iCloud data categories protected by Advanced Data Protection will be switched to Standard Data Protection using "Standard Data Protection" instead:

*   **iCloud Backup**,
*   **iCloud Drive** (although encrypted),
*   **iCloud Photos**
*   **Notes**
*   **Reminders**
*   **Safari Bookmarks**
*   (**_Note_: _**)  **Voice Memos**
*   **Wallet Passes**
*   _**Freeform**_

For users who have not yet enabled Advanced Data Protection, you will now need to take action to switch off this protection immediately.

**Next Steps**

To avoid losing your personal or operational security and prevent unauthorized access:

1.  Switch off Advanced Data Protection on all Apple devices
2.  Use the iCloud settings to completely disable ADP:
    *   Go to **iCloud Settings**
    *   Select "Advanced"
    *   Click "Turn Off" next to "Advanced Data Protection"

By taking these necessary steps, you can ensure that your personal and operational data remains secure despite this change in the US law.
