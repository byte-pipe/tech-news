# From Skeptic to Believer: A Process-Driven Approach to Vibe Coding - DEV Community

## Vibe Coding: A Structured Approach to AI-Assisted Development

The term "vibe coding" has generated skepticism in some cases, often due to misunderstandings about its intended purpose. In this article, the author reflects on their past negative reaction to vibe coding and how Keith Ballinger's approach changed their perspective.

After discussing the concept with him as part of the Agent Factor podcast, Keith presented a surprising structure for vibe coding - essentially a blueprint or template designed by the user themselves. This structure outlines not only the desired outcome but also takes into account the goals and limitations of the AI, providing an efficient workflow.

Here's a breakdown of his approach:

*   **Step 1: define the user experience**. Prioritize creating a clear User Guide and timestamp.
*   **Step 2: get a programming language suggestion**. Present three options to consider for the task at hand.
*   **Step 3: create a technical blueprint**. Outline technologies, architecture, and a working design in an Arch.md file.

This template offers a structured workflow for both creative exploration and efficient execution of AI-assisted development tasks, highlighting its value as both a tool for innovation when needed and an accelerator of expertise.
