# Congrats to the Winners of the Auth0 for AI Agents Challenge! - DEV Community

*   **Auth0 for AI Agents Challenge Winners**: The winners of this challenge were announced, and they demonstrated innovative use cases that showcased security, user control, and transparency in authentication and authorization.
*   **ESG Copilot**: A small business AI system developed by @omkar598built utilizes Auth0's security stack to automate ESG compliance. This submission highlights the importance of prioritizing security for various industry use cases.
*   **Assistant0**: An enterprise-grade AI assistant that manages multiple platforms through Auth0's comprehensive security features. The implementation showcases Client-Initiated Backchannel Authentication (CIBA) and TokenVault functionality.
*   **Study-Flow**: Hybrid authentication was integrated into an existing study management platform, demonstrating the versatility of Auth0 for AI Agents. This submission highlights the benefit of utilizing Token Vault over manual token management.
*   **Sponsorship**: An appreciation is extended to Auth0for sponsoring this challenge. The community's work with Auth0's authentication and authorization features will be appreciated.

To recap:

-   A successful challenge with innovative use cases demonstrates the importance of prioritizing security, user control, and transparency in AI agents.
-   Specific submissions like ESG Copilot and Assistant0 showcase the full power of Auth0's security stack, showcasing its ability to secure complex systems at scale.
-   Hybrid authentication approaches were demonstrated, highlighting versatility of tools that integrate multiple features.
