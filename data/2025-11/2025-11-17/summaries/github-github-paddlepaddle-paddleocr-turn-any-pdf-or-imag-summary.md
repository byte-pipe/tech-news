**PaddlePaddle Paper - PaddleOCR**

### Overview
PaddlePaddle is a powerful, lightweight OCR (Optical Character Recognition) toolkit that enables turn-by-turn PDF or image document conversion into structured data for artificial intelligence models.

### Notable Features
- Supports 100+ languages
- Bridges the gap between images/PDFs and Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Open-source under the Apache-2.0 license
