# Legendary game designer, programmer, Space Invaders champion, and LGBTQ trailblazer Rebecca Heineman has died | PC Gamer

### Rebecca Heineman (1959 - )
**Legacy and Impact**

* Became a notable title holder: National Space Invaders tournament champion in 1980
* Won recognition for her accomplishments at the US videogame level
* Designed games at Interplay, including Wasteland and Fallout
* Co-founded Interplay with Brian Fargo, Jay Patel, and Troy Worrell

### Later Life and Passings

**Cancer Diagnosis**

* Diagnosed with cancer in November 2023, prompting an aggressive treatment plan
* Entered palliative care but continued to be active through technology

### Tributes and Recognition

* Friend of Heidi McDonald, who shared a last goodbye message on Facebook
* Received the Gayming (an online gaming community) Icon award for her work promoting diversity and inclusion in tech
* Her friend Brian Fargo stated publicly that she helped pave the way for feminist attitudes within game development
