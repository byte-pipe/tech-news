# 5 must know open-source repositories to build cool AI apps - DEV Community

## Building Real-Time AI Apps with Open-Source Tools: Key Findings

**1. Stream Vision Agents**

Stream Vision Agents is an open-source framework for building real-time, multimodal AI that can see, hear, and respond in milliseconds.

* **Key features**: Supports development on GitHub and has the ability to integrate different platforms (OpenAI Realtime and Gemini Live).
* **Benefits**: Faster prototyping, no black boxes, and vendor lock-in.
* **Example use case**: Building real-time video and audio intelligence for developers who want to bring true intelligence to live video.

## 2. Open-Sora**

Open-Sora is an open-source text-to-video generation tool that enables the conversion of text or images into high-quality videos with smooth motion, consistent frames, and stability.

* **Key features**: Supports text-based input and fine-tuning on personal datasets for domain-specific applications.
* **Why it's interesting**: Offers a flexible platform for experimentation and adaptation to specific use cases.
