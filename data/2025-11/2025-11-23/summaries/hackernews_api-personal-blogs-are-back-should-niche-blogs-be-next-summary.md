## Personal Blogs Are Back: Is Niche Blogging Next?

The blogging landscape has undergone significant changes over the years, with traditional niches like finance and technology giving way to more diverse and ever-expanding topics.

### Key Points:

*   Personal blogs are back in style
*   The rules of blogging are relatively few, allowing for meaningful content creation
*   The focus of niche blogs (or "niche resources") is on providing specialized knowledge or expertise in a particular area

## Niche Blogging: Can Personal Blogs Thrive?

While personal blogging may not be as mainstream as it once was, the concept remains relevant and continues to gain traction.

Niche platforms like Problogger.com, founded by Darren Rowse in 2004, have become staples for authors and bloggers looking to establish themselves as authorities in a specific field.
