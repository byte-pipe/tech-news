The wait is over! We are excited to announce the winners of the2025 Hacktoberfest Writing Challenge.

This year's Hacktoberfest Writing Challenge brought us stories of first-time contributors finding their footing, maintainers building communities around their projects, and developers reflecting on what open source means to them. As always, we were inspired by the community's dedication to sharing their knowledge and experiences.

Since we received close to three hundred submissions, we have decided to pick five winners per prompt!

We hope you enjoyed documenting your Hacktoberfest journey and are proud of what you accomplished, regardless of whether or not you get the crown.

Without further ado, our winners.
