# Linux Gamers Cross Over 3% Mark on Steam as of October 2025

By Liam Dawe - 2 Nov 2025 at 1:03 pm UTC
Last updated: 2 Nov 2025 at 1:04 pm UTC

**Latest Update to the 3% Mark: Linux Now Accounts for 3.05%**

The 3% mark, which was first broken by Linux gamers on Steam, has now been confirmed again as of October 2025. This is a significant milestone in the adoption of Linux on Steam.

**Key Statistics:**

* Windows 94.84%
* Linux 3.05%
* macOS 2.11%

The snapshot chart from our dedicated Steam Tracker pages shows a clear trend, with 3% Linux users now surpassing Windows 10 users. This trend is not surprising given the popularity of SteamDeck and its Linux distribution.

**Distribution Breakdown:**

SteamOS Holo 64-bit accounts for 27.18%, followed by Arch Linux at 10.32%. Other distributions such as Linux Mint, Freedesktop SDK, and Bazzite also have relatively small user bases compared to Windows.

**Popular Distributions:**

* SteamOS Holo 64-bit - 27.18%
* Arch Linux 64-bit - 10.32%
* Ubuntu Core 22 64-bit - 4.55%

**Rumors Surrounding Steam Frame and SteamOS Linux:**
With the Steam Deck being in the top 10 global sellers on Steam, it's likely that Valve will share more information about their upcoming Steam Frame kit in the future.

**Linux Adoption Growth:**
The numbers suggest that millions of people have been introduced to Linux via Steam recently. Given the growth rate and popularity of Linux, it seems likely that monthly active Linux users will be over 4 million worldwide soon.
