## Breaking Down the Myth of Compilers Being Hard to Write
A series of technical writing guides has helped dispel this myth: Jack Crenshaw's "Let's Build a Compiler!" (1988) and Marcel Hendrix's Forth translation. These guides focus on simple compilation techniques using Pascal or its variants.

## The Importance of a Compiler's Internal Representation
