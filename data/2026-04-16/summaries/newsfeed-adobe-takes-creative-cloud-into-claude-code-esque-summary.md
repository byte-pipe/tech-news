# Adobe takes Creative Cloud into Claude Code-esque territory - Ars Technica

**Creative Cloud Shift: Adobe Introduces Chat-Based Interface for Unified Productivity**

Adobe is revolutionizing its creative productivity applications with a chat-based interface that can handle complex tasks across multiple applications. The new tool, dubbed "Firefly AI Assistant," promises to streamline workflows by providing users with an efficient way to offload mundane tasks while also reducing the barrier to entry for inexperienced or casual users.

**Key Features and Benefits**

* **Multi-modal project management**: Firefly AI Assistant can manage complex projects across multiple creative apps, including Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere.
* **Chat-based interface**: The tool offers a chat-like experience that allows users to send messages and interact with the assistant in real-time.
* **Contextually relevant controls**: The dynamic assistant surfaces contextually relevant controls based on the task at hand, such as sliders for image editing tasks.
* **Learning features**: Firefly AI Assistant will learn users' preferences over time and enable customization or feature removal as needed.

**Skills: Prepackaged Integration and Workflows**

* **Skill library**: Users can access a prepackaged library of skills tailored to specific tasks, or build their own custom integrations with the aid of "skills."
* **Customizable and tunable**: Skills will be designed to be easily configurable by users.

**Comparison to Adobe's AI Strategy**

The introduction of Firefly AI Assistant marks a significant shift in Adobe's AI strategy, deviating from its previous approach that focused on built-in features within existing apps. Instead, the new tool enables users to work more integratedly with their creative apps while leveraging machine learning capabilities for new workflows.

**Public Beta and Future Plan**

* **Public beta**: Firefly AI Assistant will enter a public beta period within the next few weeks.
* **Future plans**: Adobe aims to release regular updates and expand its features to support other creative apps, further cementing the tool's role in creative productivity.
