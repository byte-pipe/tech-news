*   Cal.com's decision to close its closed-source platform is driven by increasing security threats.
*   The company's shift towards openness and open-source software addresses these growing concerns.
*   Companies relying on Cal.com for online booking scheduling may need to consider alternative solutions.
*   Semi-open or open-source alternatives could offer comparable functionality at a lower cost.
