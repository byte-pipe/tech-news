# AI-Assisted Cognition Endangers Human Development

**AI-Assisted Cognition: A Threat to Human Development**

* **Risks:** The increased use of artificial intelligence (AI) in cognition is exacerbating missing opportunities and risks, as it may lead to intellectual stagnation.
* **What is AI-assisted cognition?:** AI is assisting human cognitive processes by providing external static information or aiding in the transfer of knowledge. However, current models are stuck on past knowledge, leading to a static processing style that fails to capture new events and cultural changes.
* **Impact on cognitive development:** AIs tend to reflect old patterns and ideas, causing individuals to focus on familiar concepts rather than exploring new ones, potentially hindering human growth and progress.

**The Risks of AI-Assisted Cognition**

* **Static cognition:** AI models may limit innovation by relying on past knowledge, rather than encouraging exploration and analysis of new information.
* **Lack of cultural adaptation:** AIs are unlikely to adapt quickly to changing social norms and cultural shifts, potentially hindering the development of a more inclusive and cohesive global society.

**The Dynamic Dialectic Substrate**

* **Human knowledge depends on dynamic processes:** Human understanding is influenced by ongoing cognitive efforts, including dialogue with others, new experiences, and change over time.
* **Understanding this substrate will address AI cognition risks:** By grasping the underlying nature of human knowledge and development, we can recognize where AIs differ from traditional methods of thought, potentially allowing us to mitigate their negative effects.
