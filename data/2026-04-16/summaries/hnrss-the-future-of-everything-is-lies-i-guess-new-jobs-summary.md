### Incanters and LLM Specialization

* Incanters who work with Large Language Models (LLMs) to optimize their performance, providing better results by "threating" them or manipulating inputs.
* LLMs incanters may specialize in specific tasks such as predicting customer responses or generating text for social media.

### Process Engineers

* Experts responsible for quality control of AI output through the detection and removal of errors during processing.
* Development of system integrations, proofreading software, and post-production requirements to ensure consistency in LLM outputs.

### Statistical Engineers

* Specialists developing systems to measure, model, and optimize the variability (precision and recall) of ML models.
* Research into identifying biases or outliers in AI output data, improving overall model performance.
