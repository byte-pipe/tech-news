*   **Changes in Priority:** The Trump administration has changed priorities towards promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion. This shift seems contradictory to the original law intended to prevent racism in housing.
*   **Accusations and Examples:** A small group of HUD workers have come forward with anonymous letters accusing the department of thwarting their attempts to enforce fair housing laws. These accusations indicate a potential undermining of civil rights efforts.
