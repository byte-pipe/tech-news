# Gemini Robotics ER 1.6: Enhanced Embodied Reasoning for Real-World Robotics Tasks

Today, we are introducing Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6, a significant upgrade to our reasoning-first model that enables robots to reason about their physical environment with unprecedented precision. This advancement focuses on enhancing spatial reasoning and multi-view understanding, bridging the gap between digital intelligence and physical action.

## Key Features of Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6

**Key Features:**

* **Enhanced Spatial Reasoning**: Improves precision object detection and counting
* **Multi-View Understanding**: Supports visual and spatial understanding
* **Task Planning and Success Detection**: Actively calls tools like Google Search to find information and vision-language-action models (VLAs)
* **Instrument Reading**: Enables robots to read complex gauges and sight glasses

## Advancements over Previous Models

Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6 offers significant improvements over both Gemini Robotics-ER 1.5 and Gemini 3.0 Flash, particularly in spatial reasoning capabilities:

* **Pointing Capabilities**: Allows for precision object detection and counting
* **Success Detection**: Enables robots to navigate complex tasks with greater autonomy

## Availability and Examples

Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6 is now available via the **Gemini API** and **Google AI Studio**, alongside a developer's guide for getting started.
