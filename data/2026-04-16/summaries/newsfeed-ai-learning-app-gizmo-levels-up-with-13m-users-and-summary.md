# Gizmo AI-Powered Learning App Rapidly Grows in User Base and Investment
Gizmo, anAI-powered learning platform, has witnessed a significant increase in user adoption since its launch in 2021, with over 13 million users across 120 countries. This growth is attributed to the investor's Series A funding of $22 million, which will be used to expand the company's engineering and AI teams as well as enhance its presence in the U.S. college market.

The funding marks a significant milestone for Gizmo following its earlier acquisition for TechCrunch's $3.5 million seed round. With plans to scale to around 30 employees by year-end, Gizmo aims to establish itself among other edtech startups vying for student attention.

Gizmo emphasizes the importance of gamification in retaining users. It features leaderboards, streaks, daily life limitations on incorrect answers, and social interaction through challenge sharing with friends. The platform's success can be attributed to its ability to adapt to changing consumer behavior, such as excessive screen time reducing attention spans.

While other micro-learning platforms have gained traction among teenagers and young adults, Gizmo has garnered notable interest for its AI-driven approach and engaging user experience. By investing $22 million in the company, Shine Capital is poised to bolster Gizmo's growth.

The platform's focus on interactive study materials aligns with the growing demand for innovative learning solutions amidst declining academic performance and increased screen time. With its Series A funding secured, Gizmo is well-positioned to capitalise on this trend and establish itself as a leading AI-powered learning app in the education market.
