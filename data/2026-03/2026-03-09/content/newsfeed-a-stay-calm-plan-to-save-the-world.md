# A stay-calm plan to save the world

## Alas, almost no foreign leader is as reasonable as Finland’s president, Alexander Stubb

Share
Illustration: Chloe Cushman

Feb 24th 2026
|
5 min read

If a boatcarrying heads of state ever sank in a tempest, staying close to President Alexander Stubb of Finland would be a shrewd move. Mr Stubb has shipwreck survivor written all over him. He is tall, lithe and dauntingly fit, coming second for his age group after secretly entering a Finnish triathlon last summer. As importantly, Mr Stubb, 57, has built his political career around can-do optimism and a refusal to panic. In these troubled times, his fellow world leaders have noticed, and approve.
