# LibreOffice 26.2 Release Summary

## Overview
- Announcement of LibreOffice 26.2, the latest free and open‑source office suite.
- Emphasises faster performance, a more polished experience, and full user control without subscriptions, license restrictions, or data collection.

## Key Improvements
- **Markdown** import and export capabilities.
- Improved performance and responsiveness, especially for large documents.
- Enhanced compatibility with files from proprietary and open‑core office software, reducing formatting issues.
- Refined user‑interface behavior for a cleaner, more consistent experience.
- Expanded support for open document standards, ensuring long‑term access to files.
- Hundreds of bug fixes and stability improvements contributed by the global community.

## Community & Development
- Developed by an international community of developers, designers, translators, QA testers, and volunteers.
- Focus on adding features while improving quality, consistency, and stability with each release.

## Availability
- Available for Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- Supports over 120 languages out of the box.
- Free download with no licensing fees or vendor lock‑in.
