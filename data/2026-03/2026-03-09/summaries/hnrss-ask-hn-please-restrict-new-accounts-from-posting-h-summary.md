# Ask HN: Please restrict new accounts from posting

## Post Overview
- The original poster (Oras) observes a surge of AI‑generated posts on Hacker News, especially from new (green) accounts in the Show HN section.  
- Oras asks the site team to either block new accounts from posting or provide a default filter that shows only posts from accounts meeting certain criteria.  
- The concern is that HN could become “Twitter‑like,” filled with bots and low‑quality noise.

## Moderator Response
- Moderator **dang** announces a temporary restriction on Show HN submissions from new accounts.  
- The restriction is intended only for new accounts, not for all users.  
- The goal is to ensure some community participation before a Show HN is posted, preventing the section from becoming a simple promotional checklist.
