# AI Influencer Awards Season is Upon Us

The 5th Annual AI Personality of the Year award will take place in May, with a total prize fund of $20,000.

 Entries will be judged on several criteria: quality, social clout, brand appeal, and authenticity. Winners will be recognized at an event that bills itself as "the 'Oscars' for AI personalities".

 The contest allows established creators and newcomers alike to submit their AI-inspired characters or avatars on open-platform OpenArt site.

 Entries need to have a consistent look across social media channels, be able to engage with followers reliably, represent themselves accurately, and create an authentic narrative behind their avatar.
