# 5 great indie games from GDC 2026

## Article overview
- Written by Jay Peters, senior reporter at The Verge, published March 14 2026.

## Games highlighted

- **At Fate’s End**
  - Developer: Thunder Lotus (the team behind *Spiritfarer*).
  - Genre: Action‑adventure that explores sibling relationships through exploration, combat, and dialogue choices that affect the ending.
  - Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S.
  - Release: slated for 2026.

- **Mina the Hollower**
  - Developer: Yacht Club Games (known for *Shovel Knight*).
  - Premise: A homage to *Link’s Awakening* where the player controls a mouse that can dig through the ground.
  - Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5, Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2, Xbox Series X/S.
  - Release: scheduled for spring 2026 after a delay.

- **The Melty Way**
  - Developer: indie studio (not named in the article).
  - Gameplay: Super Meat Boy‑style platformer starring a slime that gradually melts and shrinks, granting higher jumps and tighter navigation but risking death if checkpoints are missed.
  - Platform: Steam (early access).
  - Release: early‑access launch on April 24 2026.

- **Poke a Nose**
  - Developer: Jelle van Meerendonk.
  - Concept: Play as a severed hand that collects toilet paper and completes levels by poking noses; absurd premise paired with precise flying mechanics.
  - Platform: PC.
  - Release: targeted for 2026, though early 2027 is considered more likely.

- **Super Meat Boy 3D**
  - Developer: team behind the original *Super Meat Boy*.
  - Description: A 3‑D reinterpretation of the notoriously difficult 2‑D platformer, retaining floaty jumps, buzzsaws, and fast restarts.
  - Platforms: PC, PlayStation 5, Nintendo Switch 2, Xbox Series X/S.
  - Release: planned for 2026.
