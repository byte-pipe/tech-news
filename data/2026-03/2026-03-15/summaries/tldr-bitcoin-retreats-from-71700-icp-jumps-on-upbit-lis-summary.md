## Market overview
- Bitcoin slipped to around $69,500 during European trading after failing to break $71,750, a move driven by ongoing US‑Israel‑Iran tensions.
- Altcoins mostly lagged: ZEC fell 4.5 %, AAVE down 2.1 %, while DeFi tokens CRV and JUP each dropped about 6.5 % in the past 24 hours.
- AI‑related tokens bucked the trend: Internet Computer (ICP) surged >8 % after being listed on Korean exchange Upbit, and Fetch.ai (FET) rose 6 % following bullish remarks from Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang.
- Gold and the US dollar were largely unchanged; US stock‑index futures edged up 0.15 %.
- Oil remained volatile, dipping to $81/barrel before rebounding to $89 during the European session.
