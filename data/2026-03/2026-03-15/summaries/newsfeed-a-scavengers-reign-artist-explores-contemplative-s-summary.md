# A Scavengers Reign artist explores contemplative sci‑fi in new comics

## Overview
- French artist and animator Jonathan Djob Nkondo, known for *Scavengers Reign* and the Gorillaz short *The Mountain, The Moon Cave and The Sad God*, is re‑issuing two self‑published graphic novels in English.
- The books will be released in hardcover and digital formats this summer, with a limited‑edition art‑book bundle at the $99 Kickstarter tier.

## Kickstarter performance
- Nkondo launched a Kickstarter for the English editions earlier this month.
- The campaign far exceeded its $7,500 goal, raising over $100,000 to date.
- Nkondo expressed surprise at the strong support, noting he “wasn’t expecting much.”

## The two graphic novels
- **Peaceful Remission** – follows an elderly couple living in a space‑age suburb where houses are built into asteroids, exploring their strained relationship.
- **Wandering** – a wordless narrative showing two perspectives of a far‑future world: a wealthy man inside a futuristic city and a younger survivor in the surrounding wasteland.
- Both stories focus on intimate personal relationships and move at a measured, contemplative pace similar to *Scavengers Reign*.

## Artistic approach
- Nkondo prioritizes aesthetic design over practicality, first shaping forms and then applying them to fashion, architecture, and vehicles.
- The comics feature original, striking designs for architecture, technology, and environments.
- Visual storytelling is emphasized; *Wandering* contains no dialogue, encouraging readers to linger on each panel.

## Future directions
- Two upcoming projects—a new comic and a short film—will experiment with more dialogue‑heavy storytelling, presenting a different creative challenge for Nkondo.

## Publication timeline
- Kickstarter remains open for pledges.
- Shipping of the English editions is expected to begin in the summer.
