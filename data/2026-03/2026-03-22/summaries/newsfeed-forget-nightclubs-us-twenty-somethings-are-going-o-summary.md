# Gym-based socializing on the rise: younger generations seeking more than just self-improvement
## Gyms as vibrant community hubs for young adults

* Growing trend among twenty-somethings to prioritize gym attendance over drinking culture
* 47% of PureGym new members and 40% of The Gym Group's Gen Z- age group (25-29) join gyms, with more than half forming a connection on the gym floor
* Changing definition of "wellness" emphasizing social experiences beyond workout routines

## Embracing communal spaces for friendly meetups

* Gyms seeing growth in fitness classes and communal events to foster social connections
