# Do Not Turn Child Protection Into Internet Access Control

### Age Verification is No Longer a Narrow Technique

Age verification measures are expanding into various digital services across Europe, the United States, and other regions. This shift towards digital guardianship can lead to changes in how technology operates within networked platforms.

**Realities Behind Current Debates**

*   The primary concern with these new age verification systems revolves around children's exposure to online risks.
*   However, a closer examination reveals that genuine safeguarding concerns arise from manipulative techniques rather than limited safeguards.
*   **Contextual Responsibility for Personal Computing**: Regulated data models that increasingly encompass higher-level components can enable more comprehensive and context-dependent behavior.

**The Misconception Behind the Debate**

The conversation surrounding digital age verification systems has become muddled by a simplistic dichotomy. **Content Moderation vs. Guardianship**.
*   Content moderation pertains to filtering or handling specific content based on predefined criteria.
*   Guardianship involves parental supervision, education, and guidance in evaluating the suitability of materials for children.
