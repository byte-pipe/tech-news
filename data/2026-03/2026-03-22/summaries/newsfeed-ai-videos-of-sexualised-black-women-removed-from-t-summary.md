# AI-generated black female influencers banned from TikTok after BBC investigation

• At least 20 accounts have been banned from TikTok due to their use of AI-generated black female digital characters or avatars.
• The accounts often show digitally manipulated images and videos, dressed in skimpy swimwear or revealing clothing.
• Many account names include terms such as "dark", "ebony", and "noir".
• Some accounts make comments about white males, including "loves white men" and "why I need a white guy in my life".

## Analysis

These AI-generated black female influencers are part of a broader trend on Instagram and TikTok that have raised concerns over racial tropes and language used. The BBC's investigation has highlighted the manipulation and use of images to promote sexualized content.
