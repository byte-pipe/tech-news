description: 'Standout PMs don’t just ship features. They kill them. Shipping isn’t the job. Shipping the right product is. A great PM doesn’t fall in love with the roadmap. They fall in love with the problem and have the guts to say: This isn’t solving it. This adds complexity. This doesn’t matter. Every feature, setting, UI, element should fight to exist. At Nest, we had one rule: If you can’t explain why it matters, it doesn’t ship. We asked product, engineering, design... “what’s the point of this?” If someone said “it’s on the roadmap” or “leadership wants it,” that wasn’t enough. You had to tell us the why. Not the press release. Not the metric. The reason a real person would care. That one rule killed dozens of features. And it was an integral part of our product culture.
