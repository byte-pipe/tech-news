## Can AI Generate Binary Directly? Is It Feasible? Does It Make Sense?

### Giorgi Kobaidze ・ Mar 8

#discuss

#ai

#compilers

#programming

@georgekobaidzeexplores the thought experiment of skipping source code entirely and having AI generate binary output directly, then breaks down why it's nearly impossible in practice. The post covers how such an approach would destroy determinism, make incremental changes unworkable, multiply token costs across every architecture target, and remove the only interface humans can actually read and maintain.
