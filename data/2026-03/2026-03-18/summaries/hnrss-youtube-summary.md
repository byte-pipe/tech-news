# - YouTube

## YouTube Overview

### Key Points:

* YouTube is a video-sharing platform owned by Google LLC.
* It allows users to upload, share, and view videos with the help of external software and APIs.
* The platform uses artificial intelligence-powered recommendations for discovering new content.

### Structure and Functionality:

* YouTube provides various features such as search, browse, and watch individual videos.
* Users can subscribe to channels in their preferred niche, follow personal friends from their subscriptions, or simply watch recommended videos based on their video history.
* Creators, also known as publishers, can monetize their content through advertisements displayed during videos before they start playing.

### Terms of Service and Policies:

* YouTube has strict policies for user-generated content, copyright infringement, and harassment or abuse in the comments section.
* Terms and conditions are enforced to ensure a smooth use experience on the platform.

## How YouTube Works

### Test New Features

* YouTube allows users to try out new features before they roll out globally through beta testing phases.
* Participation is entirely voluntary; user feedback helps refine and improve existing functionalities.



## Additional Policies:

* The safety of all content uploaded to YouTube, including copyright infringement and harassment, must be respected by all users.
* YouTube has its own set of terms and conditions that outline the expectations for how content can be shared, viewed, or interacted with on the platform.
