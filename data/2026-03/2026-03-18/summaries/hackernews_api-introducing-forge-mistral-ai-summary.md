# Introducing Forge | Mistral AI

Mistral Forge
================

Overview
--------

Forge is a system for enterprises to build frontier-grade Artificial Intelligence (AI) models grounded in their proprietary knowledge. It bridges the gap between generic AI and enterprise-specific needs by integrating internal knowledge embedded within systems, workflows, and policies.

Key Features
-------------

* Trains models on institutional knowledge: Enterprises can train models on large volumes of internal documentation, codebases, structured data, and operational records.
* Domain-aware modeling: Organizations build models that reason using internal terminology and understand enterprise workflows.
* Pre-training and post-training methods: Allows teams to develop models and agents that are tailored to specific tasks and environments.
* Reinforcement learning: Enables organizations to align models with internal policies, evaluation criteria, and operational objectives.

Benefits
--------

* Retain control over knowledge encoding and AI systems: Enterprises can govern how their knowledge is used by AI systems.
* Develop models that reflect institutional intelligence: Models remain under the organization's control, ensuring compliance with regulations and internal governance frameworks.

## Training Models on Institutional Knowledge

Forge enables organizations to build models that internalize their domain knowledge by training them on large volumes of internal documentation, codebases, structured data, and operational records.

**Domain-Aware Modeling**

Models are developed to reason using internal terminology and understand enterprise workflows. This allows teams to develop models and agents that are tailored to specific tasks and environments.

**Stage 1: Pre-training**

Organizations build domain-aware models by learning from large internal datasets.

* Stage 2: Post-training methods

	+ Reinforce alignment with internal policies, evaluation criteria, and operational objectives.
