# Quantum Leap for Computing: The Turing Award Recipients
=====================================================

The development of quantum computing is poised to revolutionize various fields including computer science, physics, and finance.

## Meet the Founders

- **Charles Bennett** and **Gilles Brassard**: Two scientists who embarked on a groundbreaking journey in 1979 when they met in the Atlantic Ocean off Puerto Rico.
- Their conversation inspired a body of work that created quantum information theory and transformed the field of computer science.

### Key Insights

- **Disconnection between Information Science and Physics**: In the early years, it was believed that understanding quantum mechanics required delving into classical physics, but this is no longer the case for quantum computing.
- **Early Advancements in Quantum Mechanics**: Bennetts and Brassards discovered methods like quantum coin-tossing and entanglement to leverage the perceived limitations of quantum reality as a powerful tool.
- **Quantum Cryptography**: They explored using quantum mechanics to create affordable digital money that cannot be counterfeited.

## Implications

These early pioneers have played a significant role in bridging the gap between information science and physics, paving the way for the development of advanced computational technologies like quantum computing.
