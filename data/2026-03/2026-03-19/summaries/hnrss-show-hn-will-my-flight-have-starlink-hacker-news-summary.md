*   **In-flight options:** Only a few airlines offer in-flight access to Starlink. These include:
    *   JSX E145s (all)
    *   Air France A320s (no)
*   **Flight search tool:** Build on top of a database containing information on all airlines with Starlink and their aircraft types.
*   **How it works:**
    1.  Check if the airline and aircraft type have Starlink.
    2.  Look up the tail number to determine if an assigned aircraft has Starlink enabled (the tail number is usually only assigned days in advance).
    3.  If not, check if the exact plane assignment matches.

**Frequency of Availability:**

Starlink's availability on flights is patchy and hard to predict, with most airlines only offering a partial upgrade when upgrading from their previous standard equipment.
