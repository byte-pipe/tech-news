## 'You can't smell Nowruz in the air': Iran marks Persian new year under threat of strikes

# Overview
Iran's Nowruz celebration is traditionally marked by preparation and joy, but this year has been marred by war and displacement. The celebrations have taken a different tone, with many people fearing for their safety and future.

### Key Points

* **Warfare disrupting festivities**: Iran has been under US-Israeli strikes since February 28th, causing disruption to Nowruz celebrations.
* **Human rights concerns**: 3,114 people are estimated to have been killed in the conflict, including at least 207 civilians, with many more injured.
* **Displacement and displacement of celebrations**: Many people have fled Tehran due to the war, limiting visits to family homes.
* **Traditions affected by uncertainty**: Despite efforts to maintain traditions, some communities are expressing concern over the possibility of their memories being tainted by violence.
