## Amazon Expected to Move Prime Day Sale to June

Amazon is planning to move its iconic Prime Day sale from July to late June as part of a rare change to its traditional timing schedule. This shift would likely impact thousands of third-party sellers who rely on the event to attract customers and clear inventory.

### Key Points:

* Amazon plans to hold the Prime Day sale in late June instead of July
* The move is expected to affect thousands of third-party sellers across US retailers
* Prime Day has become one of Amazon's most significant commercial events, driving online spending and driving traffic on its marketplace
* Timing adjustment may alter how quarterly revenue patterns appear in financial results
* Last year's Prime Day saw 30% year-on-year rise in online spending to $24.1 billion (Source: Amazon)
