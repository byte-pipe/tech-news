# How to Stay Off Your Phone: Creative Strategies

Stay off your phone with these creative strategies:

*   **Life Kit:** Keep your phone in a different room or outside the bedroom
*   **Practical Tools:** Download blockers like Freedom, SelfControl, or StayFocusd to limit app usage
*   **Social Connections:** Host a dinner party and ask guests to check in at their own pace
