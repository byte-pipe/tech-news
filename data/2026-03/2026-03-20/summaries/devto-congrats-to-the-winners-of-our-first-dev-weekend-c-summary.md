# Congrats to the Winners of Our First DEV Weekend Challenge! - DEV Community

**DEV Weekend Challenge Winners Summary**

* Winners:
	+ TerraRun: built a running app to capture territory through a simple question, where users claim their "territory" based on the closed loop of each run.
	+ UTMACH Rides: created a mobile-first PWA for students at Universidad Técnica de Machala in Ecuador, handling coordination flow and safety features such as email verification.
	+ BagichaLink: developed an AI-powered plant swap community app that uses care tips to identify plants, allows for direct handoff of matches.

**Overview**

The DEV Weekend Challenge aimed to encourage participants to build real, functional apps related to their communities. The winners' projects demonstrated creativity and innovation in implementing innovative features and solutions to address community needs.

**Key Points**

* The challenge focused on building something with a clear connection to the participants' communities.
* Three winners were selected from a pool of submissions that met the prompt's simplicity yet delivered real, functional work.
* Participants received a $300 USD prize for participating, an exclusive DEV Badge, andDEV++ Membership certificates.

**Prizes**

Individual winners will receive:
- $300 USD
- Exclusive DEV Badge
- DeVantage+ Membership

The community was also invited to participate in the Notion MCP Challenge and other writing and frontend art projects through 2026's WeCoded Challenge.
