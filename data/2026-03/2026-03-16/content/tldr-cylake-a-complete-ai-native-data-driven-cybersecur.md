## A complete, AI-native, data-driven cybersecurity platform that doesn’t depend on the public cloud

Some of the world’s largest organizations cannot, for security concerns or regulatory purposes, depend on products that rely on the public cloud. They’re left either stitching together a patchwork of on-premises or private cloud security products—difficult to integrate and often lacking full visibility into the technical stack—or else segmenting out select, incomplete data to send to a product based in the public cloud. For organizations operating at scale and confronting today’s AI-led attacks, both of those options are insufficient and create exploitable vulnerabilities.

## Cybersecurity platform for regulated industries.

About us

We are developing a complete, AI-native, data-driven cybersecurity platform built on a single data foundation, ensuring operational sovereignty for our customers. Designed to work either on-premises or in a private cloud, we will serve organizations where visibility, control, and cybersecurity are essential. Our platform combines hardware and software components to provide an end-to-end cybersecurity system.

Rooted in a full picture of an organization’s data and operations, Cylake’s system will deliver deep analysis and complete agentic protection—without relying on public cloud or public AI infrastructure.
