# Crypto Code Commits Fall 75% as Developers Move to AI Projects

## Major Shift in Blockchain Dev Talent and Focus

*   Developer activity has decreased sharply across blockchain projects since early 2025.
*   Weekly crypto code commits have dropped around 75%, with a corresponding decline of active developers (from 850,000 to 210,000).
*   However, overall GitHub usage grows as more companies adopt artificial intelligence infrastructure.

## Development Trend Shift Toward AI

*   Artificial intelligence repositories, large language model projects, and tools like Jupyter Notebooks are experiencing rapid growth.
*   Developers are shifting their focus away from blockchain ecosystems and towards AI and machine learning areas.

## Industry Response to Decline in Blockchain Dev Talent

*   Major chains experiencing developer losses, with more experienced contributors dominating the majority of commits (from 56% to around 4.6K).
*   Competition from AI may contribute to a consolidation of the ecosystem rather than a complete collapse among blockchain projects.
