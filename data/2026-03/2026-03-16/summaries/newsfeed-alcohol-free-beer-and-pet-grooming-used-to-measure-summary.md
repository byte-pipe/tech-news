# Alcohol-free beer and pet grooming used to measure inflation

## Alcohol-free beer and Pet Grooming Added to Inflation Index

The Office for National Statistics (ONS) has made changes to the way inflation is calculated, incorporating alcohol-free beer and pet grooming into its virtual basket of goods and services. This shift reflects a changing pattern of consumer spending habits.

### Main Points:

*   Alcohol-free beer and houmous have been added to the virtual basket.
*   Motorhomes, dashboard cameras, and pet grooming are among items reviewed annually.
*   The ONS now collects prices from 760 products across retailers to gauge cost inflation accurately.
*   A new method for collecting data using supermarket scanner technology has been implemented.
