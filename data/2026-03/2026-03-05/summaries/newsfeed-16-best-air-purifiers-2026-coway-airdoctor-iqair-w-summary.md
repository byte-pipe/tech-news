## How to Shop for an Air Purifier (Key Considerations)
- Determine ideal room size and required air‑exchange rate (5 × hour recommended).
- Choose filter type: HEPA for particles, activated carbon for gases/odors.
- Evaluate noise level, especially for bedrooms or workspaces.
- Look for smart features (app, remote, auto mode) if convenience is a priority.
- Check warranty length and availability of replacement filters.

## How to Check Your Air Quality
- Use inexpensive handheld AQI meters or smartphone‑compatible sensors.
- Monitor particulate matter (PM2.5), VOCs, and humidity to gauge purifier effectiveness.

## Testing Methodology (WIRED)

## Not Recommended Models (Brief)

- Models with extremely short warranties relative to price.
