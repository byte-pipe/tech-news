# Apple's new iPhone 17e has an A19 chip, MagSafe, and 256GB of storage for $599 - Ars Technica

## iPhone 16e (Previous Version)
### Key Points:

* The iPhone 17e is a new version of Apple's basic no-frills iPhone, replacing the iPhone 16e.
* It will be available for preorder on March 4 and will be available on March 11, starting at $599.
* The new iPhone includes a new Apple A19 chip, a Ceramic Shield 2 front glass, and Apple Intelligence with 8GB of RAM.
* The phone supports MagSafe charging, which was previously a notable omission, and an upgrade from 128GB to 256GB of storage.

## iPhone 17e (New Version)
### Key Points:

* The iPhone 17e supports MagSafe charging and an upgrade from 128GB to 256GB of storage in the base model.
* It supports improved cameras, a wider-angle lens, and a Dynamic Island with ProMotion.
* The phone has a similar design to the iPhone 17, with a 6.1-inch display, USB-C port, and a 48 megapixel rear camera.
* The iPhone 17e is more appealing than the $699 iPhone 16e, but it's still $200 cheaper.

## iPhone 17e vs. iPhone 16e
### Key Points:

* The iPhone 17e has improved cameras, a wider-angle lens, and ProMotion support, but lacks ProMotion display and 128GB of storage.
* The iPhone 17e is more attractive due to the MagSafe upgrade and 256GB storage upgrade, but it's still $200 cheaper.

## Overall Summary
* The iPhone 17e is a new version of the basic iPhone, with improved cameras, a wider-angle lens, and ProMotion support compared to the $699 iPhone 16e.
* The phone supports MagSafe charging and an upgrade from 128GB to 256GB of storage, making it more appealing than the $699 iPhone 16e.
* The iPhone 17e is still cheaper than the iPhone 16e, but with better cameras and a wider-angle lens.
