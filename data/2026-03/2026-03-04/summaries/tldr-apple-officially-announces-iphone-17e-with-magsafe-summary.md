# Apple officially announces iPhone 17e with MagSafe, 2x base storage, 'soft pink' color - 9to5Mac

# iPhone 17e

* Apple has announced the new iPhone 17e, featuring MagSafe wireless charging technology, 2x base storage, and a "soft pink" color option.
* The iPhone 17e starts at the same price as its predecessor, offering twice the storage, starting at $599 for 256GB storage.
* It also offers a higher capacity option of 512GB for $799.
* The new iPhone 17e features the A19 chip, which provides exceptional performance.
* It includes the C1X Apple modem, similar to the iPhone Air (16e), which offers faster wireless connectivity.
* The 48MP Fusion camera captures high-quality photos, including portraits and videos with up to 2x optical zoom.
* iPhone 17e also supports 4K Dolby Vision video recording.
* The iPhone 17e starts pre-orders on March 4 at 6:15 am PT and reaches stores later, on March 11.

## Key Features and Specifications

* 2x base storage (256GB or 512GB)
* MagSafe wireless charging technology
* "Soft pink" color option
* A19 chip with C1X Apple modem
* 48MP Fusion camera with 4K Dolby Vision video recording
* Supports 2x optical zoom
