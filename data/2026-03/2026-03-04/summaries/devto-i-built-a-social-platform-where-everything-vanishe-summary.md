# i built a social platform where everything vanishes after 24 hours - DEV Community

## Genjutsu Social Platform

### Overview

Genjutsu is a fast-paced, developer-focused social network where everything vanishes every 24 hours. It's a temporary space for ephemeral thoughts, offering a clean and high-contrast visual style inspired by Japanese manga. With a focus on developer first design, real-time connection, and minimal clutter, Genjutsu aims to provide a space for experimentation, raw authenticity, and meaningful interactions.

### Key Features

* **24-hour lifespan**: Prolonged posts, comments, and interactions with a strict 24-hour timer.
* **Developer focus**: Optimized for sharing code and technical insights.
* **Real-time connection**: Built for instant engagement with a community of builders.
* **Manga aesthetic**: A unique visual style inspired by Japanese manga.
* **Zero clutter**: A clean, fresh feed for every day, without a permanent history.

### Why Genjutsu

* Designed to address performance anxiety and the pressure of being "on the record" for long periods.
* Offers a safe space for experimentation, raw contributions, and authentic interactions.

### Tech Stack

* **Frontend**: React with Vite for fast and lightweight functionality.
* **Styling**: Tailwind CSS for a crisp, manga-inspired look.
* **Animations**: Framer Motion for smooth and engaging transitions.
* **Backend**: Supabase for real-time operations.
* **Deployment**: Vercel for seamless and automated deployment.

### Roadmap

* **Ephemeral messaging**: Whisper-like functionality that vanishes after 24 hours.
* **Social mini-games**: Opportunities for interactive engagement and friendly competition.
* **Social sharing tools**: Dynamic open graph images and progressive web app (PWA) support.

### Development and Participation

* The Genjutsu community is actively engaged and contributes to the platform's development.
* Open-source platform, open to user feedback and suggestions.
