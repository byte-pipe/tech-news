## Overview
- MagSafe wallets attach to an iPhone (or MagSafe‑compatible case) for a slim, compact way to carry a few cards.
- Ideal for travelers or anyone who wants to carry only 2‑3 cards instead of a full wallet.

## What is MagSafe?
- Apple’s magnetic system built into iPhone 12‑17 series (excluding iPhone 16e) and iPhone Air.
- Provides fast wireless charging and a ring of magnets for accessories such as cases, chargers, and wallets.
- A MagSafe‑compatible case is required for a strong connection; non‑MagSafe cases cause a weak hold and may let the wallet slide off.

## Device Compatibility
- iPhones with MagSafe: iPhone 12, 12 Mini, 12 Pro, 12 Pro Max, 13 series, 14 series, 15 series, 16 series, 17 series, and iPhone Air.
- Android phones can use MagSafe wallets only with magnetic cases that include a MagSafe‑style ring of magnets (e.g., Google Pixel 10 series).

## Safety Note
- MagSafe does not demagnetize credit cards; all wallets in the guide include a built‑in shield.
- Hotel key cards and some gift cards may be vulnerable and should be kept elsewhere.

## Testing Methodology
- Used each wallet daily as a primary wallet.
- Checked that the wallet snaps firmly to the phone or case and stays attached during normal movement.
- Verified card fit, ease of removal, durability, and that the wallet holds the typical 2‑3 cards comfortably.
