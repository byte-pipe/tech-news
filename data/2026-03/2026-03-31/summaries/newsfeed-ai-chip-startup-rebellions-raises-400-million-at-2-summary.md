# AI chip startup Rebellions raises $400 million at $2.3 B valuation in pre‑IPO round  

## Funding overview  
- Raised an additional $400 million in a pre‑IPO round led by Mirae Asset Financial Group and the Korea National Growth Fund.  
- Total fundraising now $850 million, with $650 million secured in the last six months (Series B $124 M in 2024, Series C $250 M in November 2023).  
- Company valuation reported at approximately $2.34 billion.  

## Product announcements  
- Launched **RebelRack** and **RebelPOD**, AI infrastructure platforms for inference workloads.  
  - **RebelPOD**: production‑ready inference compute unit.  
  - **RebelRack**: scalable cluster that integrates multiple pods for large‑scale AI deployment.  

## Global expansion efforts  
- Established entities in the United States, Japan, Saudi Arabia, and Taiwan.  
- Building a technology‑partner ecosystem in the U.S. targeting cloud providers, government agencies, telecom operators, and “Neoclouds.”  
- Plans to grow presence across Asia, the Middle East, and the United States ahead of the IPO.  

## Market positioning  
- Focuses on inference‑optimized chips, a segment gaining importance as large language models move to commercial use.  
- Aims to shift AI performance metrics toward real‑world scalability, power efficiency, and economic return.  
- Positions itself as part of a new generation of chip startups challenging NVIDIA’s historic dominance, alongside efforts from AWS, Meta, and Google.  

## Executive comments  
- CBO Marshall Choy leads global expansion and declined to comment on IPO timing.  
- CEO Sunghyun Park emphasized the shift toward inference infrastructure and usable software as the new center of gravity for AI.
