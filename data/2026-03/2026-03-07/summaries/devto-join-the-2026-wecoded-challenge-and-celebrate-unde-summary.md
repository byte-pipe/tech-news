# Join the 2026 WeCoded Challenge and Celebrate Underrepresented Voices in Tech

## Overview
- We are launching the 2026 WeCoded Challenge, marking the ninth year of highlighting under‑represented and marginalized developers on DEV.  
- Originated in 2017 as “Nevertheless, She Coded,” evolved to WeCoded in 2023, and introduced the first official challenge last year.  
- This year combines reflective writing with a new code‑based visual expression track.

## Prompts
### Echoes of Experience (Writing)
- Share personal journeys, challenges, triumphs, or observations from the past year.  
- Aim to empower peers or educate allies about inclusion.  
- Use the provided “Echoes of Experience Submission Template.”

### Frontend Art: Gender Equity (Code & Visual)
- Create a frontend artwork that represents gender equity in tech (e.g., shattered glass ceiling, pioneer portrait, abstract community strength).  
- Any frontend tools are allowed.  
- Use the “Frontend Art Submission Template.”

## Prizes
- Winners receive an exclusive DEV badge and a DEV++ Membership.  
- Each prompt will have at least one winner (more possible).  
- All valid submissions earn a completion badge.

## Judging Criteria
- **Echoes of Experience:** style & presentation, clarity, impact, originality.  
- **Frontend Art:** creativity, effective use of frontend technology, aesthetic outcome.

## How to Participate
- Publish a post on DEV using the submission template for the chosen prompt.  
- Review the full rules, guidelines, and FAQ to confirm eligibility and contest requirements.

## Important Dates
- **March 6:** WeCoded 2026 begins.  
- **April 5:** Submissions due by 11:59 PM PDT.  
- **April 16:** Winners announced.  

We look forward to celebrating your voice and your code!
