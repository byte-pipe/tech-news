## Frequently asked questions

### Do I need to be an Optimizely customer?

While Opal is built by Optimizely and our customers get priority access, this cohort is open to any senior marketer. You'll learn principles and techniques that work with any AI platform.

### What is the time commitment?

The cohort runs for 5 days, with live sessions daily from 1-2pm ET. We recommend setting aside an additional hour for building your agents. Total: 2 hours/day for 5 days.

### Will I actually save 10+ hours per week?

That's the average from our 2,500+ graduates. Some save less (5-7 hours), some save way more (100+ hours). It depends on what you build and how repetitive your current tasks are. But yes, you'll save real time starting the week after the cohort.

### Is this really free?

Yes. 100% free. No credit card required. No upsells. We're making a bet that if we teach you to build great AI agents, you'll see the value of the Optimizely platform. That's it.

### Do I need to be technical?

Nope. If you can write an email, you can build an AI agent. No coding required. We've trained CMOs, content directors, and marketing managers who've never written a line of code.

### What happens after I graduate?

You keep your agents, get free Opal credits to keep building, join our graduate community with weekly office hours, and get early access to new features. Plus, we'll celebrate you on LinkedIn when you ship.

### What if I can't make all 5 sessions?

Recordings are available, but the real magic happens live. You'll miss the peer learning, live feedback, and "aha moments" from seeing others build. We recommend blocking your calendar, most students say it's the best 5 hours they've spent all quarter.
