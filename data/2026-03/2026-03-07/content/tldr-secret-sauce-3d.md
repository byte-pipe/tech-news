### Frequentlyaskedquestions

Who is this for?

Secret Sauce 3D is for professional 3D artists working in games, film, e-commerce, and beyond. Our AI tools are designed to feel native to industry-standard pipelines, helping you accelerate production without disrupting your process.

How much time will this save me?

For Game-Ready realistic assets, you can generate usable models ready for direct Unreal Engine integration—saving the full time it would take to build a prop manually.

Within a traditional SS3D pipeline, this can mean several days saved from high-poly blockout to an optimized mesh, often with a strong UV starting point. Exact savings vary depending on your workflow and project complexity.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 3-day trial for our Creator plan, with full access to all features so you can explore the complete workflow.

Can I easily cancel my plan?

Yes. You can cancel your plan at any time from your account settings.

Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you’ll continue to have full access to the app until then. After that, your subscription won’t renew.

If you run into any issues, our team is happy to help.

Is it easy to use?

Yes. SS3D is designed as a 3D copilot that assists you from concept to production-ready asset.

You can refine your 2D concept, generate a high-poly sculpt, run retopology, create UVs, and generate textures — all within a chat-style interface. Simply describe the edits you want or choose from pre-built prompts.

It’s built to feel intuitive for 3D artists and works alongside your existing DCC tools, so you can export and continue refining as needed.
