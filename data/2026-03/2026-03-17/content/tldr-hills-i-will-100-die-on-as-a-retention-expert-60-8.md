description: 'Hills I will 100% die on as a retention expert: ▶︎ 60-80% of churn happens in the first 4 months. If your in-house team or retention agency hasn''t created a post-purchase system that addresses that window specifically, you''re losing at least $1,000,000 per month (likely more) due to subpar retention. ▶︎ Slapping "Subscribe & Save 10%" is not a retention strategy. It''s a badly thought-out discount strategy. Call a spade a spade. ▶︎ Brands can hit 45-55% 30-Day Repurchase Rate and 12% 12-Month Retention Rate on product quality ALONE, and still struggle with their exit valuation because they haven''t reached their true LTV potential. ▶︎ If you want to grow your market share, your ideal LTV:CAC ratio is 3.5-4. (Less than 3, you''re getting swallowed – more than 4, you''re spending too little on CAC.) ▶︎ If your Payback Period is 90+ days and your LTV:CAC is below 3, there''s 0% CHANCE you''ll scale your brand. The math won''t allow it. ▶︎ Ghosting subscribers doesn''t protect
  you from cancellations. It creates "accidental renewals,” AKA angry customers who forgot they were subscribed, get charged, and immediately cancel. (Followed by refunds/chargebacks/bad reviews/BBB complaints...) ▶︎ Just because you send a whole lot of emails, it doesn''t mean you "do retention". "Retention agencies", looking at you. Josh Tay / Jordan Narducci / 🇺🇦 Andriy Rudnyk / Sonja Grasser, MBA / Matthew Holman / Bryan Starck – tell me if there’s anything you think I’ve missed. | 45 comments on LinkedIn'
