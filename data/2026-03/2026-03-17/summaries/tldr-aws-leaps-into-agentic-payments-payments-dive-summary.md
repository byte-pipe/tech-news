# AWS leaps into agentic payments | Payments Dive

**AWS Leaps into Agentic Payments with New Initiatives**

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is investing heavily in agentic payments, aiming to create seamless experiences where customers can use artificial intelligence to shop without human intervention. This shift marks a significant move towards achieving frictionless consumer experiences through AI-powered agents.

**Key Points:**

* AWS and Visa are collaborating to develop tools for building "network agnostic agentic workflows" for the payments aspect of AI commerce.
* The goal is to provide a secure, scalable foundation for intelligent commerce solutions.
* Payments execution layer remains crucial but important; Amazon focuses on making it easier for customers to integrate agents into existing payment rails.

**AWS' Approach:**

* The platform offers choices in model providers and partners for an agent platform.
* AWS will restrict restricted training data to prevent model misuse.
* Customers can select from multiple models, including OpenAI and Anthropic models.

**AWS' Vision:**
The company aims to create widespread adoption of agentic commerce by making it more accessible, scalable, and secure. This effort showcases Amazon's commitment to pushing the boundaries of AI-powered services and exploring new ways to integrate technology into daily life.
