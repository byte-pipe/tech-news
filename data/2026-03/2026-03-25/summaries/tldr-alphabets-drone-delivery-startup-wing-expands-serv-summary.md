# Alphabet’s drone delivery startup, Wing, expands service to the Bay Area

## Key Points
- Wing, Alphabet’s “Other Bet,” announced that its ultra‑fast residential drone delivery service will launch in the Bay Area, joining Alphabet’s autonomous ride‑hailing unit Waymo in the region.
- The company currently operates in limited zones of Atlanta, Charlotte, Houston, and Dallas‑Fort Worth, having completed over 750,000 deliveries that serve more than 2 million people.
- Customers can request drone deliveries through partner apps such as Walmart and DoorDash, as well as directly via Wing’s own marketplace.
- Alphabet recently linked its CEO’s compensation to the growth in value of businesses like Wing, providing a financial incentive for rapid expansion.
