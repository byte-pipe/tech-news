# Bafta TV Awards 2026 – Double Nominations for Aimee Lou Wood and Erin Doherty

## Main highlights
- Aimee Lou Wood receives two nominations:
  - Best Actress for **Film Club** (BBC Three) – a comedy‑drama about a young woman with agoraphobia.
  - Best Supporting Actress for **White Lotus** – playing a fun‑loving yoga teacher.
- Erin Doherty also secures two nominations:
  - Best Actress for **A Thousand Blows** (Disney+) – portraying gang leader Mary Carr.
  - Best Supporting Actress for **Adolescence** (Netflix) – as a psychologist.

## Leading programmes
- **Adolescence** – most‑nominated show with 11 nods, including:
  - Best Limited Drama.
  - Five acting nominations (Erin Doherty, Stephen Graham, Christine Tremarco, Ashley Walters, Owen Cooper).
  - Stephen Graham nominated for Best Actor (distressed dad).
- **A Thousand Blows** – seven nominations, also shortlisted for Best Drama.
- **The Celebrity Traitors** – most nominations in entertainment categories, including Best Reality Show and Best Entertainment Performance (host Claudia Winkleman).

## Voting, juries, and ceremony details
- The “Memorable Moment” award is the only category decided by public voting.
- Nearly 40 industry juries select the majority of television and craft category nominees and winners.
- The BAFTA Television Awards ceremony will be held on 10 May at London’s Southbank Centre.
