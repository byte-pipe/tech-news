# Nowruz is different amid war, even in the Iranian diaspora : NPR

**Summary**

Nowruz, the Iranian New Year, is being observed differently in the Iranian diaspora due to ongoing war. Many Iranians are struggling with grief and anxiety, while others are using the holiday as a chance to come together and process their emotions.

In contrast, some in the diaspora have chosen to tone down traditional celebrations to cope with the current circumstances. However, many organizations were able to continue hosting events despite the lack of participation.

The Iranian diaspora community is embracing Nowruz as an opportunity for unity and resistance against censorship. A new dance party called Disco Tehran has gained popularity, celebrating the music of Iran and encouraging people to "be silent against darkness."
