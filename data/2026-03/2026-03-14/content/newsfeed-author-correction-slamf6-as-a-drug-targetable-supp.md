Correction to:Naturehttps://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-026-10106-5Published online 11 February 2026

In the version of this article initially published, in the first paragraph of the Discussion, citation of a recent report showing that aberrant expression of SLAMF6 on human acute myelogenous leukemia cells suppressed T cell activation, an effect that was prevented by a blocking SLAMF6 mAb (Sandén, C. et al.Nat. Cancerhttps://doi.org/10.1038/s43018-025-01054-6(2026)), was missing and is now included in the HTML and PDF versions of the article.
