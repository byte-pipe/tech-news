# 6 worthwhile conferences for women in tech

Feature

Mar 12, 2026
5 mins
 

## For women seeking to connect and advance their IT careers, or those who support diversity and inclusion in technology fields, here are six conferences you won’t want to miss this year.

 

							Credit: 															Matej Kastelic / Shutterstock													

Women are the minority at all major tech companies, most notably at Amazon (45%), Meta (37%), Apple (35%), Google (34%), and Microsoft (32%), and those numbers drop even more when narrowed down for leadership roles, according todata from the Women in Tech network. At Google, women only comprise 29% of leadership positions, and nearly 37% at Meta. Apple, Google, and Microsoft report similar numbers at 31%, 33%, and 32%, respectively. In the industry overall, women only represent 25% of C-level leadership roles, and only 5% of those are BIPOC women.

Women also continue to report workplace culture issues, with many reporting they continue to experience microaggressions such as being spoken over in meetings, feeling pigeonholed by stereotypes, and being asked to do menial tasks such as bring food for meetings or other things unrelated to their roles.

As representation of women in tech continues to struggle, it’s critical to have avenues where women can build and foster community with others who understand what it’s like to make a career in tech.

Whether you already have a strong network of women colleagues in your industry or if you’re looking to expand your community, there are several conferences designed for women in IT, but most aren’t just for women — they’re open to allies and anyone who supports diversity in the workplace. So here are six that aim to connect women and other underrepresented groups in technology to build better communities in the tech industry.

### Simmons Leadership Conference

The annualSimmons Leadership Conferenceis a professional development and networking conference for women hosted by Simmons College, which has featured high-profile speakers such as Serena Williams, Michelle Obama, Madeline Albright, Anita Hill, Oprah Winfrey, and Barbara Walters. The conference focuses on uplifting women in leadership positions and empowering more women to seek out leadership positions, especially in industries like technology where they’re underrepresented.

Date: March 17, 2026

Location: Boston, MA

### Elevate 2026

The 9thannualElevate conferenceis a virtual event that celebrates World Creativity & Innovation Day, with a focus on tech leadership. The event includes speaker panels, networking events, and first-hand accounts from other women leaders on the challenges they’ve faced climbing the ladder in a male-dominated industry. This year’s event will include several talks on the impact of AI in engineering, cybersecurity, and career growth.

Date: April 21, 2026

Location: Online

### Women in Tech Global Summit

TheWomen in Tech Global Summitis an annual gathering dedicated to advancing women and girls in STEM fields, billing itself as the definitive platform where policy meets practice, vision becomes action, and global leaders commit to measurable change. The event provides opportunities for participants to network, impact global policy, and recognize and celebrate women in tech. The event includes speakers, career advice, workshops for professional development, networking opportunities, and a focus on improving DEI in tech.

Date: April 28-30, 2026

Location: Cape Town, South-Africa

### Women in Tech Global Conference

TheWomen in Tech Global Conferenceis an event for software engineers, product managers, designers, data scientists, and business managers to connect, collaborate, and grow. Each day of the conference has a different focus. Day one is about leadership and digital transformation, the second focuses on emerging and future technology, and the last day centers on career growth and professional development. The conference aims to bring women together globally, create a sense of community, and empower women in tech.

Date: May 12-15, 2026

Location: Virtual with in-person satellite events.

### LWTSQUAD Summit

Hosted by Lesbians Who Tech, theLWTSQUAD Summitis a combination of both a summit and leadership program, with practical hands-on workshops for participants and networking events to connect with senior executives. The event includes speakers, curated experiences and programming, networking mixers, and leadership content specifically designed for senior leaders. The leadership program allows attendees to hear personal experiences from executives, and make connections to help boost career opportunities.

Date: October 5-7, 2026

Location: New York, NY

### Grace Hopper Celebration

TheGrace Hopper Celebrationwas co-founded by Dr. Anita Borg and Dr. Telle Whitney in 1994, and claims to be the world’s largest gathering of women technologists. The conference is named after Admiral Grace Murray Hopper, who’s considered among the first computer programmers, and whose work is directly responsible for the development of COBOL. The Grace Hopper Celebration is a conference that spans several topic areas, including technology, culture, career transitions, working in the C-suite, emerging and trending technologies, and new workplace trends in tech.

Date: October 27-30, 2026

Location: Anaheim, CA
