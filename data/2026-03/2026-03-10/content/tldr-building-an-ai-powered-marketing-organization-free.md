# Building an AI-Powered Marketing Organization

March 12, 2026

2-3 p.m. ET

## The new essential CMO operating model

Join us on March 12 from 2 - 3 pm ET as Carta’s CMO, Nicole Baer, shares how she’s rewiring her marketing team’s structure, habits, and tools around AI. We’ll cover questions like:

* How did you get your team to build the habit of using AI - and for real value, not just basic tasks?
* How do you staff AI transformation on your team?
* How do you govern all the AI initiatives on your team - who decides what’s worth doing and whether it’s worth scaling?
* Looking 1-2 years out, how do you think your marketing team - and marketing teams in general - will look different?

She’s taking your questions too, so don’t miss the opportunity to get some free strategy.
