# Apple Music in iOS 26.4 – New Design for Albums, Playlists, and More  

## Main updates  
- iOS 26.4 introduces a fullscreen redesign for album and playlist pages in Apple Music.  
- The track‑list background now adopts a color that complements the cover art, creating a more cohesive visual experience.  

## Profile screen refresh  
- Tapping the profile image opens a revamped Profile screen inside Music, matching updates seen in Podcasts and TV apps.  
- The screen provides shortcuts for Apple Account actions such as adding funds, redeeming gift cards, or entering app codes.  

## Subscription details  
- Apple Music remains priced at $10.99 per month for individuals.  
- It is also available through the Apple One bundle.
