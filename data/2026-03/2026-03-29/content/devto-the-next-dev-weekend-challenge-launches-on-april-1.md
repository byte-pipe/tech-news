We're back with another installment of our newest format: theDEV Weekend Challenge!

If you missed the first one, these are short-form, high-energy challenges designed to fit right into your weekend. We’re giving you the heads-up now so you can clear your schedule!

## How It Works

Our challenge prompt will be revealed at launch.You can keep an eye on theDEV Weekend Challenge pageor look out for the official announcement post from theDEV Team. From there, you'll have the entire weekend to build, document, and submit your project. That's all there is to it!

Because our community spans every timezone on the planet, we've set the window so that everyone around the world gets at least a full weekend to participate.

## Important Dates

* Launch Time:Friday, April 16 at 2:00 AM UTC
* Submissions Due:Monday, April 20 at 6:59 AM UTC
