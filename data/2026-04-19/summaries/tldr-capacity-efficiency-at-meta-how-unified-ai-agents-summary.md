# Capacity Efficiency at Meta: How Unified AI Agents Optimize Performance at Hyperscale - Engineering at Meta

**Capacity Efficiency at Meta: Optimizing Performance through Unified AI Agents**

By Tommy Tran and Michael Zetune
===========================================================

Meta's Capacity Efficiency Program leverages an AI-agency platform to automate performance issues throughout the organization. This program aims to optimize infrastructure efficiency by finding and fixing defects, recovering power, and compressing manual regression investigation time.

**Key Highlights:**

* **Unified AI Agent Platform**: Encodes domain expertise from senior engineers into reusable, composable skills that automate both offense (finding opportunities) and defense (detecting regressions).
* **Efficiency Across Growing Product Areas**: Automated solutions have reduced waste by hundreds of megawatts, allowing the program to scale with growth without proportionally hiring more staff.
* **Faster Time-to-Resolution**: Faster automated resolution reduces manual regression investigation time, compressing it from hours to minutes.

**The Roadmap:**

* **Offense Proactively Finds Optimations**: Enhances offshse performance capabilities
* **Defensenatsively Detects and Mitigates Regressions**: Automates detection, root-cause analysis, and deploy mitigation code

By building a unified platform with standardized tools, Meta's Capacity Efficiency Program has achieved:
+ Recovered hundreds of megawatts of power
+ Saved American homes' electricity for a year
+ Automated diagnoses with ~10 hours of manual investigation compressed to ~30 minutes
+ Integrated human engineering time with automated solutions, reducing reliance on engineers

**The Future: Autonomous Efficiency Engine**
---------------------------------------------

Meta's ultimate goal is a self-sustaining efficiency engine that balances proactive innovation, defensive detection, and efficient resolution through AI-driven systems.
