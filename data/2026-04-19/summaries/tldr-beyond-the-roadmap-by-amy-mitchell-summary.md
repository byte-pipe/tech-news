# Beyond the Roadmap - by Amy Mitchell

# Beyond the Roadmap: A Framework for Moving Product Work Forward

## Key Points

- Builder PMs focus on providing a solution package without formal programs or features.
- Solution packaging is essential, but it only works when teams cannot adopt it because of unclear progress paths and lack of engagement from all relevant stakeholders.
- Temporary working groups formed to collaborate on solution packaging can facilitate progress sharing.

## How to Move Product Work Forward

1.  **Solution Packaging**: Define a repeatable way to solve customer problems without fitting neatly into feature delivery or existing programs.
2.  **Temporary Working Group Formation**: Establish an internal working group consisting of team members closest to the problem for a collaborative solution package development.
3.  **Shared Progress and Understanding**: Implement shared progress goals with the product team, allowing them to experience real value directly.

## Success Factors

-   Clear communication
-   Open feedback mechanisms
-   Flexible project management approach
