# Build to Learn vs Build to Earn - Silicon Valley Product Group : Silicon Valley Product Group

### Build to Learn vs Build to Earn: Navigating Product Team Evolution

#### Key Points:

- The project model has remained largely unchanged despite advances in technology.
- Traditionally, product models revolved around delivering features or projects through stakeholders' prioritization.
- In contrast, the modern product model emphasizes solving customer problems through discovery.

#### Original Perspective:

Although the article takes up most of its content, it maintains a clear narrative and perspective. Understanding product team evolution is crucial for effective product management in today's digital landscape.


### Summary - Bullet Points:

* **Project Model (Prioritization):**
	+ Focuses on delivering features or projects
	+ Features are created based on prioritized plans
	+ Engineers build specifications
	+ Designs are developed to meet those specs

* **Product Model (Solution Development):**
	+ Product leaders and stakeholders identify a customer problem
	+ Products team discovers solutions to solve that problem
	+ Develops prototypes, iterates until desired outcome is achieved

#### Key Differences and Implications for Product Managers:

- The best product managers prioritize both the delivery and discovery of values.
- **Value creation:** While building features is possible with modern tools, what matters is delivering solutions worth creating.

#### Real Challenge(s):

- Uncertainty about contribution roles within product teams
- Struggling to adapt to a new approach as project management's primary responsibility changes.
