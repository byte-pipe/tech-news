# Asus TUF Gaming A14 (2026) Review: GPU-Less Gaming Laptop | WIRED

**Asus TUF Gaming A14 (2026) Review: GPU-Less Gaming Laptop**

* **Design and Build:** The Asus TUF Gaming A14 is a 14-inch compact gaming laptop with an impressive design, feeling similar to the MacBook Pro at a lower price point.
* **Key Features:**
	+ Uses AMD's new Max+ chip instead of discrete graphics cards, offering integrated graphics.
	+ Has a fast and colorful display, responsive keyboard and touchpad, and decent battery life.
* **Performance:** Impressed with gameplay after loading up some titles, but the laptop's performance can't compete with more powerful gaming laptops at this price point. More power would be beneficial.
* **Design and Build Details:**
	+ Compact design with a 16:9 aspect ratio screen, resulting in more bezels and lower screen-to-body ratio.
	+ Trimmed bezels on the sides of the screen due to its 16:9 aspect ratio.
* **Key Considerations**:
	+ The laptop's design may be seen as less "gaming-centric" than previous products from Asus TUF.
	+ The keyboard and touchpad are excellent, making up for the lack of discrete graphics power.
