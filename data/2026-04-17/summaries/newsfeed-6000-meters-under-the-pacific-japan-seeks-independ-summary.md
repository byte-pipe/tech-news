## Minamitorishima: Japan's Quest for Rare Earths
*   **Location and Depth**: Located in the Pacific Ocean, one of the most remote islands in Japan's vast archipelago, situated nearly 2,000 kilometers southeast of Tokyo, at a depth of approximately 6,000 meters.

*   **Importance and Significance**: Rare earth elements are crucial to advanced technologies such as electric vehicles, wind turbines, electronic devices, semiconductors, radar systems, missiles, and more.
