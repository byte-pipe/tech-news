# AI traffic to US retailers rose 393% in Q1, and it's boosting their revenue too | TechCrunch

**AI Traffic to US Retailers Shows Steady Growth and Improved Experience**

As of March 2023, AI-driven traffic on US retailers' websites soared by 393% compared to the previous year, reflecting improved online shopping experiences for customers.

Following a surge in holiday season sales, AI traffic has remained strong, increasing by 693% within a single month. This is a significant turn-around from last year, when AI visitors accounted for only 38% of regular human traffic.

**Key Findings:**

*   39% of respondents use AI for online shopping.
*   85% say AI improves their shopping experience.
*   AI traffic converts better than living customers (42% vs 38%).
*   Engagement rates are 12% higher on AI-driven websites.
*   Shoppers spend more time on the site (48%) and browse more pages (13% per visit).

**Why Retailers Are Optimizing for AI:**

*   Inaccessible content may lead to a loss of top-of-mind customers.
