# Apple Business Launches Free Service to 200+ Countries Today

Apple has officially launched its all-new Apple Business platform globally, starting today. This free service caters to small businesses seeking better management tools for their Apple devices and is expected to be accompanied by millions migrating to the Mac with MacBook Neo. 

**Key Features:**

* Managed Apple accounts that separate private from company data
* Configurable employee and/or group hardware
* Mobile Device Management tools (MDM) to manage settings and security
* Tools to deploy apps and settings using Blueprints and the App Store
* Zero-touch deployment of new hardware
* Brand management tools with logo across various Apple services

**Additional Benefits:**

* Small business customers can manage their devices, including app deployment, and agree to an AppleCare+ for Business package (starting at $6.99 per user/month)
* New local advertising business allows businesses to:
	+ Place paid local discovery ads via their Apple Business account
	+ Create professional communications and online location ads using domain names

**Impact of Local Advertising:**

Apple's new services will enable enterprises verified by Apple Business to bid for ad placements in search results or suggested locations. This offers the option for companies to create and deploy targeted local ads through various Apple services, including Maps, Siri, and Spotlight.

The platform is currently in its second beta of iOS 26.5 and supports ads within the second map beta of iOS 26.5
