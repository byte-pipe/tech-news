# Bank of America Tech Chief Shares AI Strategy Focus

Hari Gopalkrishnan, Chief Technology and Information Officer at Bank of America, outlined the bank's AI strategy as part of the Semafor World Economy 2026 event.

Key Points:

- **AI Journey Advancements**: Bank of America is shifting from task-specific pilot projects to process transformation.
- **Governance Focus**: The bank prioritizes governance and return on investment across its entire tech stack.

Process Transformation (Four Areas):

1.  End-to-end Process Transformation
2.  Scale and Reuse AI Capabilities
3.  Governance and Return on Investment
4.  Generating AI Spending by 40% in 2026

Governance:

Bank of America identifies scaling AI across the enterprise as critical to governance and risk management.
A foundation element for this is building wide, cross-functional capabilities that can be reused repeatedly.

Return on Investment (ROI):

*   Investing heavily in AI
*   Expecting increased generative AI spending by nearly 40% in 2026.
