# 5 Ways Product Discovery Breaks Down (Part 2) - Itamar Gilad

**Understanding the Challenges of Implementing Product Discovery**

The practice of identifying the right things to build before switching to delivery is difficult to implement due to systemic problems within the product organization.

### Key Challenges:

*   **Missing Infrastructure**: The lack of measurement tools, experimentation platforms, user researchers, and data analysts creates challenges for the discovery process.
*   **Slow Release Cycles**: Poorly defined meta-goals leading to slow release rates may result in less responsiveness from the company, dissatisfying customers, and giving agile competitors an edge.

### Solution:

1.  **Prioritization**: Make product discovery a top priority by focusing on key metrics rather than building for output.
2.  **Improvement Goals**: Set explicit goals for infrastructure upgrades, such as doubling launch frequency, to validate ideas using user research and surveys.
3.  **Continuous Improvement**: Aim for incremental improvements through regular experimentation, data analysis, and iteration.

### Conclusion:

Implementing product discovery requires attention to systemic challenges within the organization. By being proactive and adaptable, teams can overcome these obstacles and drive better outcomes for their business.
