# How to Get Better at Guitar | Jake Worth

### The Key to Improving Your Guitar Playing

#### Getting Started with Justin Sandercoe's Technique

This method originated from guitar teacher Justin Sandercoe and may benefit everyone who wants to improve their playing.

#### Identifying a Common Trait among Great Musicians

Great musicians, such as those listed in the article ("Breaking the Law", "The Distance", and "The Ghost of Tom Joad"), didn't rely on tabs. Instead, they listened to music and imitated it by ear.

#### The Listening & Transcribing Approach

To improve your guitar playing, try using this technique:

1. Choose an easy song without Justin's tab.
2. Get a piece of paper with blank tabs.
3. Play the song with your guitar and paper on the table.
4. Stop and find a note on the guitar for each bar you've finished.
5. Continue this process until the entire song is transcribed.

Checking your work means comparing it to online resources or watching experienced players perform with tabs.
