# Forged Between Coal and Code - DEV Community

**WeCoded 2026: Frontend Art - Carbon Trace**

This is a submission for the WeCoded Challenge: Frontend Art.

**Showcasing My Art**

Carbon Trace is an immersive memoir designed and produced by [Your Name]. The experience consists of an ambient audio journey, shifting between scenes in response to sound cues. The circuit traces grow from barely visible to full coverage, while the ghost-drift text becomes more prominent as the narration progresses.

**Inspiration**

The idea was born after witnessing the WeCoded Challenge and feeling inspired by my own experiences with gender roles. Rather than relying on generic solutions, I sought to create an equal representation of women's lives in an industry dominated by men. [Your Name] drew from their own Appalachian coal town background when crafting the story.

**Technical Details**

Carbon Trace was built using a range of technologies:

• **Canvas 2D**: for generating artwork
• WebGL displacement effects: allowing for dynamic images to appear on screen
• GSAP timelines: controlling animations and rendering elements
• Howler.js audio: adding soundscapes to the experience

These components were carefully designed to convey the narrative's emotional arcs, while prioritizing accessibility-first interaction design.

**Key Takeaways**

* Carbon Trace is a multimedia experience that pushes beyond conventional storytelling by incorporating ambient audio shifting between scenes.
* The story is abstracted using the diamond metaphor, emphasizing equal representation of women in various industries.
* The development process has been built with accessibility and inclusivity in mind.
