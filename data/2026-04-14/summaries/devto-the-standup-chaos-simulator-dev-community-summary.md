# The Standup Chaos Simulator - DEV Community

## What I Built
- A tongue‑in‑cheek web app that simulates chaotic developer stand‑up meetings in a chat‑style UI.
- Users pick a team, assign roles, set the sprint’s doom level, and add extra chaos events.
- The simulated meeting plays out message by message, ending with a “Team Dysfunction Meter,” a copy‑ready transcript, and an exportable GIF of the whole stand‑up.
