# Microsoft isn’t removing Copilot from Windows 11, it’s just renaming it

- Microsoft confirmed that the AI assistant formerly known as “Copilot” will remain in Windows 11, but it will be rebranded under a new name.
- The change is part of a broader effort to align the assistant’s branding across Microsoft’s product ecosystem and to avoid confusion with other services that share the “Copilot” name.
- Existing Windows 11 installations will receive the rename through a future update; no functionality is being removed or altered at this time.
- Users can still access the assistant via the same interface and shortcuts they are accustomed to, with only the visual branding and naming updated.
- Microsoft’s statement emphasizes that the underlying AI capabilities, integration with Microsoft 365, and the overall user experience will stay consistent despite the name change.
