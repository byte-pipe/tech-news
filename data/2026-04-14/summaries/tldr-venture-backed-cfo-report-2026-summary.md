# State of the VC‑Backed CFO 2026 – Key Findings

## Overview
- The report, compiled by Silicon Valley Bank, benchmarks more than 200 CFOs from high‑growth venture‑backed firms.
- It examines operational trends, financial‑tool adoption, and strategic practices that shape success in the innovation economy.

## Main Takeaways

- **AI delivers measurable ROI** – 51 % of VC‑backed CFOs report a return on AI investments, with product and engineering teams leading the value creation.
- **AI impacts hiring** – 35 % of finance leaders note that AI‑driven productivity has reduced the need for junior hires; only 5 % have implemented AI‑related layoffs to date.
- **AI spending is set to rise** – Expected AI expenditure in 2026 is projected to increase 2.5 times, though the median spend is anticipated to be just $50 K.
