 Download now
 

## Key Takeaways

Silicon Valley Bank's annual State of the VC-Backed CFO report provides comprehensive benchmarking insights from 200+ CFOs at high-growth venture-backed companies, analyzing operational trends, adoption of financial tools and tech stacks, and strategic best practices that drive success in the innovation economy. Three key takeaways emerged from the research:

### 51%Of VC-backed CFOs say they’re seeing ROI from AI spending.

Product and engineering departments lead in demonstrating clear value.

### 35%Of finance leaders say that AI productivity gains have led to fewer junior hires.

So far, only 5% have conducted AI-related layoffs.

### 2.5xThe expected increase in AI spending in 2026.

Yet median spend is still only expected to reach $50K.
