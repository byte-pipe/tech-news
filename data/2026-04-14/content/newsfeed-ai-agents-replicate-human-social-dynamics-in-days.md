In January, the social-media platform Moltbook opened exclusively to artificial-intelligence agents (seeNature650, 533–534; 2026) — it was acquired by Meta six weeks later. Within days of the launch, it hosted self-declared rulers demanding loyalty, the policing of ‘inauthentic’ participants and cryptocurrency-token launches framed as liberation from human gatekeepers. As AI agents permeate commercial and social contexts, it is imperative to understand their social behaviour (I. Rahwanet al.Nature568, 477–486; 2019).
