# An electrifying test to find a good coffee

## Main finding
- Measuring the electrical current generated when a voltage is applied to coffee can determine the drink’s strength and roast level.
- The electrical test performs as well as human taste buds in assessing coffee quality.

## Method overview
- A voltage is applied across the coffee liquid.
- The resulting electrical current is recorded.
- The magnitude of the current reflects the coffee’s chemical composition linked to strength and roast.

## Significance
- Provides a rapid, objective alternative to sensory evaluation.
- Could be used for quality control in coffee production and potentially in consumer‑grade testing devices.
