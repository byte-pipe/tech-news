# A self-driving car in Texas hit and killed a mother duck, sparking neighborhood outrage | TechCrunch

**Autonomous Vehicle Accident Raises Concerns About Neighborhood in Austin, Texas**

*   In the Mueller Lake neighborhood near a local Italian eatery, residents were outraged after an autonomous vehicle (Avride) ran over a mother duck and failed to stop.
*   Avride confirmed that the vehicle was in autonomous mode at the time of the incident but disputed claims that it failed to slow down or hesitate before running over the duck.
*   The company is conducting an internal review of its safety performance and has also received an invitation to run controlled experiments to evaluate potential improvements to the technology.

**Concerns and Response**

*   Concerned residents have kept Avride informed about their concerns, which include animal welfare and community acceptance due to previous incidents with other autonomous vehicles.
*   An Italian eatery, where the incident occurred, has set up an incubator for ducks that could potentially develop egg cartons after successful incubation.

**Future Implications**

*   This incident highlights the need for responsible testing of autonomous vehicles in urban areas before deployment on public roads.
*   Avride's response to this incident is under scrutiny from local authorities and other companies operating autonomous vehicles in Austin.
