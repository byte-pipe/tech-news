# AWS boss explains why investing billions in both Anthropic and OpenAI is an OK conflict | TechCrunch

**AWS CEO Matt Garman Addresses Investment in Anthropic and OpenAI**

Matt Garman, AWS' executive chairman, discussed the recent $50 billion investment in OpenAI via his company's partnership with Anthropic. He acknowledged that investors have a high level of loyalty but emphasized the importance of managing conflicts of interest.

Garman attributed Amazon's successful cloud computing endeavors to competing with partners and other companies, highlighting AWS's experience in this area. However, he noted that such competition can sometimes be petty or aggressive in nature.

The investment comes as Amazon faces several cloud rivalries, including Oracle selling its services on the platform. Yet, Amazon remains committed to respecting investor agreements and not compromising its competitive advantages through self-disparaging investments.

AWS does invest in both Anthropic and OpenAI to support joint development projects for customers and expand their technology offerings. However, managing conflicts of interest requires making strategic partnerships with both competing companies while maintaining a strong commitment to innovation and customer needs.

* AWS' $50 billion investment in OpenAI marks significant progress in deepening its partnership
