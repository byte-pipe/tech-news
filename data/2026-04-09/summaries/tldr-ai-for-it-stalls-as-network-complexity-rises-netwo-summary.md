# AI for IT Faces Challenges as Network Complexity Increases

**Key Points**

* Enterprises are facing challenges in adopting Artificial Intelligence (AI) solutions in their networks
* Despite high expectations, progress in AI adoption has stalled and network infrastructure is experiencing pressure
* The widening gap between plans and execution threatens to derail enterprise efforts in AI deployments

## Network Infrastructure and AI Demand

* Network teams face persistent security concerns as a primary obstacle to AI deployment
* Integration with existing systems and talent shortages are also hindering AI adoption efforts
* Enterprises turning towards managed service providers (MSPs) is a growing trend to support AI initiatives

## Industry Outlook: Accelerating Growth in AI-Related Infrastructure Demand

* Data centers expect 11% increase in bandwidth within the next year due to AI workloads
* Inter-data center infrastructure growth is also expected to increase with AI-driven demand
