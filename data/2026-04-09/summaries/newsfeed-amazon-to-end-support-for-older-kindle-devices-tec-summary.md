# Amazon to end support for older Kindle devices | TechCrunch

**Amazon to End Support for Older Kindle Devices**
==================================================================

**Key Points:**

* Amazon will end support for older Kindle devices released before 2012.
* Cut off date is May 20, 2026.
* Affected models include original Kindle (1st and 2nd generation), Kindle DX, Kindle Keyboard, Kindle Fire, Kindle Touch, Kindle Paperwhite.

**New Features:**

* Devices will no longer be able to buy, borrow, or download new books directly for those released before 2012 after May 20.
* Users can only access their libraries through newer devices or the Kindle mobile app.

**Transition Considerations:**

* Amazon is offering promotions to help users transition to newer devices.
* Current models may still be usable and functional without replacement.

**Impact on E-Waste:**

* Estimated global e-waste increase by 32% by 2030, related to discarded older devices.
