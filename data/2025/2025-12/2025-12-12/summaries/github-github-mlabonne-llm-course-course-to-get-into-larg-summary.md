## Large Language Models (LLMs) Course Overview

The provided course is designed to help individuals get into the field of deep learning models, specifically Large Language Models (LLMs). The course offers multiple roadmaps and Colab notebooks for a comprehensive understanding.

## Roadmaps

There are two available roadmaps:

### 1. Introduction to Deep Learning with NLP

*   Understand the basics of programming
*   Learn about NLP frameworks (e.g., NLTK, spaCy)
*   Implement simple NLP tasks (e.g., text classification, sentiment analysis)

### 2. Advanced Topics in LLMs

*   Explore architecture design and training techniques
