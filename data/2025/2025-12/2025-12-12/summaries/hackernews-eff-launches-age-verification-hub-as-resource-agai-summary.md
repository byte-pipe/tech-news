# EFF Launches Age Verification Hub as Resource Against Misguided Laws | Electronic Frontier Foundation

### Age-Verification Resource Hub Overview

* The blog post discusses the importance of understanding age-gating laws and their implications.
* It highlights the various reasons why EFF opposes age verification mandates, which target minors or unauthorized access to adult content material.

#### Key Points:

* Age-gating laws are being proposed or implemented in several countries worldwide, affecting users most likely to be under 25 years old.
* These laws restrict online services from allowing underage individuals to sign up or use accounts.
* EFF opposes age verification mandates due to concerns they infringe on freedom of expression and limit access to content available only for adults.
