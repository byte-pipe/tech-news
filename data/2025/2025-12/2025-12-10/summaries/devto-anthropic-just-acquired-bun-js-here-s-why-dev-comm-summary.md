# Anthropic just acquired Bun.js. Here's why. - DEV Community

## Summary

Anthropic has acquired Bun, a JavaScript runtime that is faster than Node.js and also serves as a package manager, bundler, and test runner in one. The company behind Claude, Anthropic's own coding tool Claude Code, has been growing quickly, with $1 billion in annual revenue six months after its public launch.

The acquisition of Bun suggests that Anthropic is looking to establish itself as the primary operating system for AI agents. This is significant because Phase 3 of AI development involves the creation of "_agents" that generate tools and interact with each other, rather than traditional function calls. For these agents to execute effectively, they require a runtime environment that can handle tasks on multiple machines.

 Anthropic's goal in acquiring Bun may be more about demonstrating its commitment to developing reliable and scalable software for AI teams rather than focusing solely on consumer products or chat UIs. The company has already made significant investments in open-source workloads like Meteroid (a fully open-source billing platform for SaaS) and seems to see a promising future for the acquisition of Bun.
