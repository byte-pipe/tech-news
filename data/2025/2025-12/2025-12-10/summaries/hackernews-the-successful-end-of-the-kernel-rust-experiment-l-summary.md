# The End of the Kernel Rust Experiment

The annual Maintainers Summit discussed the progress of thekernel Rust experiment. After a heated debate, the consensus was that the Rust in kernel is no longer experimental but rather a core part of it.

### Key Points:

* Rust in kernel has shifted from an experimental phase to a core component.
* The end of the experiment marks its official inclusion in the Linux kernel's codebase.
* Developers involved felt that this decision comes as a welcome surprise, citing that the team is committed to making progress on the project.
