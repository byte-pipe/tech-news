# DEV Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge

## About the Challenge
The DEV Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge invites developers to share their projects with the community. This is not a traditional "Shark Tank" challenge, but rather an opportunity for developers to showcase their work.

### How to Participate
To participate in this challenge, follow these steps:

1.  Run through January 4th
2.  Upload a 1-minute pitch video on Mux explaining your project
3.  Embed the video as part of your submission

## Project Benefits

-   Share a side project you're proud of
-   Showcase a passion project you've been refining for months
-   Highlight a project that hasn't yet gotten the recognition it deserves
4.  Get $1,500 USD cash prize and DEV++ Membership
### Additional Prize Category: Best Use of Mux
If participants use Mux in an interesting way during their submission, they will receive additional prizes.

## Judging Criteria

The pitch will be evaluated using Shark Tank-inspired criteria:

*   The problem and opportunity
*   Solution and technical approach
*   Value proposition and audience benefit
*   Storytelling and pitch quality
*   Scalability and "Would You Invest?" potential

### Prizes
Both winners receive $1,500 USD cash prize and DEV++ Membership. Participants with valid submissions will also receive a completion badge on their DEV profile.

## Submission Requirements

To participate in this challenge, you need to:

-   Create an account on Mux (no credit card required)
-   Upload your project video to Mux
-   Publish a post with the embedded video
