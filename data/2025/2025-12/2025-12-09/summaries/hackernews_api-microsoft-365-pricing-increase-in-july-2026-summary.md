# Microsoft 365 Pricing Increase in July 2026

**Microsoft 365 Pricing Increase in July 2026**

On **July 1**, 2026, Microsoft will introduce new pricing for its Microsoft 365 licensing plans . To make this transition easier for tenants embedded within the Microsoft ecosystem, Microsoft wants to provide adequate time to plan. [**Figure 1: New Microsoft 365 License Pricing (source: Microsoft)**](https://graph.microsoft.com/)

The new pricing includes:

* A monthly increase of zero to $3 per month across various plans
* Equivalent price increases for other sectors like education and government
* Upward pressure on operating margins for the commercial cloud, potentially offsetting losses due to investments in AI.

**Why the Increase?**

Microsoft justifies this change by highlighting new features and functionality offered under its licensing plans:
- Security Copilot
- Functionality from the Microsoft Intune Suite (available to all E5 customers)
- New apps like Microsoft Loop, Clipchamp, and Places

Despite these added benefits, tenants must use existing apps to extract value for these services.
