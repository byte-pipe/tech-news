# Anthropic just acquired Bun.js. Here's why. - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**Anthropic Acquires Bun, Expands to AI Development**

* Anthropic, the same company behind Claude Code, acquired Bun, a JavaScript runtime, in its first major acquisition.
* The deal adds significant capabilities to Anthropic's existing tooling, packaging, and test running services.

**Context and Implications**

* Bun is faster than Node.js and offers a comprehensive ecosystem for building multiple related tools.
* Anthropic will leverage this new capability in its own AI development work, particularly in Phase 3 of its development approach.

**Comparison to Other Companies' Priorities**

* The acquisition reveals that Anthropic focuses on tooling over chat UI, demonstrating a shift towards becoming the "operating system" for AI agents.
* This decision makes sense given OpenAI's priorities and investments in consumer-facing platforms like Sky and ChatGPT.

**Rationale behind the Acquisition**

* According to founder Jarred Sumner,Anthropic believes it will win the AI industry. He cited experiences with competitors and concluded that "I think Anthropic is going to win."

**Implications for JavaScript Developers**

* The acquisition showcases the growing importance of efficient tooling and packaging in software development.
* As a result, open-source projects like Meteroid may see significant growth as they benefit from a more structured and streamlined approach
