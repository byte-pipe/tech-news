# Congrats to the Winners of the Agentic Postgres Challenge with Tiger Data! - DEV Community

**Summary**

The Agentic Postgres Challenge with Tiger Data invited participants to experiment with AI on PostgreSQL and build something innovative. The winners were chosen based on their experimentation, creativity, and technical depth. Two winners were announced:

* @simranshaikh20_50built a multi-agent code review system that achieves 4x faster performance by leveraging zero-copy database forks.
* @mayu2008created a real-time fraud detection system called FraudSwarn, which combines pg_text and pgvector for improved accuracy.

The submissions stood out due to their dramatic performance improvements:

* Simran Shaikh's multi-agent code review system reduced analysis time from 40-60 seconds to just 10-15 seconds.
* Mayuresh Vijayan's FraudSwarn system achieved a 23% better accuracy than either pg_text or pgvector alone.

Each winner received $1,000 USD, an exclusive DEV Badge, and a DEV++ Membership. The community also showcased applications that pushed the boundaries of what's possible when Postgres becomes the brain of AI systems.
