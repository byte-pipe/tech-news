# Congrats to the Winners of the Agentic Postgres Challenge with Tiger Data! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

* The Agentic Postgres Challenge with Tiger Data has concluded, and the winners have been announced.
* Ten teams participated in the challenge, showcasing innovative applications that leveraged AI and Postgres as the brain of AI systems.
* The challenges were judged by experts who recognized creativity, experimentation, and technical depth displayed by the participants.
* Some of the notable submissions include:
	+ A multi-agent code review system built by @simranshaikh20_50 with 4x faster performance using zero-copy database forks.
	+ A fraud detection system developed by @mayu2008 that achieved 23% better accuracy in real-time with hybrid search.
	+ An AI-powered platform built by @divyasinghdev, GitResume, that transformed GitHub repositories into career insights.
* The winners will receive prizes: $1,000 USD, an exclusive DEV Badge, and DEV++ Membership. Participants with valid submissions will also receive badges on their DEV profile.
* The sponsors of the challenge include Tiger Cloud.
