# Anthropic just acquired Bun.js. Here's why. - DEV Community

**Anthropic Aquires Bun Runtime**

Anthropic, behind popular AI tool Claude Code, has acquired JavaScript runtime Bun. This move signals an shift in focus towards operational software for large-scale AI projects.

### Key Points

* **Bun is faster than Node.js**: Offers improved performance.
* **Bundling and execution**: Enables seamless deployment of code on various machines without dependencies.
* **Phase 3: Agents and interactive interfaces**: Foretells the next level of AI development, where agents write tools and interact with data in real-time.

### Implications

* **Anthropic bets on operational software**: Focusing on a different business model than consumer products like ChatGPT or Sky.
* **OpenAI's acquisition patterns reveal priorities**: While OpenAI excels at chat UI development, Anthropic is betting on the most embedded aspect of system software development.
* **Jared Sumner's evaluation**: He believes Anthropic will win and has stated that decision, underscoring confidence in their platform.

### Development Context

* **Meteroid, an open-source billing platform**: A new project for Jason Muehl and other SaaS developers to build on top of Bun.
* **Bun's roadmap includes cloud hosting**: Potential future focus area where Anthropic will be a significant player.
