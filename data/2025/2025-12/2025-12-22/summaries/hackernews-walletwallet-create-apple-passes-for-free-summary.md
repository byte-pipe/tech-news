# WalletWallet — Create Apple Passes for Free

**WalletWallet**
================

A simple utility to convert physical barcodes into digital passes for Apple Wallet.

### Key Features:

* Entirely free and runs directly from your browser
* Simple and user-friendly interface
* Automatic expiration of the pass to keep Wallet tidy

### User Interface:

1. Enter membership or loyalty card barcode data
2. Configure appearance and titles for the pass
3. Upload image (optional)
4. Download and open the file to add it to Apple Wallet

 ### Note:** No sign-up, installation required.
