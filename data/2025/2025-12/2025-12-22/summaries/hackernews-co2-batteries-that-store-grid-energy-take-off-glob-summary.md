## A New Era in Electric Power Storage

A groundbreaking innovation is emerging as a key to unlocking the potential of renewable energy and addressing climate change: CO2 batteries that store grid energy for later use.

## Key Features

* **Carbon-free**: The CO2 battery stores excess renewable energy from solar or wind power, reducing emissions and dependence on fossil fuels.
* **Long-duration storage**: The facility compresses and expands carbon dioxide daily, producing 20 MW of electricity over 10 hours.
* **Replication possible**: Plans to build multiple facilities across continents in India, Europe, US, and Asia-Pacific regions.

## Benefits for a Sustainable Future

* **Carbon neutrality**: CO2 batteries help offset greenhouse gas emissions from power generation, while storing renewable energy for later use.
* **Increased grid reliability**: By providing a secure, off-peak storage solution, CO2 batteries enhance grid stability and predictability.
* **Economies of scale**: As demand grows for electricity-guzzling data centers, the partnership with Energy Dome offers a cost-effective solution.

## Practical Implications

* **Large-scale deployments**: Google plans to deploy Energy Dome facilities in key locations worldwide, prioritizing sites with significant renewable energy potential and carbon-neutral grid reliability.
* **Competing with traditional storage solutions**: CO2 batteries offer an innovative alternative to traditional battery storage methods, challenging existing infrastructure and technologies.
