## Setting Up Local Coding Models: A Guide

**Who needs local coding models?**

While local models are incredibly powerful for coding tasks like machine learning, artificial intelligence (AI), and data processing, they may not be necessary for projects that require other tools. In fact, running a full-fledged local code model on its own can become limiting due to the increased required hardware resources.

**Key points:**

* Local models can complete ~90% of software development tasks, but the extra 10% is worth paying more for.
* The best part about using local models is that you don't need to pay $100/mo minimum; in fact, they can save you money by lowering your subscription costs.
* Local models require specific hardware resources, making it better suited for smaller-scale projects.

**Takesaway:**

Local coding models are not a substitute for other tools and services. However, in situations where they make sense, they can be a valuable supplemental solution that helps reduce the overall cost of using these capabilities.
