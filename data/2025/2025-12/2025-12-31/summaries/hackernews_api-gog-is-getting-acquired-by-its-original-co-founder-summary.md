# Michał Kiciński Acquires GOG from CD PROJEKT
The GOG team is pleased to announce that Michał Kiciński, one of the co-founders of CD PROJEKT and the original creator of GOG, has acquired GOG. This acquisition aims to preserve classic games and give players true ownership, while also fostering collaboration with indie developers.

# Key Takeaways
* GOG will continue to operate independently, focusing on ethics, non-predatory business practices, and supporting indie developers.
* By acquiring GOG, Michał Kiciński emphasizes the importance of preserving classic games and giving players control over their digital libraries.
* The acquisition does not mean that CD PROJEKT games are going to stop being available on GOG; instead, new titles from CD PROJEKT RED will continue to be offered.
