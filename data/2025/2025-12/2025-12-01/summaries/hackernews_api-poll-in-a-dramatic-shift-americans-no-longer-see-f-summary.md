**Poll Reveals Shift in Attitudes Towards Four-Year College Degrees**

A recent NBC News poll finds that almost two-thirds of registered voters no longer consider a four-year college degree to be worth the cost. The survey results reflect a significant shift in attitudes towards this central tenet of the American dream.
