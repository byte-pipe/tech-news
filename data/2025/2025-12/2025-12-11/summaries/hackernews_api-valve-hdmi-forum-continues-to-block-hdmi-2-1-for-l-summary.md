## Valve: HDMI Forum Continues to Block HDMI 2.1 for Linux

### Summary

The HDMI Forum continues to block the adoption of High Bandwidth Digital Audio (HBA) over HDMI 2.1 on Linux systems, according to Valve.

This means that any Linux kernel with HBA support will not be tested against the latest HDMI Specification, as the official testing and certification process for HDMI 2.1 was blocked by the forum's decision.

### Key Points

* The HDMI Forum blocks HBA over HDMI 2.1 on Linux
* Valve: A Linux kernel without HBA support is not tested for HDMI 2.1 compatibility
* HBA is a advanced audio-visual feature that allows higher bandwidth transmission over HDMI
