# Congrats to the Winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**Summary**

The .NET development community celebrated the winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps. The challenge aimed to build stunning, functional apps from single codebases that run seamlessly across various platforms (Windows, Linux, macOS, mobile, and web). Three winners received prizes ranging from $1,000 USD to DEV Badge.

**Key Points**

* The challenge featured various app ideas, including an interactive portfolio, a virtual pet experience, and a media player.
* Winners demonstrated the power of .NET and Uno Platform by creating apps that run on multiple platforms with minimal coding effort.
* The winners presented their projects with impressive results, showcasing innovative use of AI acceleration and Hot Design Agent features.
* Three winners received prizes: $1,000 USD, DEV Badge, and exclusivity forDEV+ Membership.

**Meet the Winners**

* **Varshith Hegde**: Created an interactive portfolio experience that transformed traditional portfolios into immersive digital experiences.
* **Sujan Koirala**: Designed the Bunny Brew Café app, a delightful virtual pet experience with Hot Design Agent and AI support.
* **Sebastien Lachance**: Reimagined the classic Winamp media player using AI and Uno Platform tools.

**Prizes**

* $1,000 USD for each winner
* DEV Badge for all participants with valid submissions
* Exclusivity forDEV+ Membership

**Sponsorship**

Uno Platform partnered with the challenge to provide support and resources.
