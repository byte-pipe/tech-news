# AWS CEO Explains 3 Reasons AI Can’t Replace Junior Devs

**AWS CEO Matt Garman Explains Why AI Should Not Replace Junior Developers**

Here are three main reasons why AWS CEO Matt Garman does not believe companies should cut junior developer roles due to concerns about replacing them with AI:

*   **Junior devs often know AI tools better**: Senior staff may require longer training and time to adopt new AI tools. In contrast, junior developers have already learned these tools during their time in school.
*   **Cutting junior dev roles can lead to job instability**: Junior employees are typically less expensive than senior staff but can be difficult to reassign due to the "talent pipeline" of fresh hires needed to replace them.
