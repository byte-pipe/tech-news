**Why Coding Doesn't Feel Like Learning**

*   Daily goals and long hours can lead to burnout and frustration.
*   The pressure to consistently learn new concepts can be overwhelming.

### Key Points:

*   The invisible timer in our heads is always ticking away.
*   Fast learning often leads to shallow understanding.
*   Slowing down helps us focus on quality over quantity.

**Maintaining Attentiveness**

*   Read code carefully, rather than skim it quickly.
*   Take breaks without guilt or feeling like you're wasting time.
*   Let yourself struggle before seeking help.
