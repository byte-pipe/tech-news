# The Importance of Practical Experience over Certificates in Coding

### Overview

The author reflects on their recent pursuit of certificates in coding, particularly those offered through LinkedIn. A key realization is that the value of a certificate lies not in its completion but in building projects that demonstrate practical skills.

## Key Points

*   **Practical experience trumps certification**: The importance of creating and showcasing real-world projects rather than relying on academic certifications.
*   **Skills over credentials**: Fostering problem-solving, debugging, and creative thinking through coding endeavors is more valuable than mere completion of courses or programs.
*   **Resources needed for growth**: Identifying reliable online resources to learn theoretical foundations such as algorithms, data structures, and fundamental code concepts.

## Practical Takeaways

-   A portfolio of projects can be created using free resources online, leading to a stronger understanding of coding fundamentals and problem-solving strategies.
-   Learning from hands-on experiences and collaborating on open-source or personal projects can help individuals develop essential skills.

## Additional Insights

While certificates may look nice on a profile, the author emphasizes that real-world experience and a portfolio showcasing proficiency are more valuable than symbolic badges.
