# Congrats to the Frontend Challenge: Halloween Edition Winners! - DEV Community

## Frontend Challenge: Halloween Edition Winners
=============================================

The wait is over! We're excited to announce the winners of Frontend Challenge: Halloween Edition. Our team received an impressive number of submissions from talented developers around the world.

### Winner 1: CSS Art - Animated Vampire Lady
------------------------------------------

@annavi11arrea1b brought a stunning vampire lady to life, complete with animated blinking eyes and mysterious creatures peering from behind a castle wall. Her use of gradients and grid allows for exquisite technical prowess and artistic vision.

### Winner 2: Perfect Landing - Step Into Mystic Falls: A Halloween Story Telling Game
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

@amelia2802 created an immersive storytelling experience inspired by The Vampire Diaries. Her use of vanilla JavaScript and elegant CSS animations makes this a beautifully crafted journey that will engage users.

## Additional Winners:
-   @amandapetersen – Perfect Landing ( Landing Page)
-   @jasonnorton87 - Landing Page

These teams demonstrate exceptional technical skill, artistic vision, and imagination in their submissions. Congratulations to all participants!
