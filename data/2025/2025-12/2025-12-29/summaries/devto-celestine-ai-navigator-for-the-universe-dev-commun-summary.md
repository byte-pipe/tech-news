# Celestine: AI Navigator for the Universe 🪐 - DEV Community

**Celestine: An Intelligent AI Navigator for the Universe**

This article is a submission to the DEV's Worldwide Show and Tell Challenge. It describes the intelligent navigation system called Celestine, an enhanced version of Google Maps capable of exploring celestial bodies in 3D and "landing" on them to discover secrets.

**What Celestine Can Do**

1. **Navigate the Solar System**: Explore planets like Mercury, Venus, Earth, and others in 3D.
2. **Find Similarities between Planets**: Use AI-powered co-pilot (Gemini 2.5) that finds geological "twin" craters on Venus.
3. **Connect with Alien Worlds**: Automatically connect the user's journey to their home planet.

**Key Features**

* **Immersive 3D Experience**: Celestine renders in three dimensions, allowing users to explore planetary features interactively.
* **Multimodal Interaction**: Users can engage through voice or real-time generated video avatars (powered by Tavus).
* **Back to Earth Visualization**: Plotter visualizes coordinates of earthly analogues on a 3D globe.

**Technical Highlights**

1. **Full-Stack Application**: Combines 3D rendering with advanced AI agent orchestration.
2. **Google Maps Platform Integration**: Combines Google Maps Places API with the "Analogues Specialist Agent" (powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash/Pro).
3. **Containerized Deployment with Docker:** Cloud Run deployment is supported.

**About the Project**

This project aims to revive the concept of a "Space Mode" and enable interactive, personal exploration of celestial bodies. Celestine was built for the Google Maps Platform Hackathon, showcasing its potential as an innovative tool for deepened cosmic exploration.
