# Coding Without Pressure: How Slowing Down Helped Me Learn Faster - DEV Community

**Slowing Down Is the Key to Faster Learning**

*   The pressure to learn quickly can be overwhelming.
*   Despite feeling like progress is slow, many coders struggle with persistence.

**What It Takes to Learn Without Pressure**

1.  A focus on reading and understanding code without rushing through it.
2.  Intentional learning: focusing on specific topics or features instead of trying to cover everything at once.
3.  Encouraging the development of a mindset that allows for effort, patience, and curiosity.

**The Confidence Shift**

*   Removing pressure can lead to increased confidence in one's abilities.
*   Learning becomes more manageable as the focus shifts from speed to understanding.

**Learning Faster by Moving Slower**

1.  Focusing on retaining knowledge rather than seeking perfection.
2.  Embracing the idea that learning is an ongoing process rather than a linear one.
3.  Encouraging experimentation and exploration without fear of judgment or comparison.

**Lessons Learned: Overcoming Pressure to Learn Quickly**

*   Coders may need to redefine what it means to be "good" at coding.
*   The journey to mastery can be more enjoyable when approached with patience, persistence, and a growth mindset.
