# Advent of AI 2025 - Day 5: I Built a Touchless Flight Tracker You Control With Hand Gestures - DEV Community

**Advent of AIseries: Day 5 - Building "The Homecoming Board"**

**Main Idea**
The article discusses the development of a gesture-controlled flight arrival display, named "The Homecoming Board," which allows people to navigate using hand gestures in freezing cold temperatures.

**Key Points:**

* The user must wear gloves and mittens while interacting with the display.
* At least two distinct hand gestures are required for navigation, real-time flight data, and audio feedback.
* Flight data is received via a third-party API from "The OpenSky Network."
* Computer vision capabilities are used to recognize gesture patterns.

**Main Idea**: A development challenge has been completed utilizing Goose, an open-source AI agent that supports custom workflows and LLM integration, to create a complex project involving hand gestures for navigation in flight arrival displays.
