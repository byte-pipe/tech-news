# Building a Stunning Travel Agency Landing Page with Uno Platform — My WOW Factor Challenge Submission 🤩 - DEV Community

**Building a Stunning Travel Agency Landing Page with Uno Platform**

### What We Did

We designed and built a modern travel agency landing page entirely powered by Uno Platform. The goal was to create an immersive experience that would make the judges say "WOW" at least once.

**Why We Chose Uno Platform**

The initial project for the Uno Platform Hot Design Challenge ended with an Instagram-style profile page, but after re-reading the second prompt, WOW Factor, we decided to create something big and more immersive this time:

* A modern, clean travel-agency landing page
* Entirely powered by Uno Platform
* Feature animations, confetti celebration subscription section

**Design Goals**

To make the WOW Factor prompt a success, our goal was simple: make the judges say "WOW" at least once. We focused on creating a visually engaging home screen that:

* Captures attention
* Features bold hero sections and smooth performance
* Utilizes creative visuals with mock data animations

**Design Principles**

Our design vision included:

* A living, interactive hero section
* Smooth vertical scrolling
* Travel websites rely heavily on immersion through storytelling (destination cards with mock data)
* Subtle animations for delight without slowing down performance
* Clean, structured XAML for readability and ease of extension

### How We Built It With Uno Platform

We took advantage of Uno's capabilities to create:

* Mock Data & Visual Assets to speed up development
* Grid & StackPanel layout composition for clean grid structure
* Responsive & visually bold buttons & CTAs
* Lightweight confetti animation as a small but impactful visual effect

The entire project was built on top of Uno, leveraging its features to ensure seamless cross-platform experience.

### The WOW Factor Results

With Uno's powers, we created an immersive travel agency landing page that:

* Grabs attention with clean design and creative visuals
* Provides an interactive home screen with smooth performance
* Uses mock data animations for a fun storytelling effect
