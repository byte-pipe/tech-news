# OpenAI declares ‘code red’ as Google catches up in AI race | The Verge

* OpenAI Declares 'Code Red' as Google Catches Up in AI Race *
Technology and AI News

By Robert Hart, Dec 2, 2023

In a significant development for the tech industry, CEO Sam Altman of OpenAI has declared "code red," urging staff to improve its flagship product ChatGPT.

* This means that despite being the leader initially, OpenAI is no longer the clear favorite in the AI race. New competitors like Google and Anthropic are closing in fast.*

CEO Sam Altman's statement emphasized the need for improvement across various aspects of ChatGPT, including enhancements to its core features such as:

  * Greater speed
* Improved reliability
* Better personalization
And more ability to answer more questions

   The initiative includes a weekly team call and temporary transfers to accelerate development.
