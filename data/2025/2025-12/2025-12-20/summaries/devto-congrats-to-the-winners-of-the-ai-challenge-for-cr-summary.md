# Congrats to the Winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**Congratulations to the Winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps**

The DEV Community announced three winners for the "AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps":

1. **Interactive Bento Grid Portfolio**
   Developed by @varshithvhegdebuilt, transforming a traditional portfolio into an immersive digital experience.

2. **Bunny Brew Café**
   Created by @sujankoirala021, showcasing Hot Design Agent and AI to reduce iteration time in the 1997 Winamp media player interface.

3. **New Winamp Interface**
    Designed and built by ArchieCoderDev reimagine Classic Winamp with AI-driven tools, complete with visualizers, custom controls, and more.

The winners will each receive a $1,000 prize, exclusive DEV Badge, and DEV++ Membership for their achievement in the challenge. Uno Platform partnered with DEV to facilitate the development of cross-platform apps using .NET.

As part of the celebration, the Community highlighted the power of technology to create innovative applications that can be accessed across multiple platforms.
