# Celestine: AI Navigator for the Universe 🪐 - DEV Community

Here's a concise and informative summary of the article:

**What I Built:** Celestine, an intelligent AI navigator for the Solar System.

**Key Features:**

* 3D rendering of planets
* "Land" mode to explore crater details
* AI co-pilot (Gemini 2.5) that acts as a bridge between alien worlds and our own
* Geological twin discovery using Google Maps Platform

**Gallery:** A closer look at the core feature, with features like:
	+ Finding similar geological structures on Earth
	+ Visualizing terrestrial analogues on a 3D globe
	+ Seamless planetary navigation through the interface

**Technical Highlights:**

* Full-stack application combining React and react-three-fiber
* AI system built with Google's Agent Development Kit (ADK) and Gemini 2.5
* Backend is Python-based, containerized with Docker, and deployed on Google Cloud Run

**Story Behind It:** A passion project to revive the dream of a "Space Mode" while making cosmic exploration deeply personal.
