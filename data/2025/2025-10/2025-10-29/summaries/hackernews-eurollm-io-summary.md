## EuroLLM: The Largest and Most Advanced Multilingual Large Language Model

### Overview

EuroLLM is a state-of-the-art large language model developed in Europe to support official 24 EU languages. It was designed with multilingual capacity in mind, trained on diverse data from all languages.

### Key Features

- **High-performance**: Excellent performance in various NLP tasks such as question answering, summarization, and translation.
- **Multilingual models**: Models pre-trained and finetuned on text from multiple EU languages.
- **Open-source**: Available for free use by researchers, organizations, and citizens of Europe.

### Notable Variants

- **EuroLLM-9B**: The current flagship model with a 9 billion parameter capacity and training on over 4 trillion tokens.
- **Euro LLMM:** A smaller variant for edge devices.
