# Congrats to the Google AI Multimodal Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

Here's a concise and informative summary of the article:

**Introduction**
Google AI has announced the winners of its multimodal challenge, highlighting the transformative power of multimodal AI. The platform welcomes participants from home to create various applications that incorporate vision, audio, and text understanding.

**Key Highlights**

* Competition was fierce, with many impressive submissions showcasing technical sophistication and creative problem-solving.
* Winners demonstrated remarkable ingenuity in their work, transforming smartphones into intelligent eyes, generating personalized videos, and creating interactive learning experiences.
* The competition will recognize the top three winners: Sunbeam (an AI-powered visual assistant), VisionGen (a "Video-to-Video" generation tool), and Reverse Engineering Reality (an interactive learning experience).
* Participants with valid submissions can receive $1,000 USD, a DEV++ Membership, and an exclusive DEV badge.

**Future Opportunities**
The Google AI Studio Multimodal Challenge announces new challenges to continue pushing the boundaries of multimodal AI. Follow the platform for more exciting opportunities to build, create, and win.
