# Building Disco Week 2: Making Event Networking Less Random 🪩 - DEV Community

**Building Disco: Effective Networking App for In-Person Events**

### Key Points:

* Built a new series documenting the journey of Building Disco, an app for in-person events networking.
* Formed pilot partnership with York University to validate idea and gain real-world feedback.
* Found two friends who share the same vision and passion as the founding team member.

### Summary:

Building disco is a more effective networking app for in-person events that helps users connect with like-minded people based on their goals. To build the app, the founder started by recruiting two friends to join him as the team. The idea gained momentum after sharing it publicly, through demoing it at Startup Open House and incubators such as DMZ. As part of this journey, the founder realized that creating an app is just the beginning, not solving the biggest issue in networking events: meeting the right people at the right time.

### What disco solves:

* Creates a platform for users to connect with others attending in-person events based on their shared goals.
* Connects potential collaborators or clients who aren't looking for business opportunities at events.

### Lessons Learned and Future Steps:

* Founding the app requires discipline to stay focused on creating a product that brings value to its users.
* The idea should not be seen as luck, but rather as a solution to an existing problem.
