**In Memoriam/GBNewby**

**Dr. Gregory B. Newby (2001-2023)**

* A Canadian-born doctor who became CEO of Project Gutenberg Literary Archive Foundation for over 20 years.
* Worked closely with Distributed Proofreaders and served as a voting board member.
* Returned to Canada after government work in the Yukon Territory, where he contributed to its development.

**Career and Contributions**

* Coordinated efforts on ebooks made available through Project Gutenberg through Open Audiobook Collection of AI-narrated audiobooks (2023).
* Produced over 75,000 accessible books with many being edited by Distributed Proofreaders.
* Was named one of "The Best Inventions of 2023" by TIME magazine.

**Ties to Project Gutenberg**

* Partnership with Microsoft and MIT enabled the production of AI-narrated audiobooks for ebook distribution through Project Gutenberg (Collaboration).
* Supported Project Gutenberg's growth into over 75,000 books.
* Led Project Gutenberg until his passing.
