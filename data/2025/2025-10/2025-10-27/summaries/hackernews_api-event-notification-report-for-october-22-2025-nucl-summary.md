### Notifications and Observations
--------------------------------------

- Both trains of Control Room Emergency Ventilation System (CREVS) were inoperable for 10 minutes due to a blown fuse.
- This condition is being reported as an eight-hour, non-emergency notification per 10 CFR 50.72(b)(3)(v).
- Upon discovery of the condition, fuel movement and core alterations were suspended in accordance with the TS LCO required actions.
- There was no impact on the health and safety of the public or plant personnel.

**Technical Specification (TS) Notation**
---------------------------------------------

* Both trains of CREVS are being noted as a limiting condition for operation (LCO 3.7.10 Condition `E' and 3.7.11 Condition `D').
- Upon discovery of the condition, fuel movement and core alterations were suspended in accordance with the TS LCO required actions.
