**Heroku "Back to School" AI Challenge Winners**

The company announces the winners of its annual "Back to School" AI challenge, which encourages students to build AI-powered applications to make the back-to-school transition smoother, smarter, and more successful.

**Winners:**

* **Crazy Creative Winner:** @prime_299792 built an AI-powered study management platform with MentorMind, a multi-agent architecture, that helps teachers plan lessons in minutes.
* **Educator Empowerment Winner:** @victor_lakra_e1910abe17fcbuilt an AI-powered teacher assistant tool with heroku Managed Inference and Claude Sonnet 4 that streamlines lesson planning for educators.

**Recognition:**

Each winner receives $1,000 USD, aDEV++ Membership, and an exclusive DEV badge. All eligible students participate in the challenge will also receive a completion badge for rising to the challenge! The company thanks Heroku for sponsoring this challenge and provides opportunities for students to build with cutting-edge AI features.
