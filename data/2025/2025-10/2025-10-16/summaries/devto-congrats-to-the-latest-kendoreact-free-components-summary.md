# Congrats to the latest KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners Announced**

The KendoReact team has announced the winners of their latest free components challenge. The contest invited participants to create use cases that showcase modern UI components from KendoReact.

* **Overall Prompt Winner**: Arya Pratap Singh created an "intelligent student hub" that addresses academic life management pain points.
* **EduBox AI Student Hub**: KAOutar Benhadine built a game that challenges players to differentiate between AI-generated and real photos, showcasing the power of AI in education.
* **Bot or Not: Can You Spot the AI?** KAOutar Benhadine also won with a "Daily Challenge mode" that features 3 rounds, progressing difficulty, and an admin panel.

The winners will receive $1,000 USD each, a DEV Badge, DEV++ Membership, and a completion badge for rising to the challenge. The organizers express gratitude toProgressfor sponsoring this challenge and wish participants continued success in future initiatives.
