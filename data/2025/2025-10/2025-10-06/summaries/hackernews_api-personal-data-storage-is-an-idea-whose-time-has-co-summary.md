# Personal data storage is an idea whose time has come

**Socially Aware Cloud Storage: Addressing Key Design Issues and Advancing the Vision**
===========================================================

The article discusses the concept of "Socially Aware Cloud Storage," which aims to address three crucial design issues in current cloud storage systems. Developed by Tim Berners-Lee, this vision gives users ownership over their data, including control over where it is stored and accessed.

**Key Design Issues:**

*   **Centralized identity management:** Users and groups are identified using URIs, making it challenging to access and manage data for a specific role or group.
*   **Commodity storage:** Storage elements are accessible on a global scale, regardless of application-specific requirements, leading to inconsistencies in resource allocation.

**Solid Protocol: A Roadmap to Socially Aware Cloud Storage**
-------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Establishing commodity-readwrite storage:** Users can request access to their data from various sources and store it locally or with trusted third-party providers.
*   **Decentralized ownership and control:** Apps running on Solid don't explicitly own user data; they must consent to sharing, providing an additional layer of protection.
