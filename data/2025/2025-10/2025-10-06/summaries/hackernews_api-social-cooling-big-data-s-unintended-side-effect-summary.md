## Effect of Social Screening on Personal Choices

*   Big data collection and AI-driven scoring can influence behavior by targeting individuals with specific characteristics.
*   This "social screening" effect can lead to conforming behaviors, eroding individuality.

*   The pressure to avoid being perceived negatively online can result in self-censorship, where people choose not to express themselves freely.
*   This phenomenon is reminiscent of the Black Man syndrome, where marginalized groups are disproportionately targeted for negative labeling.
