# Behind the Scenes: How We Judge DEV Challenge Submissions - DEV Community

**How We Decide Who Wins Challenges**

The community frequently asks how we judge challenge submissions. To answer this question, we follow a multi-step process that combines automated checks, community input, and professional review with sponsor involvement.

### Step-by-Step Process

*   **Initial Review:** Our automated system verifies submissions to ensure they meet our qualifications.
*   **Judge Review:** Two types of judges participate simultaneously: volunteer judges review entries from both systems while paid judges provide additional expertise from external sources.

**Key Roles and Partnerships**

*   **Volunteer Judges:** Dedicated community members with diverse perspectives help identify standout entries before the real judging begins. They're passionate about the community and bring valuable insights.
*   **Paid Judges:** Contracted professional judges with expertise in evaluating submissions assist us in reducing potential bias within our volunteer-driven process.

**Ensuring Fairness**

*   Our internal team reviews all recommendations from both volunteer and paid judges to select top submissions for sponsors.
*   Sponsors can also participate directly in the final judging process, selecting winners from our shortlist. Additionally, they have access to the full list of submitted entries.
