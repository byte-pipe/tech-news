# Offline card payments should be possible no later than 1 July 2026 | Sveriges Riksbank

## Summary

The Riksbank and representatives from the payment market have reached an agreement to increase the possibility of offline card payments in Sweden by no later than July 1, 2026.

Key points include:

* The goal is to increase resilience to disruptions in the digital payments system
* Measures will be implemented to enable offline payments for essential goods such as food, medicine, and fuel
* Participants in Swedish card payments will work together to implement the measures
* The Riksbank will lead this work and monitor its implementation
