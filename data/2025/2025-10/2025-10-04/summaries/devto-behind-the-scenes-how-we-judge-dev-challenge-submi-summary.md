# Behind the Scenes: How We Judge DEV Challenge Submissions - DEV Community

**How DEV Judges Choose Challenge Winners: A Step-by-Step Guide**
=================================================================

The DEV community has a question that gets to the heart of their judging process: how do they decide who wins these challenges? The answer lies in a multi-step approach that combines automated checks, community input, professional review, and sponsor involvement. Here's what happens from submission to selection:

### Step 1: Automated Validation
---------------------------

*   Submissions tagged with #challengesubmission are initially checked for validity using AI-powered filters.
*   These checks ensure the posts accurately represent challenge topics and aren't spammy.

### Step 2-3: Volunteer and Paid Judge Review (Parallel Process)
--------------------------------------------------------

Once submissions pass validation, two types of judges review entries simultaneously:

*   **Volunteer Judges**: Community members volunteer to review submissions. They help identify standout entries and share their recommendations with the DEV team.
*   **Paid Judges**: Contracted professional judges provide expertise and bandwidth while ensuring fairness during evaluation.

### Step 4: DEV Team Review
---------------------------

DEV evaluates all recommended submissions, including those from volunteers and paid judges.
This step involves reviewing submissions to ensure completeness and accuracy of evaluations.

### Step 5: Sponsor Input (Optional)
----------------------------------

Sponsors may choose to participate directly in the final selection process.

**Conclusion**
----------

By following this multi-step approach, DEV ensures fairness and thoroughness during their judging process. The combination of automated validation, community input, professional review, and sponsor involvement ultimately yields accurate winners for popular challenges.
