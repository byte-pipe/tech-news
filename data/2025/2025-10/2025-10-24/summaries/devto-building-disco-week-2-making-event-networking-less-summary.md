# Building Disco: A Journey of Self-Discovery in Networking App Development

## Key Points:

*   Introduced the journey of building Disco, a networking app aimed at connecting people at in-person events based on their goals.
*   Discussed finding the right team members, setting a clear vision, and securing partnerships to bring about success.
*   Highlighted the challenges faced by introverts in networking events and the potential for meaningful connections through chance rather than luck.

## What Disco is Offering:

*   An app that connects people at in-person events based on their goals for attending.
*   User profiles allow users to share what they're looking for, increasing the chances of meeting relevant parties.

## Building Process and Outcomes:

*   Partnered with York University to validate ideas and gain real-world feedback.
*   Featured demos at events, including Startup Open House in Toronto, to gather user input and improve the product.
