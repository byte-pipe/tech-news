# Congrats to the latest KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners**

The KendoReact Free Components Challenge has concluded with three winners. The winners were chosen based on their creative use cases, showcasing the modern UI components of KendoReact.

### Overall Prompt Winner
Arya Pratap Singh's "EduBox" student hub addresses academic life chaos, using AI-powered tools to streamline workflows in various fields.

### Code Smarter, Not Harder Winner
Kaoutar Benhadine built an engaging game challenging players to distinguish AI-generated images from real photos.

### RAGs to Riches Winner
Chijioke Osadebe developed a telemedicine platform, Consultify, that addresses language barriers and offers context-aware functionality.

**Sponsors**
Progress for sponsored the challenge with NucliaRAG; Progress continues to lead React development with KendoReact. Kathryn Grayson and her team were also mentioned as sponsors.

### Prizes
Each winner will receive $1,000 USD, an exclusive DEV Badge, and aDEV++ Membership.
All participants will receive a completion badge for rising to the challenge!

### Next Steps
The next KendoReact Free Components Challenge is expected later this year.
