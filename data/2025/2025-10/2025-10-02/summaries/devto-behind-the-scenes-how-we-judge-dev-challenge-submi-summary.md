# Behind the Scenes: How We Judge DEV Challenge Submissions - DEV Community

**How We Decide Who Wins These Challenges**

The community's question about how you evaluate each challenge submission has sparked interest. To provide a clear understanding of our process, we'll walk through the steps involved in selecting winners.

### Step 1: Automated Validation

We start by confirming that posts tagged with #challengesubmission are legitimate submissions. This ensures that only actual entries are considered.

### Step 2 & 3: Volunteer and Paid Judge Review (Parallel Process)

After AI validation, we have both volunteer judges, who are community volunteers, and paid judges, external professional reviewers, to review entries simultaneously.

*   **Volunteer Judges**: Community members identify standout entries and recommend winners. Their diverse perspectives and passion for the community add value to the process.
*   **Paid Judges**: Contracted professionals bring additional expertise and bandwidth, reducing potential bias in community-based judging. They evaluate entries and provide winner recommendations.

### Step 4: DEV Team Review

Our internal team reviews volunteer and paid judge recommendations, as well as any uncovered gems that may not have been surfaced earlier rounds.

### Step 5: Sponsor Input

Depending on the challenge and sponsor preferences, they may actively participate in the final judging process. Alternatively, sponsors can advocate for specific entries during the review period.
