## The Problem: Webhooks in Discords

Webhooks are powerful, flexible, and enable custom messages on Discord. However, creating them requires:

* Writing custom scripts
* Hosted bots that charge every "premium" feature, or manual copying webhook payloads like it's 2017.
Their workflow is slow, error-prone, and frustrating, especially managing multiple servers or announcements.
