# Changes to Gmailify & POP in Gmail Starting January 2026

The upcoming changes affect two key features of Google's Gmail service: Gmailify and POP. **Key Points:**

- Gmail will no longer support integrating specific third-party features (spam protection, inbox organization, better email notifications) with the primary account.
- These changes aim to provide a more secure and current experience.

**What Users Need to Do:**

* Continue using your original account without new features.
* Learn how to add another email account to the Gmail app instead of relying on IMAP connections for syncing third-party accounts.

## Changes to POP Connections

Gmail will no longer support "Check mail from other accounts" and its associated IMAP features. To maintain access to third-party accounts, users need to:

- Use a standard IMAP connection supported by the Gmail mobile app.
- Learn how to add another email account to the Gmail app.

## Frequently Asked Questions**

- Will Existing Messages Stay Accessible?
   All pre-deprecated messages will remain accessible in Gmail.

- Can Third-Party Accounts Still Use Other Email Services?
  Yes, users can still use third-party accounts (like Yahoo! and Outlook) with the Google Mail mobile app on Android and iPhone/ iPad.
