# Beyond Hacktoberfest: Building a True Open Source Journey - DEV Community

## The Importance of Creating Opportunities in Open Source Contributions

Hacktoberfest is an event that welcomes new contributors to appreciate, give back to, and support open source projects. However, this excitement can sometimes distract from creating meaningful contributions. The author emphasizes the importance of having a unique path in open source instead of searching for opportunities.

## Benefits of Contributing Meaningfully

* Creating meaningful issues helps retain engagement and motivates others to contribute.
* Assigning tasks early on ensures that new contributors are given realistic goals and challenges.
* Avoids distractions from searching for opportunities, allowing focus on actual contributions.

The author points out a few common pitfalls experienced during Hacktoberfest, such as:

* Submitting PRs without checking if the issue is already being worked on.
* Ignoring the spirit of collaboration and giving back to open source projects.
* Underestimating the time and effort required for initial tasks.

## Strategies for Contributing Meaningfully

* Assign issues early on to ensure clear goals and a realistic timeline.
* Focus on creating PRs that can be merged into existing codebases or feature branches.
* Prioritize tasks based on their difficulty and relevance to the project's needs.

The author concludes by emphasizing that Hacktoberfest is an event, not a competition. By fostering open communication and collaboration, true benefits of contributing to open source projects can be realized.

## Key Takeaways

* Create meaningful issues instead of searching for opportunities.
* Assign tasks early on to ensure clarity and realism.
* Focus on creating PRs that can be contributed to existing codebases or feature branches.
* Prioritize tasks based on their difficulty and relevance to the project's needs.

By adopting these strategies, contributors can make a positive impact in open source projects while enjoying the spirit of collaboration and giving back.
