# Congrats to the latest KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**KendoReact Free Components Challenge Winners**

The latest KendoReact Free Components Challenge winners have been announced, showcasing creative use cases that integrate modern UI components from the library. The top three winners are:

1. **Arya Pratap Singh**: Intelligent Student Hub for managing academic life across multiple tools.
2. **KAOUTAR BENHADINE**: Engaging game featuring AI-generated images and Daily Challenge mode with progressive difficulty.
3. **Chijioke Osadebe**: Consultify, a healthcare application that revolutionizes telemedicine through context-aware RAG and multilingual communication.

Each project demonstrates the versatility of KendoReact's modern UI components and highlights the potential of integrating AI tools to accelerate workflows.

**The Winners Received:**

* $1,000, an exclusive DEV Badge, DEV++ Membership
* Completion badges for rising to the challenge!

The sponsors' thanks go to Progressfor and @kathryngraysonand team for the opportunity to work together on the challenge.
