# Congrats to the Google AI Multimodal Challenge Winners! - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

## Winner Announced: Google AI Multimodal Challenge
The competition for the Google AI Studio Multimodal Challenge was fierce, with participants submitting innovative applications that combined multimodal AI techniques to solve real-world problems.

### Winners:
* Sunbeam: an AI-powered visual assistant designed for the visually impaired worldwide.
* VisionGen: a "Video-to-Video" generation tool that uses JSON prompting to automate prompt generation.
* reverse Engineering Reality: a creative tool that transforms curiosity into an interactive learning experience using Google's AI capabilities.

## Recognition and Rewards
The three winners will each receive $1,000 USD, DEV++ Membership, and an exclusive DEV badge. All participants with valid submissions will also receive completion badges on their DEV profiles.

## Thanks and Future Plans
Google AI partnered with the community to host this challenge, highlighting the power of multimodal AI in solving complex problems. The team at Google AI was great to work with, and they encourage future challengers to share their ideas and solutions online.
