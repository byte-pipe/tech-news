# Samsung’s 60% DRAM Price Hike Signals a New Phase of Global Memory Tightening - BuySellRam

## Samsung's 60%DRAMPrice Hike Signals a NewPhase of GlobalMemoryTightening

### Key Points:

* Samsung hikes memory chip prices by up to 60%
* The move marks one of the steepest price increases in years and underscores the strain on global supply chains
* Demand from AI data centers is straining supply chains, leading to increasing contract and spot prices for DRAM modules
* Prices surge by as much as 60% since September due to rapid growth in AI infrastructure demands

### Impact:

* Implications can be felt across PC, server, and IT hardware industries well into 2026
* Buyers across these markets will face higher prices for high-density server DRAM modules
* Suppliers are responding with reduced orders for traditional DRAM products that power laptops and desktops
* Growing demand from AI data centers is putting pressure on suppliers to prioritize high-bandwidth memory (HBM)

### Causes:

* Supply constraints, production prioritization for HBM, and global inventory shortages among leading buyers such as cloud providers and enterprise OEMs contribute to the price hike
* Industry analysts point to a perfect storm of factors driving up demand for AI infrastructure despite supply chain limitations
