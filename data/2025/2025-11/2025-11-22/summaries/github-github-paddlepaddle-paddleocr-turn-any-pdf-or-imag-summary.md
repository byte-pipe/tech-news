### About
#### **PaddleOCR**: A powerful, lightweight OCR (Optical Character Recognition) toolkit that connects images and PDFs with LLMs (Large Language Models).

* Supports up to 100+ languages
* Turn any document into structured data for your AI

### Features
* Bridges the gap between images and PDFs with LLMs
* Lightweight and highly efficient
* Support for multiple languages
