# Bitchat for Gaza - messaging without internet

**Bitchat: A Secure Messaging App for Palestinian Communities**

**Introduction**
_____________________________________________________________________
Bitchat is a new messaging app designed to provide secure communication options for Palestinians in areas with restricted access to electricity, telecoms, internet routing, and communication infrastructure.

**Why Bitchat?**
______________________

*   Due to Israel's deliberate actions causing frequent blackouts in Gaza, Palestinians are isolated from each other and the outside world.
*   Even when using regular communication methods, conversations can be monitored and recorded, exposing users to surveillance and potential targeting.
*   The primary human right to communicate freely is being denied to Palestinians.

**The Solution**
______________

Bitchat provides a reliable alternative for messaging and communication, functioning even without internet or power access. Each phone can connect simultaneously through Bluetooth, allowing messages to be distributed farther than the immediate area affected by connectivity issues.

Additionally, Bitchat enables global network coverage with its no-server architecture and use of "Geohash" channels for location-based chats.


**Staying Connected in Crisis Situations**
_____________________________________

Bitchat offers a crucial lifeline during times when traditional networks are out, particularly for Palestinian communities affected by civil unrest. **This app helps individuals stay connected to their loved ones, organize with community members, and share critical information**.

**Validation and Community Support**
____________________________

Bitchat has gained the validation of organizations like T4P, a non-profit that evaluates new technologies, ensuring they meet high security standards for Palestinian communities when under threat. Technologically advanced Palestinians can rely on Bitchat as their most trusted method of communication during civil unrest.
