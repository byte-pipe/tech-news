# Build in Public: Day Zero - DEV Community

Here is a concise and informative summary of the article:

**What is WYKRA?**
WYKRA is an online tool that helps users find relevant voices and communities on various topics by searching for individuals, places, and organizations online.

**Problem Statement:**
The problem with finding online audiences is inefficient and leads to manual searching using hashtags, profile hopping, or tabs. This process can be time-consuming and prone to errors.

**WYKRA's Origins**
The agency built WYKRA as a Real-Time AI Agent challenge powered by Bright Data and n8n. Initially, the tool focused on Instagram, but after its completion, the creators re-evaluated its purpose and decided to build it in public for the open-source community.

**Market Analysis:**
The market currently offers outdated solutions such as influencer spreadsheets, paid databases, or manual content research. The solution provided is promising, addressing the pain points with automated searching and analysis.

**Key Insights:**

* The internet is vast, but finding online audiences efficiently is inefficient.
* WYKRA's success lies in its ability to analyze public data and provide meaningful insights.
* Finding relevant voices and communities requires more than just hashtags (although they play a role).
* Building a tool like WYKRA can help users grow their audience without spending hours searching for mentions.

**Next Steps**
With the challenge now resolved, the team plans to continue developing WYKRA in the open community, with three months of development planned. This will allow them to refine the tool and contribute back to the public space.
