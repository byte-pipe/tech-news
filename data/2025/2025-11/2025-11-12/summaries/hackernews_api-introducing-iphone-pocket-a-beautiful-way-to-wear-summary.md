iPhone Pocket is a 3D-knitted construction designed by ISSEY MIYAKE that fits any iPhone, along with various pocketable items. Available at select Apple Store locations starting November 14 in France, Greater China, Italy, Japan, Singapore, South Korea, the UK, and the US, as well as on apple.com.

The design features a ribbed open structure reminiscent of pleats, allowing users to comfortably wear or tie it onto bags. A color palette with eight options: lemon, mandarin, purple, pink, peacock, sapphire, cinnamon, and black, offers a range for personalization.
