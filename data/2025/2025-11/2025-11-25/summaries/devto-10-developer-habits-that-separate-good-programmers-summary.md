# 10 Developer Habits That Separate Good Programmers From Great Ones

Excellent Developer Habits are essential for building great programs over time. This list highlights the fundamental habits that differentiate good programmers from great ones.

## Habit 1: They Read Code Far More Than They Write It

Being an avid code reader is a critical habit in programming, and great developers spend more time reading than writing alone. This habit sets them apart from average programmers who often rely too heavily on raw intelligence. A voracious code reader helps them improve their coding skills, understand different development approaches, and ultimately become better professionals.

## Habit 2: They Document Their Code Thoroughly

The second habit separates great programmers from the rest is documenting their code thoroughly. By recording important details about each component or function, they can identify issues quickly during testing or when collaborating with others on projects. Effective coding practices make it easier to modify and maintain their existing codebase over time.
