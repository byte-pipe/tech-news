## The Bittersweet End of XML Standardization Effort

XSLT (Extensible Stylesheet Language Transformations) was a powerful tool for transforming and formatting XML documents. However, its demise at the hands of Google may be seen as a tragic fate.

The development and adoption of XSLT were influenced by Microsoft's ActiveX Controls, which in turn impacted the success of Mozilla Corporation, leading Microsoft to end its involvement with XSLT in 2004 due to high licensing fees.
