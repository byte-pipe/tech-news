**From Chatbot to Autonomous Agent**

The article introduces Tongyi DeepResearch, a fully open-source website agent that achieves performance on par with OpenAI's DeepResearch across a comprehensive suite of benchmarks. **Key Points:**

- Tongyi DeepResearch is a fully open-source Web Agent that outperforms proprietary and openly sourced Deep Research agents.
- The agent achieves high scores across multiple benchmarks, including Humanity's Last Exam (HLE), BrowseComp, and xbench-DeepSearch.
