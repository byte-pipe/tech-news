# 5 must know open-source repositories to build cool AI apps - DEV Community

**Open-Source Repositories for Building AI-Powered Apps**

Teams are racing to ship AI-powered features, from solo founders building chatbots to enterprise teams automating workflows. However, the focus should be on finding the right open-source tools and frameworks that give developers full control, transparency, and freedom to experiment.

### Real-Time Intelligence with Stream Vision Agents

Stream Vision Agents is an open-source framework for building real-time, multimodal AI that can see, hear, and respond in milliseconds. It's built for developers who want to bring true intelligence to live video without being locked into a single model or transport provider.

* **Key Features:** Open source, forkable, multi-platform support (Stream Video or WebRTC-based SDK), flexible providers (OpenAI Realtime, Gemini Live, etc.)
* **Example Use Case:** Coaching a golf spin with YOLO and OpenAI Realtime as the brain. YOLO detects pose, while the Realtime API responds to movements as they happen.

### High-Fidelity Text-to-Video Generation

Open-Sora is an open-source repository for converting text or images into high-quality videos that look stable over time. It supports text-to-video and image-to-video generation and allows fine-tuning on datasets to generate domain-specific content such as marketing clips, story scenes, or simulations.

* **Key Features:** Supports text-to-video and image-to-video generation, customizable, flexible datasets
* **Example Use Case:** Converting text into marketing clips with Open-Sora. Fine-tune it for specific domains if needed.
* **Repository:** [Open-Sora](https://github.com/hugett/open-soda)
