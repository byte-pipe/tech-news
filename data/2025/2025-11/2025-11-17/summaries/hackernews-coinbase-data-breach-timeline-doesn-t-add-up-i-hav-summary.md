## The Coinbase Data Breach: A Four-Month Mystery

*January 7, 2025:* The author received an email with sensitive information about their account, including a withdrawal request for 2.93 ETH.

The attacker revealed to the Coinbase security team that they had obtained detailed personal information and compromised the author's account details using stolen data.
*January 13, 2025:* The Head of Trust & Safety responded to the author, acknowledging robustness in their report but failing to provide an explanation for how their private account data was accessed.

This revelation sparked two follow-up calls from Coinbase: asking for clarification on the account balances and requesting that they investigate the breach. However, these questions were met with silence for four months.
*No responses:* were received from Coinbase regarding the investigation into the breach until May 15, 2025, when the company publicly disclosed what had happened. The information revealed:
