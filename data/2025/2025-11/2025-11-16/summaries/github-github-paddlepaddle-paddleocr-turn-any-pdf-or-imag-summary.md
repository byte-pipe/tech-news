**PaddlePaddle and PaddleOCR**

* **Overview**: A powerful, lightweight OCR (Optical Character Recognition) toolkit that integrates images and PDFs with Large Language Models (LLM).
* **Features**: Supports 100+ languages, turns any document into structured data for AI applications.
* **Purpose**: Provides a bridge between image and PDF data and Large Language Models (LLMs).
* **Usage**: Used in various AI and ML projects to extract structured data from documents.
