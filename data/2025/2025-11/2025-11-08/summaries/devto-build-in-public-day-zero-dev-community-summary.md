# Build in Public: Day Zero - DEV Community

**What is WYKRA and its Problem**

WYKRA is a small agent built for the Real-Time AI Agents challenge using Bright Data and n8n. Its initial purpose was to find influencers on Instagram about bread in Barcelona. However, after completing the challenge, the idea failed to disappear, and its developers started thinking it had potential.

**The Difficulty of Finding Relevant Voices Online**

Despite understanding that finding relevant voices online is painful, the market currently offers:

* Outdated influencer spreadsheets
* Priced databases with questionable accuracy
* Agencies charging high startup fees
* Manual strategies involving hashtags, profile hopping, and too many tabs

This indicates that there is a lack of efficient solutions for discovering voices in different communities.

**The Problem is Wider than a Single Platform**

WYKRA's initial focus was narrowly defined as Instagram influencers. However, the problem is more general: it affects various online platforms, people, places, and communities that shape taste, trust, and attention. The solution should be able to find voices for different niches.

**Early Lessons from the Challenge Version**

The first attempt at WYKRA showed promise in extracting data from Instagram using Bright Data's API and producing meaningful summaries. However, a crucial step was identifying the right accounts to focus on.

In summary, WYKRA is actually about finding relevant voices online that can be used for marketing, entrepreneurs, indie founders, creators, or anyone trying to reach their target audience without spending hours searching through platform search results. The solution could benefit from breaking its initial narrow scope and expanding to cover a broader range of platforms.
