## What is AWS Builder Center?

AWS Builder Center is a place to connect with builders who understand your journey. You can share solutions, influence AWS product development, and access useful content that accelerates your growth.

Join the Community

AWS Builder Center integrates the previous AWS Developer Center and community.aws for a cohesive experience to connect, learn, and influence the platform you build on.

## Why This Matters for Builders

### Learn at Your Own Pace

Access hands-on tutorials, workshops, and interactive resources tailored to every skill level. Explore AI playgrounds, discover new tools in the Toolbox, and experiment with AWS services through the Free Tier—all organized to support your actual building journey.

### Connect with People Who Get It

Find AWS Heroes, Community Builders, User Groups, and Cloud Clubs all in one place. Connect with builders who understand the specific challenges you're facing—whether you're debugging a serverless function at 2am or architecting your first multi-region deployment.

### Your Voice Shapes AWS

The Wishlist feature lets you submit feature requests and product suggestions directly to AWS teams. Vote on ideas from other builders, and watch as the community collectively influences the AWS product roadmap. Your real-world challenges and needs become part of the conversation that shapes AWS services.

### Break Down Language Barriers

Content is automatically available in 16 languages, with machine translation for user-generated posts and comments. Collaborate with builders worldwide without language getting in the way.

## Built for Builders

Your profile becomes your builder identity across AWS services via AWS Builder ID. Share your projects, expertise, and ideas with a custom URL and QR code. All your posts, wishes, and community interactions live in one centralized view.

## Join the Community

If you're building on AWS (or thinking about it), Builder Center gives you a space to connect with others on the same journey, access resources that actually help you ship faster, and have a real say in where AWS goes next.

Join the Community

AWS Builder Center was designed to help you connect, learn, and build with fellow AWS builders, so enjoy your journey together!
