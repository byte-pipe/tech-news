The key issue is that many APIs claiming to be RESTful do not properly leverage the concept of hypermedia as the engine of application state (HATEOAS), which is a crucial aspect of true RESTful design.

This presents a significant opportunity for solo developers. Businesses and organizations are increasingly relying on APIs to power their applications and services, but the vast majority of these APIs are not truly RESTful.
