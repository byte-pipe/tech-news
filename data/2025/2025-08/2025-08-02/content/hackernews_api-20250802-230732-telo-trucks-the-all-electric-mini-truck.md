## Built to handle everything you need

The TELO MT1's 60-inch truck bed extends into the backseat via an innovative midgate, accommodating everything from 4-by-8-foot sheets of plywood to 8-foot surfboards with the tailgate up.

A specially designed storage tunnel under the bed doubles as a footwell for a third row of seats allowing you to comfortably seat up to eight passengers.

The MT1's secure tonneau cover protects your belongings, making TELO ideal for storing work gear and weekend essentials on the go.
