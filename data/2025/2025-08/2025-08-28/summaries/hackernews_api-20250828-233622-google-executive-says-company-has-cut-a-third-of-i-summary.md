# Google executive says company has cut a third of its managers

The article discusses a significant change in management structure within Alphabet, the parent company of Google. According to Brian Welle, vice president of people analytics and performance, Google has eliminated over one-third (35%) of its managers overseeing small teams. This reduction is part of an effort to "be more efficient as we scale up" and reduce bureaucracy.
