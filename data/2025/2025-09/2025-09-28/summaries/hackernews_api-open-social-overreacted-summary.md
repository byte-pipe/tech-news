## The Emergence of Open Social Movement

Open social apps are gaining traction, challenging the dominance of existing platforms. The open source model has not yet achieved widespread adoption, but it has played a significant role in industry standardization.

## **The AT Protocol**

Bluesky's "AT Protocol" is the latest thought leader among proponents of open social. Although imperfect and still in development, this protocol offers several key advantages:

  -   It **prioritizes user control**: by introducing private rooms and profiles, users can own their data.
    *Example:* You interact with other users on your platform, sharing content and information.
      **User Profile:** When interacting with others, I share a username and some basic info.

    *Example:* Alice wants to create a post about her trip. She posts it on our website.
      **Private Room:** Alice creates a private room for this activity, so users cannot see what she posted without permission.
