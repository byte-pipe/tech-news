# Either You Die a Developer, or Live Long Enough to See Yourself Become a Product Manager - DEV Community

Here's a concise and informative summary of the article:

**The Evolution of Role and Skills**

* Product Managers should develop product sense to succeed, but many struggle with this aspect.
* The tech job exodus is expected due to changing landscape and increased AI-powered processes.

**Three Main Roles for Developers**

1. **Product-Minded**: Think like users; write stories before code, notice friction, and propose better flows.
2. **Problem Solvers**: Love hard problems, patterns, and clean solutions; often require more investment in UX; expect to develop clear specs and user lens alongside Product Managers.

**Trends and Expectations**

* AI is driving skill development: focus on crystal-clear specs, problem-solving, and user empathy.
* Roles like PM/Tech Lead or VP of Engineering will naturally progress with new skills.
* The lines between roles are blurring, as developers can adapt to new situations and learn new skills.
