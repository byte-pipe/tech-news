## What I Built

EduSpark AIis astudent-centriclearning assistant designed to transform traditional study materials into engaging, digestible formats. It tackles common student pain points likenote overload, doubt resolution, and fatigueby:

Converting notes (image, PDF, PPT, audio) into concisesummaries.Generatinganalogiesto simplify complex concepts.Offeringread-aloudfunctionality for passive learning.Creatingflashcardsandquizzesfor active recall.Providing an AI-powered mentorchatbotfor personalized guidance.

## How I Used Google AI Studio

I used Google AI Studio to power the multimodal intelligence behindEduSpark AI:

Gemini 2.5 Proenabled seamless processing of images, audio, and PDFs.Implementedspeech-to-textfor audio notes and lectures.UsedGemini’s reasoningto generate analogies and concept maps.Integrated theme toggling and local storage for persistent user experience.

## Multimodal Features

EduSparkAI thrives on multimodal capabilities:

Image/PDF to Notes: Students upload files, and the app extracts key points and summaries.

Audio to Notes: Record or upload lectures; AI transcribes and analyzes them.

Notes to Flashcards/Quiz: Converts summaries into interactive learning tools.

Read Aloud: Summaries are spoken aloud for auditory learners or tired minds.

AI Mentor Chatbot: Offers contextual help and concept reinforcement.
