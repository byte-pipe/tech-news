# "Your Profile Doesn't Match the Role" and Other Interview Horror Stories - DEV Community

**Interview Horror Stories: A Collection of Challenges**

Many job interview situations can be frustrating, awkward, or downright unprofessional. This article presents several cases of what I call "interview horror stories," highlighting the challenges and misunderstandings that people may face during their hiring efforts.

### 1. **Your Profile Doesn't Match the Role**

*   Scenario: A young candidate applies for a job in another city.
*   Description: The company invites them to an in-person interview, which they drive two hours to attend. During the meeting, the interviewer expresses concern about their ability to perform management tasks, despite having no experience with such responsibilities.
*   Lesson: Accepting an offer without fully understanding the roles and expectations is a rookie mistake.

**How It Went**

*   The candidate arrived late, causing them to worry that they had failed to meet the timeline.
*   Despite spending hours driving to get to the interview, the company's "help full" response belied their unprofessional actions.
