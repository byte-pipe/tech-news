# Announcing Cloudflare Email Service’s private beta

# Introducing Private Beta: Revolutionizing Email Management with Cloudflare

By private beta, we're introducing Email Sending, a new capability in the Cloudflare Email Service. This service allows developers to send transactional emails directly from their applications using Cloudflare Workers.

## Key Benefits

* **Simplified**: Email management becomes easier for developers as the solution is simple and reliable.
* **Integrated with Workflows**: Email Sending joins the popular Email Routing product, streamlining email experiences across your application.
* **Reinventing Delivery**: With low latency and global coverage, emails are delivered quickly and reliably.

## Enhanced Workflow

* **New Product**: Cloudflare Email Service, a single, unified developer experience for all email needs.
* **Single Point of Integration**: Integrating with developers' workflows to make developing with email feel natural.
