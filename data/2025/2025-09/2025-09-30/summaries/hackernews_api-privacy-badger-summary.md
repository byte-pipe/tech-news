# Privacy Badger

**Privacy Badger: A Solution for Online Tracking Transparency**

**What is Privacy Badger?**
Privacy Badger is a free browser extension developed by the digital rights nonprofit EFF to help users control online tracking and advertising.

**Key Points**

* Stops third-party trackers from secretly monitoring user activity on the web
* Automatically blocks advertisers that are suspected of tracking users across multiple websites without consent
* Designed to ensure transparency and accountability in online advertising

**Browser Add-ons for Different Browsers**
The extension provides add-on links for download, allowing users to install it manually into their browsers:

* Chrome
* Firefox
* Edge
* Opera
* Other browsers (alternative options)

**Alternative Installation Options**
Users can opt for other browser versions or alternative installation methods:

* **Add to Chrome**: A direct link is provided in the article.
* **Other browsers**: Additional information and links are also shared.

**How Privacy Badger Works**
The extension analyzes website behavior, identifying trackers that may be attempting to track users without their consent. It then blocks these ads from loading, ensuring greater online tracking transparency.

**Third-Party Trackers**
Privacy Badger identifies third-party trackers on websites and automatically blocks them from collecting user data.

**How does Privacy Badger Block Ads?**
The extension acts as a gatekeeper to the online advertising domain, detecting potential tracking attempts and blocking advertisements when necessary.

**What is Global Privacy Control (GPC)?**
GPC refers to a feature in Privacy Badger allowing users to block multiple websites or domains using a single slider interface.

**Alternatives and Limitations**
While not all ad blockers prevent tracking, some may have limitations on their reach in certain scenarios. Additionally, GPC might not be supported across all browsers.

**Configuration and Setup**
Users can customize the extension settings via the Privacy Badger menu to manage cookie blocking on specific websites or domains.
