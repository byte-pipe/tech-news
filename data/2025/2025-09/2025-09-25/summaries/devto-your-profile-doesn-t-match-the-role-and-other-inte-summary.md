# "Your Profile Doesn't Match the Role" and Other Interview Horror Stories - DEV Community

# Interview Horror Stories

**Your Profile Doesn't Match the Role**

*   A candidate applies for a job in a different city and the company invites them for an in-person interview.
*   The interviewer reveals that their profile doesn't match the open role, but asks them to drive two hours just to inform them of this discrepancy.

**Management Tasks... For an Intern**

*   An intern is offered an internship opportunity at a local startup where management tasks are suggested as part of the job description.
*   During the interview, the interviewer explains that they want the company to manage office operations, even though the candidate has no experience with these tasks and may not have the necessary skills or resources.

**Fast Rejections**

*   A candidate is offered a job at a startup but requires significant financial support, such as paying for gas.
*   Despite being a college intern, the role requires administrative tasks that would be difficult to complete during internship hours.
