# Usabiility oversights · Issue #22 · eu-digital-identity-wallet/av-doc-technical-specification · GitHub

**Usability Oversights in Kam1NI's Wallet Solution**

**Summary:**

* The solution assumes everyone has a smartphone, but users without one (e.g. granny) may struggle with verifying their age online.
* Implementing such a system would disrupt web browsing experience and require users to verify age every time they visit a website.

**Key Points:**

* Users without smartphones will face challenges in verifying their age online
* A solution requiring user verification every time might hinder web browsing experience
* Implementing such a system may rely on specific programming languages (e.g. Java) limiting startups

**Important Notes:**

* For small start-ups, implementing the proposed system could be detrimental to survival.
