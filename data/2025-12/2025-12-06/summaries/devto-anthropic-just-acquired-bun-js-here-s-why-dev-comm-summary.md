# Anthropic just acquired Bun.js. Here's why. - DEV Community

Anthropic just acquired Bun, a JavaScript runtime that's faster than Node.js and offers more features than Claude Code. This acquisition marks Anthropic's first major investment in the cloud, with no revenue to speak of yet. Here are the Key Points:

* **Bun**: Faster-than-Node.js JavaScript runtime with package manager, bundler, and test runner functions.
* **Open-source**: MIT-licensed and has 7 million monthly downloads.
* **First acquisition for Claude Code**: Anthropic is betting on this new product to lead their cloud development strategy.
* **Cloud implications**: Bun's adoption could signal a shift towards more efficient cloud-based AI development.
* **Key features**: Bundles projects into single-file executables, eliminating dependency issues.
* **Development tool ecosystem**: The acquisition highlights the importance of reliable software tools in AI development.

**Context**

Anthropic's ownership of Claude Code indicates their conviction in this new product, and they're seeking to build a cloud hosting platform around Bun. This move may signal a renewed focus on cloud-based AI development.

* **Bun is potentially good**: If it delivers on its promises, it could be a valuable addition to any team.

  - Bun's adoption might affect the way we develop cloud-based AI applications.
