# Congrats to the Winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps! - DEV Community

Here's a concise and informative summary of the text:

**Event Name:** AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps

**Objective:** To celebrate three winners who built stunning apps using APIs (Application Programming Interfaces) from single codebases and showcased their capabilities on multiple platforms.

**Winners:**

1. **Varshith V Hegde**: Developed "Interactive Bento Grid Portfolio", an immersive digital experience that transforms a traditional portfolio into a museum-like setup.
2. **Sujan Koirala**: Created "Bunny Brew Café", a delightful interactive pet experience with animated visuals and AI acceleration.
3. **Sebastien Lachance**: Designed the new "Winamp interface" with AI-powered animation, controls, and visualizers.

**Prizes:** Winners will receive $1,000 USD each, an exclusive DEV Badge, DEV++, and completion badges on their DEV profiles.

**Sponsorship:** Uno Platform collaborated on this challenge, providing flexibility to write code seamlessly across multiple platforms.
