**5 Essential Terminal Commands to Boost Productivity**

*   **1. cd (Plus Tab Completion)**
    The traditional GUI approach involves navigating through file explorers. However, when combined with tab completion, terminal commands become much neater.
    *   When prompted for a directory path, enter `cd` and press Tab to auto-complete the name.
*   **2. mkdir -p**
    This command creates directories and their subfolders at once, making it ideal for project setup.
    *   Use `mkdir` (short for "Make Directory") with the `-p` flag to automatically create subdirectories below the target directory.

    Example: `mkdir -p my-app/{src/components,assets,tests}`

*   **3. cp -r`
    This command provides a safe and efficient way to copy files. It also includes options for calculating file sizes and renaming multiple folders at once.
    *   Use `cp` (short for "Copy") with the `-r` flag to recursively copy directories and all their contents.

As you progress into more complex projects, switching to terminal commands can significantly improve your productivity and efficiency. By mastering these 5 essential commands, you'll be able to simplify your workflow and accelerate tasks without compromising quality.
