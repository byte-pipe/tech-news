The wait is over! Thank you all SO MUCH for your patience, we understand this announcement was significantly delayed.

With that said, we are very excited to announce the winners ofFrontend Challenge: Halloween Edition.

Reviewing submissions for this challenge was an absolute delight filled with spooky creativity and Halloween spirit. We loved seeing both the technical skill and imaginative approaches the community took with both prompts.

We hope you enjoyed working on your submission and are proud of what you accomplished, regardless of whether or not you get the crown.

Read on for the winners!
