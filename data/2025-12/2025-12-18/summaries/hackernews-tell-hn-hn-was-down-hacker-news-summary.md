# Tell HN: HN was down | Hacker News

**Hacker News Down**

HN was down for approximately 16 hours, as reported by various users. The outage occurred when HN experienced a Bad Gateway error on all authenticated requests with a 502 status code.

Although the site's status page suggested otherwise (https://hackernews.onlineornot.com/-), it later turned out to be untruthful and provided outdated information (https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46301823). The last post before the outage was also incorrect, as it claimed "0 minutes ago" when in fact it occurred around 1:32:57 PM GMT.

The reported average time per comment before the outage was approximately 4 seconds. Users have expressed some skepticism about the site's reliability and performance issues, with one user stating, "Dang manages HN in his sleep!"
