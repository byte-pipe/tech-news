# Tesla’s 4680 battery supply chain collapses as partner writes down deal by 99% | Electrek

* **Tesla's 4680 Battery Supply Chain Collapses as Partner Writes Down Deal on 99%**

South Korean battery material supplier L&F Co. has announced that its massive supply deal with Tesla has been slashed by over **99%** to $7,386 from a value of approximately $2.9 billion.

* **The Collapse of the Tesla 4680 Program and Vehicle Sales**

This collapse marks another devastating blow to Elon Musk's ambitious goal of ramping up production of the 4680 battery cell using Tesla's own cells, with the Cybertruck being the only vehicle currently utilizing this technology.

* **Why L&F Was Forced to Write Down the Contract**

The decrease in demand for the high-nickel cathode (a major component used in 4680 cells) may be attributed to Tesla becoming a less reliable customer, ultimately leading to the cancellation of their massive deal with L&F and potentially even winding down production.

* **Industry Insight: What's at Stake for Tesla**

The 4680 cell is widely considered crucial to enabling Tesla's plan to halve battery costs by producing cheaper electric vehicles. With its supply chain disrupted, it remains uncertain whether Tesla will meet its projected targets and maintain market dominance in the low-volume pickup segment.
