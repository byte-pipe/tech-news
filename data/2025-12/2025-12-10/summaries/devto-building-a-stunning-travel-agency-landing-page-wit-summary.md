# Building a Stunning Travel Agency Landing Page with Uno Platform — My WOW Factor Challenge Submission 🤩 - DEV Community

Building Stunning Travel Agency Landing Pages: A Journey from Uno Platform Hot Design Challenge to WOW Factor Submission

After completing a successful project for the Uno Platform Hot Design Challenge on Instagram-style profile pages, I found myself drawn back to the challenge once again. The second prompt, WOW Factor, felt like an invitation I couldn't ignore. This time, I opted to build something bigger and more immersive – a modern, clean travel-agency landing page entirely powered by Uno Platform.

My goal was simple: make the judges say "WOW" at least once. To achieve this, I focused on creating an attention-grabbing home screen with creative visuals, mock data, animations, smooth performance, and a memorable design vision.

**Design Vision**

I set out to create a hero section that felt alive, featuring large images, a layered layout, and a welcoming confetti animation after subscribers signed up. Smooth vertical scrolling was also essential for an immersive experience. Destination cards were designed with mock data, clean cards with rounded corners and subtle shadows, and responsive images.

**Cross-Platform Experience**

I leveraged Uno Platform's grid and stack panel composition to create a smooth, seamless cross-platform experience. Buttons and CTAs were chosen for their visual boldness and responsiveness. Animations included a lightweight confetti effect that added a touch of delight without slowing performance.

## Key Achievements

* Created an attention-grabbing home screen with creative visuals and animations
* Smoothed scrolling throughout the landing page
* Used clean, structured XAML architecture for best results
* Successfully incorporated mock data and visually appealing destination cards

**Why I Chose Uno Platform**

I chose Uno Platform because of its ability to handle cross-platform UI seamlessly. With its robust set of APIs and extensive documentation, creating a high-quality experience across multiple platforms was child's play. My experience with Uno helped me understand the importance of attention-grabbing graphics, animations, and smooth transitions in an immersive design.

Overall, building a travel agency landing page for the WOW Factor prompt from Uno Platform Hot Design Challenge was a rewarding experience that delivered a stunning result. I'm proud to have created something that not only impressed judges but also added value to the user's attention-grabbing experience.
