# Advent of AI 2025 - Day 5: I Built a Touchless Flight Tracker You Control With Hand Gestures - DEV Community

**The Advent of AI Series - Day 5: Building 'The Homecoming Board'**

• **Overview**: The Homecoming Board is a gesture-controlled flight arrival display that utilizes hand gestures, real-time flight data, and audio feedback to navigate for people wearing gloves and mittens.
• **Main Features**:
	+ Two distinct gestures for navigation
	+ Real-time flight data
	+ Audio feedback for successful gesture recognition
• **Key Technology Stack**:
	+ React + TypeScript with SSR (TanStack Start)
	+ MediaPipe for gesture recognition
	+ TheOpenSky Network API for real-time flight data

This is the first time the author has built an app with computer vision, demonstrating its accessibility and effectiveness in leveraging AI and Tensorflow.js.
