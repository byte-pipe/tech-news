# Congrats to the Winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps! - DEV Community

**AI Challenge Winners: Introducing the Pioneers of Cross-Platform Apps**

The annual AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps has concluded with inspiring results, showcasing innovative uses of .NET and Uno Platform to develop stunning cross-platform apps. The community's collective effort has led to three exceptional winners.

### Prompt Winners:

*   **WOW Factor** by `@varshithvhegdebuilt`: An interactive bento grid portfolio that transforms traditional portfolios into immersive digital experiences.
*   **Bunny Brew Café**: A delightful virtual pet experience, created by `@sujankoirala021`, showcasing Hot Design Agent and AI-accelerated innovation.
*   **New Winamp interface**: Developed by `@archiecoderdevreimagined` using 1997 Winamp as a starting point, this application utilizes AI to create an entirely new interface.

### Prizes for the Prompt Winners:

*   Each winner will receive $1,000 USD
*   Exclusive DEV Badge
*   DEV++ Membership

A significant thank you is extended to Uno Platform for their sponsorship and support throughout the challenge.
