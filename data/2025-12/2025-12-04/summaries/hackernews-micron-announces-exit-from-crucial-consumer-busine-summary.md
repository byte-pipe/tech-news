# Micron Announces Exit from Crucial Consumer Business | Micron Technology

**Micron Announces Exit from Crucial Consumer Business**

Micron Technology, a leader in innovative memory and storage solutions, has announced its decision to exit the Crucial consumer business. The company plans to sell Crucial consumer-branded products through key retailers, e-tailers, and distributors worldwide until the end of fiscal Q2 (February 2026). This move will allow Micron to focus on core enterprise and commercial segments.

**Key Points:**

* Micron Technology, Inc. (Nasdaq: MU) decides to exit the Crucial consumer business
* The company will continue to sell Crucial consumer products through the consumer channel until Q2 2026
* Micron will work closely with partners and customers through this transition
* The company will provide continued warranty service and support for Crucial products
