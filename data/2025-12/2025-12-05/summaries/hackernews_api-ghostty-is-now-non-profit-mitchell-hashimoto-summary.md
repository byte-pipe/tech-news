## Announcement: Ghostty Now Non-Profit

* December 3, 2025
* Hack Club sponsors Ghostty as a non-profit organization
* Fiscal sponsorship allows Ghostty to maintain its free and open source model while benefiting from guidance on compliance, donations, accounting, and governance.

## Why Become a Non-Profit?

* **Commitment to Sustainability:** As a non-profit, Ghostty will have the freedom to develop sustainably without relying solely on personal involvement.
* **Legal Protections**: A non-profit structure provides essential legal protections, ensuring financial transparency and safeguarding stakeholders from misuse of funds.
* **Flexibility in Mission Adaptation:** Non-profits can alter their mission with ease, allowing for adaptability to changing circumstances.

## Benefits for Ghostty

* **Enhanced Transparency**: The public benefits from increased information on the project's progress and finances.
* **Community Engagement:** A non-profit organization fosters a more open-door approach, encouraging collaboration and adoption of the community.
* **Expanded Financial Support**: With fiscal sponsorship, Ghostty can now accept tax-deductible donations in the United States, providing a sustainable funding stream.

## Technical Impact

* Nothing changes from a technical standpoint, as our goals remain focused on improving the Ghostty GUI release process.
