# Koralm Railway

The Koralm Railway is a key infrastructure project in Austria, connecting the regions of Styria and Carinthia with improved efficiency and comfort. One of the most important aspects of this project is its impact on reducing travel times by increasing capacity along the route. This can be achieved through modernization and upgrading existing train lines to allow for faster and more comfortable journey options.

## Benefits of the Koralm Railway

* **Faster Journey Times**: The new railway line aims to reduce travel times between Graz and Klagenfurt from three hours to 45 minutes.
* **Greater Ease of Access**
Increased accessibility: The region benefits due to easier access from European neighboring countries, including Hungary and Italy.
Improved road transportation with reduced emissions
