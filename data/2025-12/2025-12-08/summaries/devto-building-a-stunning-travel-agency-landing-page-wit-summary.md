# Building a Stunning Travel Agency Landing Page with Uno Platform — My WOW Factor Challenge Submission 🤩 - DEV Community

**Building a Stunning Travel Agency Landing Page with Uno Platform**

This is an article submission for the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps - WOW Factor.

After completing a recent project on Instagram-style profile pages for the Uno Platform Hot Design Challenge, I realized that I had fallen in love with the journey and experienced something unexpected. Once again, the creative flow, instant feedback, and smooth cross-platform experience drew me back in.

Upon re-examining the second prompt - WOW Factor, I felt like an invitation to continue building something exciting.

This time, I decided to create a modern and clean travel agency landing page for Uno Platform, powered by full-width imagery, elegant typography, destination cards, animations, and even a confetti celebration subscription section. My goal was simple: make the judges "WOW" at least once.

**Why Build a Travel Agency Home Screen**

To answer my goals:

* A visually engaging home screen is perfect.
* I chose a theme that inspires emotions like in travel agencies (coffee shop).
* Creativity, mock data, animations, and visuals all come together to create an immersive experience.
* This style is ideal for grabbing attention across platforms.

**Key Features and Design Vision**

For the landing page, my goals were:

* A welcoming hero section with a lively animation after subscribing.
* Smooth vertical scrolling.
* Destination cards with mock data (clean, rounded corners).
* Subtle animations (small-scale transitions).
* A clean, structured XAML coding style.

I started by using Uno Platform's mock data and visual assets. Then, I composed the layout using Grid & StackPanel layout structures.

**How I Built It**

I incorporated:

* Mock Data & Visual Assets
* Grid & StackPanel layout composition
* Buttons & CTAs (responsive and visually bold)
* Animations (confetti effect)

Unfamiliarity with Uno Platform allowed me to create something new, even as an expert.
