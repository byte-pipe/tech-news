# Advent of AI 2025 - Day 17: Building a Wishlist App with Goose and MCP-UI - DEV Community

Here is a concise yet comprehensive summary of the article:

**Advent of AI Day 17: Wishlist App with Goose and MCP-UI**

This post leverages Goose, an open-source AI agent, to build a Willisisk MCP (Wishlist Mechanical Product Consumer) app. The app allows users to create, view, grant, and delete wishes in a visually appealing interface.

**Key Features:**

*   Users can add wishes with categories and priorities
*   View all wishes in a nicely formatted UI
*   Grant wishes when they come true
*   Delete wishes no longer desired

The implementation stores wishes in memory based on MCP session ID, demonstrating the flexibility of Goose to adapt to users' workflows. The app seamlessly integrates with MCP servers and is available as a desktop app or CLI (Command Line Interface).
