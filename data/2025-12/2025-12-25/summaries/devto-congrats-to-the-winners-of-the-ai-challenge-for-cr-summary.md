# Congrats to the Winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps! - DEV Community

**Congrats to the Winners of the AI Challenge for Cross-Platform Apps! - DEV Community**

The winners of the .NET and Uno Platform AI Challenge for cross-platform apps have been announced. The challenge received many impressive submissions from participants, showcasing their skills in developing applications that run seamlessly across Windows, Linux, macOS, mobile, and web platforms.

**Three Winners**

1. **Varshith Hegde**: Built an interactive Bento Grid Portfolio that transforms the traditional portfolio into an immersive digital experience using .NET and Uno Platform.
2. **Sujan Koirala**: Created Bunny Brew Café, a delightful virtual pet experience with Hot Design Agent and AI-powered tools for development.
3. **Sebastien Lachance did not finish in third place**

The winners will receive the following prizes:

* Prize 1: $1,000 USD; Exclusive DEV Badge; DEV++ Membership
* Prize 2: No prize mentioned (no information on award for second winner)
* Prize 3: Not specified
