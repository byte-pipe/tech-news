We are so thrilled to introduceDEV's Worldwide Show and Tell Challengepresented byMux!

Running throughJanuary 4, this challenge invites you to record a 1-minute pitch video about your project and share it with the community. Consider this our version of "Shark Tank" but without the sharks.

Have you been thinking about a project for months but haven't gotten started? Take this as your signal to start building!

Previous projects welcome too!This is your moment to show off that side project, startup, or previous challenge submission that you worked so hard on but didn't get the recognition for.

Whether it's a weekend hack, a passion project you've been refining or thinking about for months, or something in between, we want to see it!

## Our Prompt 🎥

Show off any side project you're proud of.Record a 1-minute pitch video explaining what your project is, what makes it special, and why you built it. Upload the video to Mux and embed it as part of your submission!

## Judging Criteria

Your pitch will be evaluated using Shark Tank–inspired criteria:

1. Problem & Opportunity- How clearly you explain the problem and why it matters
2. Solution & Technical Approach- How well your app solves the problem and how thoughtful/creative your implementation is
3. Value Proposition & Audience Benefit- Who it's for and what they get out of it
4. Storytelling & Pitch Quality- Clarity, structure, and how engaging your 1-minute pitch is
5. Scalability & "Would You Invest?" Potential- How big this could be and whether it feels investable

## Prizes

Both Overall Prompt Winner and Best Use of Mux Winnerwill each receive:

* $1,500 USD cash prize
* DEV++ Membership
* Exclusive DEV Badge

All Participantswith a valid submission will receive a completion badge on their DEV profile.

## How To Participate

To participate, you'll need to create afree Mux account(no credit card required), upload your video to Mux, and publish a post with your Mux video embedded using the submission template below.

Show and Tell Challenge Submission Template

 

### Project Requirements

* Must be a software side project that you are building/coding or have built/coded
* Should be a web or mobile app
* Must be your own code
* Make testing easy for us!If your app requires logging in, please provide testing credentials in your submission and/or clear instructions on how to best test your application for judges.
* App Store/TestFlight links (optional)
* GitHub Repo (optional)
* Live demo link (optional)

### Pitch Video Requirements

* Must be 1 minute or less
* Should clearly cover:What your app does/solvesWhy you built itWhat makes it unique or specialHow it works
* What your app does/solves
* Why you built it
* What makes it unique or special
* How it works
