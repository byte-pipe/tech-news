# Developers vs AI: Are We Becoming AI Managers Instead of Coders? - DEV Community

**Developers vs AI: Can We Become AI Managers Instead of Coders?**

In this article, developers have shifted from being creators to curators, and now more time is spent on reviewing and decision-making rather than coding. While not a bad thing, this change highlights the importance of staying sharp in various skills needed to stay ahead.

* **The Rise of AI Management**
	+ Developers are no longer solely responsible for code implementation
	+ More focus on architecture, business understanding, debugging, communication, security, and critical thinking
	+ The role of a developer has evolved from creator to manager, requiring better guidance and oversight
* **AI Training vs. Development Skills**
	+ AI assistance is becoming more prominent in development workflows
	+ Developing skills in prompting, logical breakdowns, and clear writing can lead to better results with AI
	+ The danger lies in relying on AI rather than developing one's own reasoning
* **Staying Ahead of the Curve (2025 Focus)**
	+ Develop focus areas for staying productive: architecture & systems thinking, business understanding, debugging intuition, communication & clarity, security, and critical thinking
* **Conclusion**
	Despite the shift from coding to management, developers will still rely on AI assistance
	Emphasizing skills such as architecture, business understanding, debugging, and clear writing is crucial in staying ahead of the curve
