# AI used in warfare needs a strong ethical framework

## Key points
- The editorial argues that artificial intelligence in warfare must be governed by clear rules.
- A strong ethical framework is essential for responsible AI deployment in conflicts.
- The authors introduce the CARE principles to guide AI governance in warfare.

## CARE principles
- **Collective benefit** – AI systems should serve the broader good and reduce harm.
- **Authority to control** – Human oversight and decisive control must remain with accountable entities.
- **Responsibility** – Developers and operators are accountable for the outcomes of AI use.
- **Ethics** – Moral considerations must be integrated into design, deployment, and evaluation.
