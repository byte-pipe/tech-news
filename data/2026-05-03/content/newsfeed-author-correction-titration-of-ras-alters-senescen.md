Correction to:Naturehttps://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-024-07797-zPublished online 7 August 2024

In the originally published version of this article, the t-SNE plot coloured by gene expression, labelled “Cdkn1a” (p21) in Extended Data Fig. 2e, was incorrect and instead showed “Cdkn1b” (p27). This has now been corrected in the HTML and PDF versions of the article to display the appropriateCdkn1adata. This error does not affect the text or conclusions of the study.
