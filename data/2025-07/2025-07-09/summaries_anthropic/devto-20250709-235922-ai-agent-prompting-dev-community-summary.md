1. TripIt (www.tripit.com) - Automatically organizes travel plans into a master itinerary; Freemium model with paid Pro subscription.
2. Google Trips (www.google.com/travel/trips) - Pulls travel information from Gmail and suggests activities/restaurants; Free to use.
3. Inspirock (www.inspirock.com) - Generates personalized day-by-day itineraries based on user preferences; Freemium model.
4. Journy (www.journy.travel) - Provides custom travel planning by connecting users with local experts; Subscription-based pricing.
5. Utrip (www.utrip.com) - AI-powered trip planning with recommendations based on interests; Freemium model.

- The global online travel market is expected to grow from $522 billion in 2019 to $833 billion by 2025, a CAGR of 8.1%.
- 71% of travelers want more personalized trip planning, and 61% are willing to share personal data to get better recommendations.
- The use of AI in the tourism industry is projected to grow at a CAGR of 31.2% from 2020 to 2027.
- Millennials and Gen Z travelers are driving the demand for more personalized, tech-enabled travel experiences.
