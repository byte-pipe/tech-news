Additionally, the author's concern about the reliability and trust in LLM-based solutions indicates that there may be a market for tools that combine the power of LLMs with the transparency and control of code generation. As a solo developer, you could potentially build and market such tools to businesses and individuals in need of reliable automation solutions.

Overall, this article highlights an opportunity for solo developers to build tools and applications that leverage code generation to solve "boring problems" that people and businesses are willing to pay for, while addressing the limitations of current MCP and LLM-based approaches.
