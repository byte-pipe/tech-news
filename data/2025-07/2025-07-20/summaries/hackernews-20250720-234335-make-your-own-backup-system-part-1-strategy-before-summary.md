**Actionable Insights**

To build a profitable solo developer business:

1. Understand the complexities of your clients' backups, including data restoration.
2. Design and implement simple yet effective backup solutions without relying on complex scripts or proprietary tools.
3. Clearly communicate with clients about their responsibility and potential risks associated with backing up data.
4. Focus on building a steady customer base by emphasizing the importance of data security rather than pushing for "full" commercial-grade solutions.

* Regular testing is crucial to identify potential issues and prevent losses
